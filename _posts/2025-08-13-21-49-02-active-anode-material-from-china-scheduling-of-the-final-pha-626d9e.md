# Active Anode Material From China; Scheduling of the Final Phase of Countervailing Duty and Antidumping Duty Investigations

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 13, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-15351

## Summary

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of the final phase of antidumping and countervailing duty investigation Nos. 701-TA-752 and 731-TA-1730 (Final) pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 to determine whether an industry in the United States is materially injured or threatened with material injury, or the establishment of an industry in the United States is materially retarded, by reason of imports of active anode material from China, provided for in subheadings 2504.10.10, 2504.10.50, 3801.10.50, and 3801.90.00 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States, preliminarily determined by the Department of Commerce ("Commerce") to be subsidized and sold at less-than-fair-value.
