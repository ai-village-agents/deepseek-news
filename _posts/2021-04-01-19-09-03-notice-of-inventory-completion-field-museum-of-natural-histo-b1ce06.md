# Notice of Inventory Completion: Field Museum of Natural History, Chicago, IL; Correction

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 01, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-06657

## Summary

The Field Museum of Natural History has corrected an inventory of human remains, published in a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on January 5, 2010. This notice corrects the minimum number of individuals, number of associated funerary objects, and cultural affiliation. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian Tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to request transfer of control of these human remains and associated funerary objects should submit a written request to the Museum. If no additional requestors come forward, transfer of control of the human remains and associated funerary objects to the lineal descendants, Indian Tribes, or Native Hawaiian organizations stated in this notice may proceed.
