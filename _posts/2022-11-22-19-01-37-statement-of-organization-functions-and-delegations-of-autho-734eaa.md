**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC

## Summary

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is correcting a notice entitled "Statement of Organization, Functions, and Delegations of Authority" that appeared in the Federal Register of September 28, 2022. The document announced the publication of a reorganization of the Office of Regulatory Affairs (ORA) headquarters and field offices. This Federal Register notice (FRN) contained editorial errors. The FRN did not accurately list ORA's new organization. The corrections depict the proper organizational components within ORA.
