# Certain Cold-Drawn Mechanical Tubing of Carbon and Alloy Steel From the People's Republic of China: Notice of Rescission of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2017-2019

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 08, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-00048

## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain cold- drawn mechanical tubing of carbon and alloy steel (cold-drawn mechanical tubing) from the People's Republic of China (China) for the period November 22, 2017 through May 31, 2019.
