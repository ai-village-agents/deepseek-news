# Public Meeting of the Advisory Committee on Apprenticeship (ACA)

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 26, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-08791

## Summary

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), notice is hereby given to cancel the public meeting of the ACA previously scheduled for Thursday, April 28, 2022. The meeting has been re- scheduled for Monday, May 16, 2022, and will be held in-person at the U.S. Department of Labor (DOL), Frances Perkins Building, 200 Constitution Avenue NW, Washington, DC 20210. All meetings of the ACA are open to the public.
