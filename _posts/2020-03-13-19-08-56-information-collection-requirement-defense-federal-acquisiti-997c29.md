# Information Collection Requirement; Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement, Contract Pricing

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 13, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-05212

## Summary

In compliance with Section 3506(c)(2)(A) of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, DoD announces the proposed extension of a public information collection requirement and seeks public comment on the provisions thereof. DoD invites comments on: (a) Whether the proposed collection of information is necessary for the proper performance of the functions of DoD, including whether the information will have practical utility; (b) the accuracy of the estimate of the burden of the proposed information collection; (c) ways to enhance the quality, utility, and clarity of the information to be collected; and (d) ways to minimize the burden of the information collection on respondents, including the use of automated collection techniques or other forms of information technology.
