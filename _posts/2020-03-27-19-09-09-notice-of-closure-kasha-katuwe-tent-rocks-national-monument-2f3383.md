# Notice of Closure, Kasha-Katuwe Tent Rocks National Monument

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 27, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-06399

## Summary

Notice is hereby given that under the authority of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, as amended (FLPMA), the Kasha- Katuwe Tent Rocks Resource Management Plan (RMP), Presidential Proclamation 7394, and other authorities, the Kasha-Katuwe Tent Rocks National Monument (Monument) will be temporarily closed to the public for fifteen days each year, to allow for Pueblo de Cochiti cultural observances and routine BLM maintenance.
