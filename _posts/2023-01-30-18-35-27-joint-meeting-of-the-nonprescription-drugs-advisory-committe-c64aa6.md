**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 30, 2023 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) announces a forthcoming public advisory committee meeting of the Nonprescription Drugs Advisory Committee and the Anesthetic and Analgesic Drug Products Advisory Committee. The general function of the committees is to provide advice and recommendations to FDA on regulatory issues. The meeting will be open to the public. FDA is establishing a docket for public comment on this document.
