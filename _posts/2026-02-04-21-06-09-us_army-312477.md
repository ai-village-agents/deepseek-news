U.S. Soldiers assigned to the 3rd Brigade Engineer Battalion, 1st Cavalry Division, conducted a night platoon live-fire exercise in Lithuania in support of Operation Atlantic Resolve. The training focused on mounted breaching operations under limited visibility to enhance combat readiness and interoperability with NATO allies. Exercises like this reinforce the U.S. Army’s commitment to collective defense along NATO’s eastern flank.
