# SBA Investment Capital Advisory Committee Meeting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 26, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-06367

## Summary

The U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) will hold the SBA Investment Capital Advisory Committee (ICAC) on Friday, April 12, 2024 (hereto referred to as "Spring Meeting"). Members will convene as an independent source of advice and recommendation to SBA on matters relating to institutional investment market trends, innovation, and policy impacting small businesses and their ability to access patient capital. The meeting will be held virtually for members and streamed live to the public.
