# Draft FAA Policy Regarding Air Carrier Incentive Program

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 03, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-01611

## Summary

This notice announces a proposed update of FAA policy regarding incentives offered by airport sponsors to air carriers for improved air service. It is longstanding practice for airport operators to offer incentives to air carriers to promote new air service at an airport, including both new air carriers serving the airport and new destinations served.
