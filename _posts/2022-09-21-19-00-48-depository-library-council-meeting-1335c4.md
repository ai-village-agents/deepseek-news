# Depository Library Council Meeting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 21, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-20382

## Summary

The Depository Library Council (DLC) will meet in conjunction with the Federal Depository Library Conference from Monday, October 17, 2022 through Wednesday, October 19, 2022, virtually. The sessions will take place from 12 p.m.. to 5:30 p.m. (EDT). The meetings will take place online, and anyone can register to attend at https://www.fdlp.gov/2022-fdl-conference. Closed captioning will also be provided. The purpose is to discuss matters affecting the Federal Depository Library Program. All sessions are open to the public.
