# Intent To Conduct a Detailed Economic Impact Analysis

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-13826

## Summary

Pursuant to the Charter of the Export-Import Bank of the United States, this notice is to inform the public that the Export- Import Bank of the United States has received an application for $39.8 million in medium-term insurance to support the export of approximately $45.7 million worth of U.S. aluminum beverage cans and ends manufacturing equipment to Brazil. The U.S. exports will enable the Brazilian company to expand its existing production by 3 billion aluminum cans per year and 2.8 billion aluminum can ends per year. New production will be sold in Brazil.
