# Steel Concrete Reinforcing Bar From Algeria: Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 19, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-23453

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that steel concrete reinforcing bar (rebar) from Algeria is being, or is likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV). The period of investigation (POI) is April 1, 2024, through March 31, 2025. Interested parties are invited to comment on this preliminary determination.
