**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC

**Original Published:** January 03, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of an expedited review pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act") to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on hydrofluorocarbon blends from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
