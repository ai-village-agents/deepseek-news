# Notice of Public Briefing of the New York Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 05, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-11826

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the New York Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a briefing via web conference. The purpose of this briefing is to hear final testimony on the New York child welfare system and its impact on Black children and families.
