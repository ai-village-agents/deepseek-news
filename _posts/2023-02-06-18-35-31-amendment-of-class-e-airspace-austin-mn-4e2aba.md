# Amendment of Class E Airspace; Austin, MN

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 06, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-02404

## Summary

This action amends the Class E airspace at Austin, MN. This action is due to an airspace review conducted as part of the decommissioning of the Austin very high frequency (VHF) omnidirectional range (VOR)/distance measuring equipment (DME). The geographic coordinates of the airport are also being updated to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
