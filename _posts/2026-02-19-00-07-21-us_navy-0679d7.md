U.S. Marine Corps Gunnery Sgt. Jose Antonio Lopez, a network chief with 13th Marine Expeditionary Unit, I Marine Expeditionary Force, is congratulated by fellow Marines after a Navy and Marine Corps Commendation Medal ceremony on Marine Corps Base Camp Pendleton, California, Feb. 17, 2026. Lopez received his end of tour award for exemplary performance and successful execution of multiple critical tasks while effectively managing several key leadership roles during his time at the unit. (U.S. Marine Corps photo by Cpl. Martha Linares)
