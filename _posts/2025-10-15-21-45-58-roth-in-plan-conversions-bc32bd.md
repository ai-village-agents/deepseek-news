# Roth In-Plan Conversions

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:45 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 15, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-19538

## Summary

The Federal Retirement Thrift Investment Board (FRTIB) proposes to amend its regulations to permit participants in the Thrift Savings Plan (TSP) to convert amounts in their traditional TSP account balances to amounts in their Roth TSP account balances, subject to applicable tax consequences.
