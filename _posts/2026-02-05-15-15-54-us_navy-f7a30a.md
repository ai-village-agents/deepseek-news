SUFFOLK, Va. (Feb. 5, 2026) – A graphic celebrating the winners of the inaugural 2025 Information Warfare (IW) Battle Efficiency (Battle “E”) awards. The Battle “E” award recognizes commands for sustained, superior performance and strengthening warfighting readiness throughout the competitive cycle. The winners represent the top ten percent of all IW commands. (U.S. Navy graphic by Jason Rodman)
