# Notice of Intended Repatriation: San Diego State University, San Diego, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 20, 2024 00:00 UTC

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), San Diego State University (SDSU) intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
