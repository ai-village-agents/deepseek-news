# Notice of OFAC Sanctions Actions

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 07, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-09648

## Summary

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing the names of one or more persons that have been removed from OFAC's Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Persons List (SDN List). Their property and interests in property are no longer blocked, and U.S. persons are no longer generally prohibited from engaging in transactions with them.
