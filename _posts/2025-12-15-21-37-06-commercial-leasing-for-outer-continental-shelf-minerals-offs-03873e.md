# Commercial Leasing for Outer Continental Shelf Minerals Offshore the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands-Request for Information and Interest; Extension of Comment Period and Technical Correction

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 15, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-22833

## Summary

The Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) announces a 30- day extension of the comment period for the request for information and interest (RFI) for leasing of the Outer Continental Shelf minerals in and around an area offshore the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands (CNMI), referred to as the RFI Area. BOEM also provides a technical correction in the description of the RFI Area.
