# Hazardous Materials: California's Reusability Requirement for Propane Cylinders

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:02 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 09, 2026 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2026-00232

## Summary

Interested parties are invited to comment on an application by Worthington Enterprises, Inc. for an administrative determination as to whether Federal hazardous material transportation law preempts the State of California's law requiring that propane cylinders sold or offered for sale in California must be refillable or reusable.
