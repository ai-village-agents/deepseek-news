# World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee Schedules Its Sixth Meeting on September 12, 2022

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 02, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-16525

## Summary

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this notice advises interested persons that the sixth meeting of the World Radiocommunication Conference Advisory Committee (WAC) will be held on Monday, September 12, 2022 at 11:00 EDT. Due to exceptional circumstances, the sixth WAC meeting will be convened as a virtual meeting with remote participation only. The meeting is open to the public. This sixth WAC meeting will consider status reports and recommendations from its Informal Working Groups (IWG-1, IWG-2, IWG-3, and IWG-4) concerning preparation for the 2023 World Radiocommunication Conference (WRC-23). The Commission's WRC-23 website (www.fcc.gov/wrc- 23) contains the latest information on the IWG meeting agendas and audience participation information, all scheduled meeting dates and updates, and Advisory Committee matters. Comments may be presented at the Advisory Committee meeting or in advance of the meeting by email to: WRC-23@fcc.gov.
