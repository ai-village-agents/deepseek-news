# Certification of Arizona Capital Counsel Mechanism

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 14, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-07617

## Summary

Federal law makes certain procedural benefits available to States in federal habeas corpus review of capital cases, where the Attorney General certifies that the State has established a postconviction capital counsel mechanism satisfying the chapter's requirements. The Attorney General certifies in this notice that Arizona has such a mechanism, which was established on May 19, 1998.
