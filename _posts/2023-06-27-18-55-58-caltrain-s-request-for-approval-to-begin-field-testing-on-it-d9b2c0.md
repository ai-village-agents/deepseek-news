# Caltrain's Request for Approval To Begin Field Testing on Its Positive Train Control Network

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 27, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-13644

## Summary

This document provides the public with notice that, on June 14, 2023, Caltrain (PCMZ) submitted a request to field test its Stadler KISS Electric Multiple Units (EMUs) that have been equipped with PCMZ's Interoperable Electronic Train Management System (I-ETMS) technology. FRA is publishing this notice and inviting public comment on PCMZ's request to test its positive train control (PTC) system.
