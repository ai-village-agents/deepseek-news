# Acid Rain Program: Excess Emissions Penalty Inflation Adjustments

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 20, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-20476

## Summary

The Acid Rain Program requires sources that do not meet their annual Acid Rain emissions limitations for sulfur dioxide (SO<INF>2</INF>) or nitrogen oxides (NO<INF>X</INF>) to pay inflation- adjusted excess emissions penalties. This document provides notice of the annual adjustment factors used to calculate excess emissions penalties for compliance years 2025 and 2026.
