U.S. Army Soldiers assigned to 512th Field Hospital, 30th Medical Brigade, 21st Theater Sustainment Command treat a simulated casualty during a field training exercise on Rhine Ordnance Barracks, Kaiserslautern, Germany, Feb. 4, 2026. This training ensures the casualty is being safely lifted and transported over to where help can be found. (U.S. Army photo by Pvt. Robert Palmer)
