# Notice of Public Meetings of the Pennsylvania Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 03, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-21879

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Pennsylvania Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a business meeting on Wednesday October 11, 2023 from 12 p.m.-1 p.m. eastern time. The purpose of the meeting is for the Committee to select the next topic of study.
