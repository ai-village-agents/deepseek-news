The U.S. Navy recently declared initial operational capability for the Small Diameter Bomb II on the F/A-18E/F Super Hornet, marking a significant milestone for the fleet's precision-strike technology. Also known as StormBreaker, SDB II is a network-enabled weapon that allows aircrews to successfully engage moving and stationary targets in all weather conditions.
