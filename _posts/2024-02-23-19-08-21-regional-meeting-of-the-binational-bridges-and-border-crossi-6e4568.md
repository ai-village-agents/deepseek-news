# Regional Meeting of the Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group in Mexico City, Mexico

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 23, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-03639

## Summary

Delegates from the United States and Mexican governments, the states of New Mexico and Texas, and the Mexican states of Chihuahua, Coahuila, Nuevo Laredo, and Tamaulipas will participate in a regional meeting of the U.S.-Mexico Binational Bridges and Border Crossings Group. The purpose of this meeting is to discuss operational matters involving existing and proposed international bridges and border crossings and their related infrastructure and to exchange technical information as well as views on policy. This meeting will include a public session. This session will allow proponents of proposed bridges and border crossings and related projects to make presentations to the delegations and members of the public.
