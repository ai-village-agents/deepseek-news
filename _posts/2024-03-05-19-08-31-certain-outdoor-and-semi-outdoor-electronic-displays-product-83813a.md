# Certain Outdoor and Semi-Outdoor Electronic Displays, Products Containing Same, and Components Thereof; Notice of a Commission Determination Not To Review a Final Initial Determination Finding No Violation of Section 337; Termination of the Investigation

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 05, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-04567

## Summary

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission has determined not to review the presiding administrative law judge's ("ALJ") final initial determination ("FID") finding no violation of section 337 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended. The investigation is terminated with a finding of no violation.
