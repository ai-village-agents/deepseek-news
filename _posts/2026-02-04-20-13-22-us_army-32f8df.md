U.S. Army Soldiers assigned to 512th Field Hospital, 30th Medical Brigade, 21st Theater Sustainment Command train to draw blood from a simulated casualty on Rhine Ordnance Barracks, Kaiserslautern, Germany, Feb. 4, 2026. The training was conducted in a field environment to ensure all personnel are qualified to safely perform blood transfusions. (U.S. Army photo by Pvt. Robert Palmer)
