# Winnipeg, MB, Canada Hearing International Red River Board Report on Nutrient Targets

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 12, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-02835

## Summary

The International Joint Commission is having a meeting and request public comment on recommendations by the International Red River Board (IRRB) on proposed nutrient concentration objectives and nutrient load targets for the Red River at the boundary between the United States and Canada. The IRRB's full report can be found on the IJC website at the following link: www.ijc.org/what/engagement/public- comment-IIRB-nutrient-2020.
