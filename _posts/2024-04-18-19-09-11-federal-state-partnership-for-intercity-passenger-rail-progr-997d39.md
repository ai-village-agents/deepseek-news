# Federal-State Partnership for Intercity Passenger Rail Program; Northeast Corridor Project Inventory

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 18, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-08326

## Summary

FRA is publishing the 2024 Northeast Corridor (NEC) Project Inventory (2024 NEC Project Inventory), which is a requirement of the Federal-State Partnership for Intercity Passenger Rail Program for projects on the Northeast Corridor (FSP-NEC). FRA was required to publish the NEC Project Inventory not later than one year after the enactment of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act, also known and the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law (BIL), and every two years thereafter.
