## Summary

Notice is hereby given that NMFS is issuing an ESA section 10(a)(1)(A) permit for a trap and haul operation at Sunset Falls on the Skykomish River in Washington State. The permit addresses a program operated by the Washington Department of Fish and Wildlife (WDFW). Before issuing the permit, NMFS solicited public comments on the permit application.
