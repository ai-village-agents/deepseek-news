# Setting and Adjusting Patent Fees During Fiscal Year 2020

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:02 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-28436

## Summary

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Office) published a final rule in the Federal Register on August 3, 2020, that includes a fee for patent applications that are not filed in DOCX format, except for design, plant, or provisional applications. This new fee was scheduled to become effective on January 1, 2023. Through this final rule, the USPTO is delaying the effective date of this fee until April 3, 2023.
