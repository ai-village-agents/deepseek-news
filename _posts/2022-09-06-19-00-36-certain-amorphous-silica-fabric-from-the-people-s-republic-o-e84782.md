# Certain Amorphous Silica Fabric From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Affirmative Determinations of Circumvention

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 06, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-19124

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that amorphous silica fabric with 70-90 percent silica content (70-90 percent ASF) from the People's Republic of China (China) is circumventing the antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) orders on certain amorphous silica fabric (ASF) from China.
