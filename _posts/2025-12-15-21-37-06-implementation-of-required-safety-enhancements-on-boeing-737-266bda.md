# Implementation of Required Safety Enhancements on Boeing 737 MAX Airplanes

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 15, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-22787

## Summary

Section 501 of the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2023 restricts the issuance of airworthiness certificates for, and the operation of, Boeing 737 MAX aircraft unless certain safety enhancements are incorporated. This Notice announces the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA)'s plan for implementing and addressing these requirements.
