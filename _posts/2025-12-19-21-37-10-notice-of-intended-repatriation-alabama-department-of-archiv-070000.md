# Notice of Intended Repatriation: Alabama Department of Archives and History, Montgomery, AL

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 19, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-23345

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Alabama Department of Archives and History intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
