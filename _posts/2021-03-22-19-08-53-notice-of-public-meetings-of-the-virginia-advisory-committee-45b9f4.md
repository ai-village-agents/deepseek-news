# Notice of Public Meetings of the Virginia Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 22, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-05841

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Virginia Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Committee) will hold a meeting via web conference on Friday, April 2, 2021 at 12:30 p.m. Eastern Time. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss panelist nominations for the Committee's forthcoming study on policing accountability in the state.
