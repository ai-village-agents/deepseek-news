# Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 19, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-10729

## Summary

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is modifying the system of records entitled, "National Prosthetic Patient Database (NPPD)-VA" (33VA113) as set forth in the Federal Register. This system is used to furnish administrative and clinical statistical procurement and prescription information, including total cost and summary of activity, including equipment usage, data to VA and other health care providers, both Federal and non-Federal, to aid in furthering the improvement of health care, research, and education.
