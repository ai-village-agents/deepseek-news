# South Atlantic Fishery Management Council; Public Hearings

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 16, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-08047

## Summary

The South Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold public hearings via webinar pertaining to Regulatory Amendment 34 to the Snapper Grouper Fishery Management Plan for the South Atlantic Region. The amendment would designate artificial reefs in the federal waters off the coasts of North Carolina and South Carolina as Special Management Zones (SMZs) and implement fishing gear restrictions within the SMZs.
