# Draft 2023 Marine Mammal Stock Assessment Reports

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:53 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 29, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-01653

## Summary

NMFS reviewed the Alaska, Atlantic, and Pacific regional marine mammal stock assessment reports (SAR) in accordance with the Marine Mammal Protection Act (MMPA). SARs for marine mammals in the Alaska, Atlantic, and Pacific regions were revised according to new information. NMFS solicits public comments on the draft 2023 SARs. NMFS is also requesting new information for strategic stocks that were not updated in 2023.
