# Information Collection: Submission for the Office of Management and Budget Review

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 20, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-05904

## Summary

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) invites public comment on our intention to request the OMB's approval for renewal of an existing information collection in order for the United States to fulfill its responsibilities as a participant in the U.S./International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) Safeguards Agreement. The information collection is entitled, "Design Information Questionnaire--IAEA N-71 and Associated Forms N-72, N-73, N-74, N-75, N-76, N-77, N-91, N-92, N- 93, N-94."
