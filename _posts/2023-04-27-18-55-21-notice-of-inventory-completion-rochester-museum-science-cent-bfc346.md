# Notice of Inventory Completion: Rochester Museum & Science Center, Rochester, NY

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 27, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-08895

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Rochester Museum & Science Center (RMSC) has completed an inventory of human remains and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The human remains were removed from an unknown location in New York.
