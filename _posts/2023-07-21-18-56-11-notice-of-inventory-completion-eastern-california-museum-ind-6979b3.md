# Notice of Inventory Completion: Eastern California Museum, Independence, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 21, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-15517

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Eastern California Museum (ECM) has completed an inventory of human remains and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and any Indian Tribe. The human remains were removed from Inyo County, CA.
