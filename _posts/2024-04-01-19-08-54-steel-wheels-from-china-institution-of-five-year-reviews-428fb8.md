# Steel Wheels From China; Institution of Five-Year Reviews

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 01, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-06740

## Summary

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act"), as amended, to determine whether revocation of the countervailing and antidumping duty orders on imports of steel wheels from China would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. Pursuant to the Act, interested parties are requested to respond to this notice by submitting the information specified below to the Commission.
