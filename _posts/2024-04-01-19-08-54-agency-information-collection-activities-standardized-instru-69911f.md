# Agency Information Collection Activities; Standardized Instructions and Format To Be Used for Interim and Final Progress Reporting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 01, 2024 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Appraisal Subcommittee (ASC) is issuing this Notice of Request (Notice) for public comment on the standardized Appraisal Subcommittee Progress Report (ASC-PR) format to be used for both interim and final progress reporting for all ASC grants and submission to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) of proposed collection of information. In conjunction with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the ASC has submitted to the OMB a request for review and approval of information collection listed below. The purpose of this Notice is to allow an additional 30 days for public comment from all interested individuals and organizations.
