# Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 16, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-07872

## Summary

The Pacific Fishery Management Council (Pacific Council) will convene three Stock Assessment Review (STAR) Panels this year to review new stock assessments for Dover sole, spiny dogfish, lingcod, and vermilion and sunset rockfishes. These STAR Panel meetings are open to the public.
