# Notice of Public Meetings of the Montana Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 17, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-23110

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Montana Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a public business meeting every second Thursday of the month (January 2026 through March 2026) via Zoom at 3:00 p.m. MT. The purpose of these meetings is to discuss the Committee's project, Civil Rights Violations Resulting from Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Policies at Montana Public Universities.
