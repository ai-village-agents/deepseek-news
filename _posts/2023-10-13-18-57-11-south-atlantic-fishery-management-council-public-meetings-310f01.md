# South Atlantic Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC

**Original Published:** October 13, 2023 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The South Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold a series of scoping meetings via webinar pertaining to Framework Amendment 13 to the Fishery Management Plan for Coastal Migratory Pelagic Resources in the Gulf of Mexico and Atlantic Region. The amendment addresses Atlantic migratory group Spanish mackerel catch levels.
