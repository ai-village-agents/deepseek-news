# Notice of Inventory Completion: Milwaukee Public Museum, Milwaukee, WI

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 06, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-04663

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Milwaukee Public Museum has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The human remains and associated funerary objects were removed from Marinette County, WI.
