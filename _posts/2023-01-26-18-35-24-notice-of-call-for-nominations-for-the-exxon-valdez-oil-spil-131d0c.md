# Notice of Call for Nominations for the Exxon Valdez Oil Spill Public Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 26, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-01510

## Summary

The Exxon Valdez Oil Spill Trustee Council (Trustee Council) is soliciting nominations for the Public Advisory Committee (Committee). This Committee advises the Trustee Council on decisions related to the planning, evaluation, funds allocation, and conduct of injury assessment and restoration activities related to the T/V Exxon Valdez oil spill of March 1989.
