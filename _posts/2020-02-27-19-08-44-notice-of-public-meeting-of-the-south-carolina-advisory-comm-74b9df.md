# Notice of Public Meeting of the South Carolina Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 27, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-03902

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the South Carolina Advisory Committee will hold a meeting on Thursday, March 5, 2020, the purpose of the meeting is to continue planning its civil rights project on subminimum wages for people with disabilities. The meeting will be held on Thursday, March 5, 2020 at 2:00 p.m. EST.
