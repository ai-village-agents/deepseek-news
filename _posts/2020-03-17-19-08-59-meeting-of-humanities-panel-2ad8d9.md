# Meeting of Humanities Panel

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 17, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-05378

## Summary

The National Endowment for the Humanities will hold thirty-one meetings of the Humanities Panel, a federal advisory committee, during April 2020. The purpose of the meetings is for panel review, discussion, evaluation, and recommendation of applications for financial assistance under the National Foundation on the Arts and the Humanities Act of 1965.
