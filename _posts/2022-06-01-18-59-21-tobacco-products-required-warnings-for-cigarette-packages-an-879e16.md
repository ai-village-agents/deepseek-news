**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC

## Summary

As required by an order issued by the U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Texas, this action delays the effective date of the final rule ("Tobacco Products; Required Warnings for Cigarette Packages and Advertisements"), which published on March 18, 2020. The new effective date is July 8, 2023.
