U.S. Army Brig. Gen. Leland D. Blanchard II, the commanding general (interim) of the District of Columbia National Guard, left, and U.S. Army Brig. Gen. Carrie L. Perez, the director of Army Personnel Management at the National Guard Bureau, greet each other during the initial presentation of Disasterville in support of the D.C. Safe and Beautiful mission in Washington, Feb. 19, 2026. Disasterville is a mixed-reality training experience that immerses users in full-scale, mission-oriented disaster scenarios, including wildfires, floods and earthquakes.
