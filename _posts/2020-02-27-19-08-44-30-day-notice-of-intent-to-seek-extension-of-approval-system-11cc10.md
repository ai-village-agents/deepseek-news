# 30-Day Notice of Intent To Seek Extension of Approval: System Diagram Maps

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 27, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-03990

## Summary

As required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), the Surface Transportation Board (STB or Board) gives notice of its intent to seek approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for an extension of the collection of system diagram maps, described below. The Board previously published a notice about this collection in the Federal Register on December 12, 2019 (84 FR 67,989). That notice allowed for a 60-day public review and comment period. No comments were received.
