**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:36 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register

**Document Number:** 2023-06685

## Summary

NMFS is prohibiting directed fishing for pollock in the West Yakutat District of the Gulf of Alaska (GOA). This action is necessary to prevent exceeding the 2023 total allowable catch of pollock in the West Yakutat District of the GOA.
