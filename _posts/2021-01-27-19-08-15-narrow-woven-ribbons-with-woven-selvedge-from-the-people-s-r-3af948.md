# Narrow Woven Ribbons With Woven Selvedge From the People's Republic of China: Preliminary Results of Countervailing Duty Administrative Review; 2018

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 27, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-01786

## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that countervailable subsidies have been provided to producers and exporters of narrow woven ribbons with woven selvedge (ribbons) from the People's Republic of China (China). The period of review (POR) is January 1, 2018 through December 31, 2018. Interested parties are invited to comment on these preliminary results.
