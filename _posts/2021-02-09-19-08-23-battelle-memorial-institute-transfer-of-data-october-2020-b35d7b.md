# Battelle Memorial Institute; Transfer of Data (October 2020)

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 09, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-02576

## Summary

This notice announces that pesticide related information submitted to EPA's Office of Pesticide Programs (OPP) pursuant to the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA) and the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA), including information that may have been claimed as Confidential Business Information (CBI) by the submitter, will be transferred to Battelle Memorial Institute in accordance with the CBI regulations. Battelle Memorial Institute has been awarded a contract to perform work for OPP and access to this information will enable Battelle Memorial Institute to fulfill the obligations of the contract.
