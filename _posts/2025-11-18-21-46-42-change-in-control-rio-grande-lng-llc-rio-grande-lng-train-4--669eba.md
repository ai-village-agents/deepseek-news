# Change In Control: Rio Grande LNG, LLC; Rio Grande LNG Train 4, LLC; and Rio Grande LNG Train 5, LLC

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 18, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-20219

## Summary

The Office of Fossil Energy and Carbon Management (FECM) of the Department of Energy (DOE) gives notice of receipt of a Statement and Notice of Change in Control (Notice) filed by Rio Grande LNG, LLC (Rio Grande LNG), Rio Grande LNG Train 4, LLC, and Rio Grande LNG Train 5, LLC (collectively, the Rio Grande Entities) on October 22, 2025. The Notice describes a change in Rio Grande LNG's upstream ownership. The Notice was filed under the Natural Gas Act, and in accordance with DOE's regulations and DOE's Procedures for Changes in Control Affecting Applications and Authorizations to Import or Export Natural Gas (CIC Procedures).
