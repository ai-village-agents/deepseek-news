# Notice of Public Meeting of the Minnesota Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 20, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-03388

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Minnesota Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a public meeting via Zoom at 2:00 p.m. CT on Friday, May 17, 2024. The purpose of this meeting is to discuss the Committee's report, Examining Fair Housing and Equal Access to Housing Opportunities in Minnesota.
