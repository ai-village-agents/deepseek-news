On Feb. 25, 1863, Sgt. Milton W. Cline arrived at the Army of the Potomac’s headquarters for detached duty with the new Bureau of Miliary Information.  As chief of scouts for the BMI, he would prove particularly proficient at intermingling with Confederate forces to gather information.
