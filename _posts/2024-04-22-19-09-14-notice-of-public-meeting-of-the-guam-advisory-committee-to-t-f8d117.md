# Notice of Public Meeting of the Guam Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 22, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-08556

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Guam Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a virtual business meeting via Zoom at 9:00 a.m. ChST on Tuesday, April 30, 2024 (7:00 p.m. ET on Monday, April 29, 2024). The purpose of this meeting is to discuss the Committee's project, Overrepresentation of FAS Members in the Criminal Justice System on Guam.
