# Notice of Public Meeting of the Connecticut Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 14, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-03112

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Connecticut Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will convene a business meeting on Thursday, March 16, 2023, 12:00 p.m. Eastern Time. The purpose of the meeting is to continue to review, edit, and vote on a draft report on the civil rights implications of algorithms.
