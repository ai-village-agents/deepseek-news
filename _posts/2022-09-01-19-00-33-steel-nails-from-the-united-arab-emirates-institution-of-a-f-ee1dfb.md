# Steel Nails From the United Arab Emirates; Institution of a Five-Year Review

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 01, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-18909

## Summary

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted a review pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act"), as amended, to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on steel nails from the United Arab Emirates would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. Pursuant to the Act, interested parties are requested to respond to this notice by submitting the information specified below to the Commission.
