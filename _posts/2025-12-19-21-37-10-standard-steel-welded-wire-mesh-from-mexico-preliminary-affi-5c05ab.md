# Standard Steel Welded Wire Mesh From Mexico: Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Circumvention of the Antidumping Duty and Countervailing Duty Orders; Correction

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 19, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-23455

## Summary

On September 16, 2025, Commerce published in the Federal Register the preliminary determination of the circumvention inquiry of the antidumping and countervailing duty (AD/CVD) orders on standard steel welded wire mesh (wire mesh) from Mexico. This notice corrects the inadvertent omission of a word in Appendix II. This notice also corrects language in the certification agreement to properly reflect the name of the exporting party.
