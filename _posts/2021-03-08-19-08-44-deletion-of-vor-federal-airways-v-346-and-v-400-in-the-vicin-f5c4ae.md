# Deletion of VOR Federal Airways V-346 and V-400 in the Vicinity of Beauce, Canada.

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 08, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-04155

## Summary

This action removes VHF Omni-directional Range Federal airway V-346 and V-400, in the vicinity of Beauce, Canada. This action is necessary due to Canada's civil air navigation services provider, NAV Canada, planned decommissioning of the Beauce, Canada, VHF Omni-directional Range (VOR)/Distance Measuring Equipment (DME) navigation aid, which provides navigation guidance for segments of the routes. NAV Canada has deleted the portions of both airways that resided in their airspace.
