# Notice of Opportunity for Public Comment on a Proposed Change of Airport Property Land Use From Aeronautical to Non-Aeronautical Use at Shawnee Regional Airport, Shawnee, OK

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 13, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07902

## Summary

The FAA is considering a request from the City of Shawnee, Oklahoma to change approximately 72 acres, located on the west side of the airport bordered by North Leo Street and West Independence Avenue, from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the conversion of the airport property.
