The 1463rd Transportation Company of the Michigan National Guard was officially welcomed home during a Yellow Ribbon reintegration event held this weekend in Lansing, Michigan. The event, which follows a 14-month activation in support of U.S. Northern Command (USNORTHCOM), brings soldiers and their families together to connect with vital resources for a successful transition back to civilian life.
