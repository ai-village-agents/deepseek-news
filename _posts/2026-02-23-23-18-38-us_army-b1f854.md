The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers is launching a nationwide initiative to deliver projects with greater efficiency. This 
new approach will accelerate project delivery through smarter contracting, higher design standards, and a reformed 
partnership pipeline. The plan restores discipline by focusing resources on the highest-priority projects that serve the 
national interest, ensuring the critical infrastructure America needs is delivered faster
