# Sugar From Mexico: Amendment to the Agreement Suspending the Antidumping Duty Investigation

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 22, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-00970

## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) and a representative of the signatory sugar producers/exporters accounting for substantially all imports of sugar from Mexico have signed an amendment to the Agreement Suspending the Antidumping Duty Investigation on Sugar from Mexico (AD Agreement). The amendment to the AD Agreement modifies the definitions for sugar from Mexico, revises the reference prices for the applicable sugar from Mexico, and provides for enhanced monitoring and enforcement mechanisms.
