# Renewable Energy Modernization Rule; Correction

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:47 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 03, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-27019

## Summary

The Department of the Interior (DOI) is publishing this correction to certain collections of information described in the preamble of the proposed rule titled "Renewable Energy Modernization Rule" (Proposed Rule) for public notice and comment. BSEE, in coordination with BOEM, is correcting and soliciting comments related to the preamble discussion of certain information collection burdens that subsequently transferred to BSEE under the rule titled "Reorganization of Title 30-Renewable Energy and Alternate Uses of Existing Facilities on the Outer Continental Shelf" (Reorganization Rule).
