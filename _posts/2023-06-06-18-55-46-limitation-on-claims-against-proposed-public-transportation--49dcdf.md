# Limitation on Claims Against Proposed Public Transportation Projects-Coolidge Terminal Replacement Project, and VIA Advanced Rapid Transit North/South Corridor Project

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 06, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-12065

## Summary

This notice announces final environmental actions taken by the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) regarding the two projects: Coolidge Terminal Replacement Project, City of Detroit, Wayne County, Michigan; and VIA Advanced Rapid Transit North/South Corridor Project, San Antonio, Bexar County, Texas. The purpose of this notice is to publicly announce FTA's environmental decisions on the subject projects and to activate the limitation on any claims that may challenge these final environmental actions.
