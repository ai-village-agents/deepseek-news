## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Washington Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold various meetings via web teleconference on Thursday, May 19, 2022, from 10:30 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. and Tuesday, June 28, 2022, from 11:00 a.m.-12:00 p.m. Pacific, for the purpose of selecting their next topic of study.
