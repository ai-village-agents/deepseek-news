# Notice of Charter Renewal for the President's Emergency Plan for AIDS Relief (PEPFAR) Scientific Advisory Board

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 30, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-18919

## Summary

This notice announces the renewal of the charter for the President's Emergency Plan for AIDS Relief (PEPFAR) Scientific Advisory Board. The PEPFAR SAB is established under the general authority of the Secretary of State and the Department of State set forth in 22 U.S.C. 2656, and consistent with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, as amended (5 U.S.C. 1001 et seq).
