# Notice of Inventory Completion Amendment: Trinity University, San Antonio, TX; University of Texas at Austin, Texas Archeological Research Laboratory, Austin, TX; and University of Texas at Austin, Vertebrate Paleontology Laboratory, Austin, TX

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 18, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-20111

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), Trinity University (Trinity), University of Texas at Austin, Texas Archeological Research Laboratory (TARL), and and University of Texas at Austin, Vertebrate Paleontology Laboratory (VPL) have amended a notice of inventory completion published in the Federal Register on August 5, 2025. This notice amends the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations with cultural affiliation.
