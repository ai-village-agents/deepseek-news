**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC

## Summary

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the U.S. Department of State announces that the PEPFAR Scientific Advisory Board (SAB) will hold a virtual meeting of the full board. The meeting is open to the public and a public comment session will be held during the meeting. Pre-registration is required for providing public comment.
