# Benefits Payable in Terminated Single-Employer Plans; Interest Assumptions for Paying Benefits

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 14, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-02887

## Summary

This final rule amends the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation's regulation on Benefits Payable in Terminated Single- Employer Plans to prescribe certain interest assumptions under the regulation for plans with valuation dates in March 2020. These interest assumptions are used for paying certain benefits under terminating single-employer plans covered by the pension insurance system administered by PBGC.
