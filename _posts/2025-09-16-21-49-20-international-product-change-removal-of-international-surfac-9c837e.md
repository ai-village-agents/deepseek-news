# International Product Change-Removal of International Surface Air Lift

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 16, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-17792

## Summary

The Postal Service gives notice of the filing of a request with the Postal Regulatory Commission to remove International Surface Air Lift (ISAL) and two types of negotiated service agreement products that include ISAL from the Competitive Product List and to make accompanying classification changes in the Mail Classification Schedule.
