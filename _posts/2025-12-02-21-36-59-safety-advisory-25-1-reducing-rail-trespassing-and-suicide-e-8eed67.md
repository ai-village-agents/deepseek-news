## Summary

The Federal Transit Administration (FTA) is issuing Safety Advisory 25-1 to recommend State Safety Oversight Agencies (SSOAs) direct Rail Transit Agencies (RTAs) within their jurisdiction to use their Safety Management System (SMS) to mitigate risk related to rail trespassing and suicide events.
