**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC

## Summary

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is reopening the comment period for the Information Collection Request (ICR) number 2170.09, for the proposed Air Emissions Reporting Requirements (AERR), published in the Federal Register on August 9, 2023. The EPA is reopening the comment period on this ICR to October 18, 2023.
