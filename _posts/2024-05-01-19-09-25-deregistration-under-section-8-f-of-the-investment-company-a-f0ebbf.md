## Summary

Applicant seeks an order declaring that it has ceased to be an investment company. The applicant has transferred its assets to Amplify ETF Trust, and on January 29, 2024, made a final distribution to its shareholders based on net asset value. Expenses of $9,282,629.85 incurred in connection with the reorganization were paid by the applicant's investment adviser and the acquiring fund's investment adviser.
