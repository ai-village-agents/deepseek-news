# Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: California State University, Sacramento, Sacramento, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 23, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-23283

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the California State University, Sacramento intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of objects of cultural patrimony and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The cultural items were removed from Placer and Yuba Counties, CA.
