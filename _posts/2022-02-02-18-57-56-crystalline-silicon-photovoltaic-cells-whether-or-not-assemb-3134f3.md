# Crystalline Silicon Photovoltaic Cells, Whether or Not Assembled Into Modules, From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review and Final Determination of No Shipments; 2018-2019; Correction

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 02, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-02106

## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) published a notice in the Federal Register of October 25, 2021, in which Commerce announced the final results of the 2018-2019 administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on certain crystalline silicon photovoltaic cells, whether or not assembled into modules, from the People's Republic of China (China). This notice inadvertently omitted Wuxi Suntech Power Co., Ltd/Luoyang Suntech Power Co., Ltd. from the list of companies with no shipments of subject merchandise to the United States during the period of review (POR).
