# NASA Federal Advisory Committees; Charter Renewal

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 05, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-22221

## Summary

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), and after consultation with the Committee Management Secretariat, General Services Administration, the NASA Administrator has determined that renewal of the charter of NASA Advisory Council is in the public interest in connection with the performance of duties imposed on NASA by law. The renewed charter is for a two-year period ending September 30, 2025.
