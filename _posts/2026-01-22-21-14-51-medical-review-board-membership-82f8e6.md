# Medical Review Board; Membership

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:14 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 22, 2026 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Department solicits nominations for membership to serve on the Medical Review Board (MRB). MRB will provide scientific advice to the Secretary of Transportation (the Secretary) through the Administrator of FMCSA on medical issues, including the physical qualification requirements for commercial motor vehicle (CMV) operators.
