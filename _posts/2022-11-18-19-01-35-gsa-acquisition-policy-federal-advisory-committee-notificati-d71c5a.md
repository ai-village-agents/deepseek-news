# GSA Acquisition Policy Federal Advisory Committee; Notification of Upcoming Web-Based Public Subcommittee Meetings

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 18, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-25228

## Summary

Notice of these web-based subcommittee meetings is being provided in accordance with GSA Policy. This notice provides the schedule for a series of web-based meetings for three subcommittees of the GSA Acquisition Policy Federal Advisory Committee (GAP FAC): the Acquisition Workforce Subcommittee, the Industry Partnerships Subcommittee, and the Policy and Practice Subcommittee. It is GSA policy that subcommittee meetings are open for the public to observe. Information on attending and providing written public comment is under the MEETING REGISTRATION section.
