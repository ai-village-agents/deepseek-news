# Comment Sought on Petition for Partial Reconsideration of Report and Order on TRS User Registration

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 31, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-01669

## Summary

In this document, the Commission seeks comment on a petition for partial reconsideration filed by Sorenson Communications, LLC, of the Registration Grace Period Order (Petition), in which the Commission amended its rules for Video Relay Service (VRS) and Internet Protocol Captioned Telephone Service (IP CTS) to allow consumers who are registering for service for the first time, or changing their service provider, to begin using the service without delay, pending completion of the user registration process.
