# Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: Gilcrease Museum, Tulsa, OK

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 04, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-09474

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Gilcrease Museum intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects, sacred objects, and objects of cultural patrimony and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The cultural items were removed from unknown locations.
