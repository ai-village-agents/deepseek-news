# Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: The University of California, Berkeley, Berkeley, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 08, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07603

## Summary

The University of California, Berkeley, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, has determined that the cultural items listed in this notice meet the definition of sacred objects. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian Tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to claim these cultural items should submit a written request to the University of California, Berkeley. If no additional claimants come forward, transfer of control of the cultural items to the lineal descendants, Indian Tribes, or Native Hawaiian organizations stated in this notice may proceed.
