U.S. Army Soldiers assigned to the 1021st Engineer Vertical Construction Company Louisiana National Guard, emplace obstacles as a part of the Opposing Force during Combined Resolve 26-05 at Joint Multinational Readiness Center's Hohenfels Training Area, Germany, Feb. 21, 2026.
