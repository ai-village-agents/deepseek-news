# Notice of Public Meetings of the Colorado Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 24, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-03874

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Colorado Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will convene briefings and business meetings on Wednesday, March 15, 2023 and Wednesday, April 19, from 3-4 p.m. Mountain Time. The purpose of the meetings is to hear from speakers and continue to plan for additional briefings on public school attendance zones.
