**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC

## Summary

This action amends the Class E airspace at Oakwood, TX. The FAA is finalizing this action due to an airspace review conducted as part of the decommissioning of the Leona very high frequency (VHF) omnidirectional range (VOR) as part of the VOR Minimal Operational Network (MON) Program.
