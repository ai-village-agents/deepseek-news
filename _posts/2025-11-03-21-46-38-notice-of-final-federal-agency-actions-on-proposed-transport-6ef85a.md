# Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Transportation Project in California

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 03, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-19745

## Summary

The FHWA, on behalf of the California Department of Transportation (Caltrans), is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans that are final agency actions. These actions relate to the proposed Bay Lights 360 Project. The project will replace the existing LED fixtures and add light fixtures to the inward-facing side of the suspension cables, creating a 360-degree viewing experience. The actions relate to a proposed highway project, Bay Lights 360, on Interstate 80 in San Francisco County, State of California. Those actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
