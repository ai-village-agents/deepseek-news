# Federal Civil Monetary Penalties Inflation Adjustment for 2021

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC

**Original Published:** March 15, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-05312

## Summary

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) has adopted a final rule making inflation adjustments to civil monetary penalties within its jurisdiction. This final rule represents the annual 2021 inflation adjustments of monetary penalties. These adjustments are required by the Federal Civil Penalties Inflation Adjustment Act Improvements Act of 2015.
