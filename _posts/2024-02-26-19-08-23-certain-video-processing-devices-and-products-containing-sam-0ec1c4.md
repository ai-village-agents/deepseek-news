## Summary

Notice is hereby given that on February 5, 2024, the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ") issued the Final Initial Determination on Violation of Section 337 in this investigation. On February 20, 2024, the ALJ issued the Recommended Determination on Remedy and Bond ("RD").
