# Amendment of Class D and Class E4 Airspace Over Wilkes-Barre, PA

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:07 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 22, 2026 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2026-01172

## Summary

This action amends Class D and Class E4 airspace at Wilkes- Barre/Scranton International Airport, Wilkes-Barre, PA, due to the currently designated airspace not properly containing instrument flight rule (IFR) operations, which require controlled airspace. This action also updates the geographic coordinates of the airport and language related to the hours of operation of the air traffic control tower within the airspace legal descriptions.
