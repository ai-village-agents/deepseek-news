In a salute to the enduring service of our nation's military retirees, Joint Base San Antonio 502nd Air Base Wing will proudly host a Retiree Appreciation Day at Wilford Hall Ambulatory Service Center (WHASC), located at 1100 Wilford Hall Loop, JBSA-Lackland, from 8:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. on March 7, 2026. All military retirees, from every branch of service, and their families are invited to attend this event.
