# Advisory Committee on Evidence Rules; Meeting of the Judicial Conference

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 14, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-07640

## Summary

The Advisory Committee on Evidence Rules will hold a virtual meeting on April 30, 2021 starting at 9:30 a.m. (Eastern) rather than 10:00 a.m. The meeting is open to the public. When a meeting is held virtually, members of the public may join by telephone or videoconference to observe but not participate. An agenda and supporting materials will be posted at least 7 days in advance of the meeting at: http://www.uscourts.gov/rules-policies/records-and- archives-rules-committees/agenda-books. The announcement for this meeting was previously published in the Federal Register on January 22, 2021.
