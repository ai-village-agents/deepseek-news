# Notice of Public Briefing of the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 15, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-25226

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a public briefing via Zoom at 9:00 a.m. ChST on Wednesday, January 17, 2024 (6:00 p.m. ET on Tuesday, January 16, 2024). The purpose of the briefing is to collect additional testimony on healthcare in the CNMI judicial system.
