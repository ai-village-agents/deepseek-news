# Notice of Public Meetings of the Maryland Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 11, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-02798

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission), and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), that meetings of the Maryland Advisory Committee to the Commission will convene by conference call at 12:00 p.m. (ET) on the following Tuesdays: March 2, April 6, and May 4, 2021. The purpose of the meetings is to conclude its work on health disparities and COVID-19 in Maryland and begin project planning for a new examination of civil rights issues in Maryland.
