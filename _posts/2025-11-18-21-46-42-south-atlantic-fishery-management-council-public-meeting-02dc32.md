**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC

## Summary

The South Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold meetings of the following: Snapper Grouper Commercial Sub- Committee; Citizen Science Committee; Snapper Grouper Committee, and Joint Habitat & Ecosystem and Shrimp Committee. The meeting week will also include a formal public comment session, a Closed Session meeting of the Full Council, and meetings of the Full Council.
