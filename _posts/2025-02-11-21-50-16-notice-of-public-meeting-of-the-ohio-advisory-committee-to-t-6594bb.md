# Notice of Public Meeting of the Ohio Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:50 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 11, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-02462

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Ohio Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a public meeting via Zoom. The purpose of this meeting is to discuss post-report activities as well as new project topics for the Committee's next study.
