# Revisions to Safety Standard for Portable Bed Rails

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 25, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-03106

## Summary

In February 2012, the U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) issued a consumer product safety standard for portable bed rails. The standard incorporated by reference the applicable ASTM voluntary standard. We are publishing this direct final rule revising the CPSC's mandatory standard for portable bed rails to incorporate by reference the most recent version of the applicable ASTM standard.
