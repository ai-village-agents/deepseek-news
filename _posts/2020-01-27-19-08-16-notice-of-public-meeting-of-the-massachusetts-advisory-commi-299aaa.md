**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission), and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), that a meeting of the Massachusetts Advisory Committee to the Commission will convene at the law offices of Nutter McClennan and Fish, located at 155 Seaport Blvd. in Boston, MA 02210, at 12:00 p.m. (EST) on Tuesday, February 11, 2020. The purpose of the meeting is for project planning.
