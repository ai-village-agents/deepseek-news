William Houston, director of the Naval Nuclear Propulsion Program for the Department of the Navy and Department of Energy, addresses naval officers and chief petty officers from Reactor Department aboard Nimitz-class aircraft carrier USS Carl Vinson (CVN 70), Feb. 5, 2026.
