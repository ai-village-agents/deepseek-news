# Endangered Species; Receipt of Recovery Permit Application

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:36 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 01, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-21613

## Summary

We, the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, have received an application for a permit to conduct activities intended to enhance the propagation and survival of endangered species under the Endangered Species Act (ESA). We invite the public and local, State, Tribal, and Federal agencies to comment on this application. Before issuing the requested permit, we will take into consideration any information that we receive during the public comment period.
