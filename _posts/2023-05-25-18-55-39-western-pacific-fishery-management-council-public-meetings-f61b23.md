# Western Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 25, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-11216

## Summary

The Western Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold its 148th Scientific and Statistical Committee (SSC) and its 195th Council meeting to take actions on fishery management issues in the Western Pacific Region. The Council will also hold meetings of the following advisory groups and standing committees: American Samoa Advisory Panel (AP); Fishing Industry Advisory Committee (FIAC); American Samoa Regional Ecosystem Advisory Committee (REAC); American Samoa Standing Committee (SC); and Executive and Budget SC.
