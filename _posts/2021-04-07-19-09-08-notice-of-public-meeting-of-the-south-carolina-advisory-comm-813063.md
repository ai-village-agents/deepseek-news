# Notice of Public Meeting of the South Carolina Advisory Committee.

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the South Carolina Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting via- teleconference on Thursday, May 6, 2021, at 12 p.m. (EST) the purpose of the meeting is to for the Committee to discuss the release of its report on Subminimum Wages for People with Disabilities in South Carolina.
