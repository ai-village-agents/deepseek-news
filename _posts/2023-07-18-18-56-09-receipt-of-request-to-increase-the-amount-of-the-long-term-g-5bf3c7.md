# Receipt of Request To Increase the Amount of the Long-Term General Guarantee on the Interest of Secured Notes Issued by the Private Export Funding Corporation (PEFCO)

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 18, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-15096

## Summary

This Notice is to inform the public that Export-Import Bank of the United States ("EXIM") is expected to consider one or more requests to increase the amount of the long-term general guarantee on the interest of Secured Notes issued by the Private Export Funding Corporation (PEFCO). Comments received within the comment period specified below will be presented to the EXIM Board of Directors prior to any final action during the fiscal year ending on September 30, 2023.
