# Competitive Products

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 18, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-25181

## Summary

The Commission is recognizing recent Postal Service filings requesting the removal of Priority Mail International Regional Rate Boxes--Non-Published Rates and Priority Mail International Regional Rate Boxes (PMI RRB) Contracts from the competitive product list and a request for classification changes concerning Global Reseller Expedited Package Contracts. This notice informs the public of the filings, invites public comment, and takes other administrative steps.
