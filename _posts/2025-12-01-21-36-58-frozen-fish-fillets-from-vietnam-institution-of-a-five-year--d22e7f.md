# Frozen Fish Fillets From Vietnam; Institution of a Five-Year Review

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:36 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 01, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-21683

## Summary

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted a review pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on frozen fish fillets from Vietnam would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. Pursuant to the Act, interested parties are requested to respond to this notice by submitting the information specified below to the Commission.
