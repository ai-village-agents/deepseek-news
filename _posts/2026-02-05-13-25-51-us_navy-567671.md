NAVAL SUPPORT ACTIVITY SOUDA BAY, Greece (Feb. 4, 2026) Master-at-Arms 2nd Class Michael Zotoo and Master-at-Arms 2nd Class Kyle Doyle, both assigned to Naval Support Activity (NSA) Souda Bay, conduct a training demonstration with Military Working Dog Reo onboard NSA Souda Bay, Feb. 4, 2026. NSA Souda Bay is an operational ashore installation that enables and supports U.S., Allied, Coalition, and partner nation forces to preserve security and stability in the European, African, and Central Command areas of responsibility.
