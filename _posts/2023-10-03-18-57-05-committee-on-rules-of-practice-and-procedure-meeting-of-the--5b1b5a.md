# Committee on Rules of Practice and Procedure; Meeting of the Judicial Conference

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 03, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-21830

## Summary

The Committee on Rules of Practice and Procedure will hold a meeting in a hybrid format with remote attendance options on January 4, 2024 in Austin, TX. The meeting is open to the public for observation but not participation. An agenda and supporting materials will be posted at least 7 days in advance of the meeting at: https:// www.uscourts.gov/rules-policies/records-and-archives-rules-committees/ agenda-books.
