# Federal Support for Local Decision-Making Public Listening Session

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 08, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-14387

## Summary

This notice announces a public meeting hosted by the Bureau of Transportation Statistics (BTS) in support of the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (IIJA). The meeting will be a facilitated listening session that will document data and analytical challenges local decision-makers face making informed infrastructure investments. Based on feedback shared during the meeting, BTS will review the data and data analysis tools needed to assist local officials and will develop a road map to prioritize the Federal Government's support and funding requests for updating and developing the data assets.
