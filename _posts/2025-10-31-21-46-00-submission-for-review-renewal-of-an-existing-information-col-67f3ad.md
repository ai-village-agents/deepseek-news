**Original Published:** October 31, 2025 00:00 UTC

## Summary

Healthcare and Insurance, Office of Personnel Management (OPM) offers the general public and other federal agencies the opportunity to comment on the Consumer Assessment of Healthcare Providers and Systems (CAHPS[supreg]) surveys. The CAHPS[supreg] surveys ask consumers and patients to report on and evaluate their experiences with health care. These surveys cover topics that are important to consumers and focus on aspects of quality that consumers are best qualified to assess, such as the communication skills of providers and ease of access to health care services.
