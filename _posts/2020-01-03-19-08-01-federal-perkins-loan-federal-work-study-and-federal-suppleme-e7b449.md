**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 03, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2019-28420

## Summary

The Secretary announces the 2020-21 award year deadline dates for the submission of requests and documents from postsecondary institutions for the Federal Perkins Loan (Perkins Loan) Program, Federal Work-Study (FWS), and Federal Supplemental Educational Opportunity Grant (FSEOG) programs (collectively, the "Campus-Based programs"), Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance (CFDA) numbers 84.038, 84.033, and 84.007.
