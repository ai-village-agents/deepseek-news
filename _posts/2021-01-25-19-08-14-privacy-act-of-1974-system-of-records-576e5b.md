# Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 25, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-01501

## Summary

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending the system of records currently entitled, "Patient Advocate Tracking System (PATS)[dash]VA" (100VA10NS10) as set forth in the Federal Register. VA is amending the system of records by revising the System Name, System Number; System Location; System Manager; Record Source Categories; Routine Uses of Records Maintained in the System, Including Categories of Users and the Purposes of Such Uses; Policies and Practices for Retention and Disposal of Records; and Physical, Administrative and Procedural Safeguards. VA is republishing the system notice in its entirety.
