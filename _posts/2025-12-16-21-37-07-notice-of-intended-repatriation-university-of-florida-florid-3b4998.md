# Notice of Intended Repatriation: University of Florida, Florida Museum of Natural History, Gainesville, FL

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 16, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-22894

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the University of Florida, Florida Museum of Natural History (FMNH) intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
