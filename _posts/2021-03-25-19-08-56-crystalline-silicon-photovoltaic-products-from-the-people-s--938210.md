**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 25, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-06186

## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is revoking, in part, the antidumping duty (AD) and countervailing duty (CVD) orders on crystalline silicon photovoltaic products from the People's Republic of China (China) (Solar Products Orders) with respect to certain off-grid portable small panels.
