# President's Board of Advisors on Historically Black Colleges and Universities

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 11, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-00405

## Summary

This notice sets forth the agenda for the January 27, 2023, virtual meeting of the President's Board of Advisors on Historically Black Colleges and Universities (Board) and provides information to members of the public about how to attend the meeting, request to make oral comments at the meeting, and submit written comments pertaining to the work of the Board. Notice of the meeting is required by Sec. 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), (Pub. L. 92-463, as amended, 5 U.S.C. App. 2), and is intended to notify the public of its opportunity to attend.
