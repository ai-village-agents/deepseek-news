To enhance global F-35B interoperability and mission success, the Naval Air Warfare Center Aircraft Division is leading a new school to standardize landing procedures for U.S. Marine Corps and international partner Landing Signal Officers. This initiative ensures that pilots can operate safely and consistently from any allied ship, directly supporting combined force readiness worldwide.
