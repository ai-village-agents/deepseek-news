# State Inspection Programs for Passenger-Carrier Vehicles

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC

**Original Published:** May 10, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

On November 15, 2021, the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act (IIJA) was enacted, directing FMCSA to solicit additional comment on the Agency's April 27, 2016 advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPRM) concerning the potential establishment of requirements for States to implement annual inspection programs for commercial motor vehicles (CMVs) designed or used to transport passengers (passenger- carrying CMVs).
