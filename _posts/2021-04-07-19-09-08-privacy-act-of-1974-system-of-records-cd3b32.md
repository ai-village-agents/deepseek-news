# Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 07, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-07091

## Summary

The Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection (CFPB or Bureau) established CFPB.029, Public Health and Safety System, a system of records under the Privacy Act of 1974. This system of records maintains information collected in response to a public health emergency or similar health and safety incident, such as a pandemic, epidemic, or man-made emergency, that is necessary to ensure a safe and healthy environment for individuals who are occupying CFPB facilities, attending CFPB-sponsored events, or otherwise engaged in official business on behalf of the Bureau. This notice was previously published in the Federal Register for public comment. Upon receipt and consideration of a public comment, the Supplementary Information is being edited to clarify legal requirements that apply to the system.
