**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC

**Original Published:** March 09, 2023 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is approving revisions to the State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted by the New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection (NJDEP) in 2009 for New Jersey's motor vehicle inspection and maintenance (I/M) program. This final rule will maintain consistency between the State adopted rules and the federally approved New Jersey SIP. The EPA proposed to approve this rule on October 20, 2022, and received no comments.
