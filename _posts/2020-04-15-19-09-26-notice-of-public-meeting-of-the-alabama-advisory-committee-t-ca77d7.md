# Notice of Public Meeting of the Alabama Advisory Committee To Discuss Civil Rights Topics in the State

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 15, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-07907

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Alabama Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Thursday, April 9, 2020, at 1:00 p.m. (Central) for the purpose discussing edits to the final draft of the Voting Rights report. Additionally, the Committee may discuss future topics of study.
