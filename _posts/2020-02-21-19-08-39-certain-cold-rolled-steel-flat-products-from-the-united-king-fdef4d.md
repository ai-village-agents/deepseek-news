# Certain Cold-Rolled Steel Flat Products From the United Kingdom: Rescission of Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2018-2019

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 21, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-03501

## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) is rescinding the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain cold- rolled steel flat products (CR Steel) from the United Kingdom (UK) for the period September 1, 2018, through August 31, 2019, based on the timely withdrawal of the request for review.
