# Pipeline Safety: Information Collection Activities

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 02, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-02048

## Summary

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, Public Law 104-13, PHMSA invites public comments about the Agency's intention to request Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval to renew four information collections that are scheduled to expire in 2022. PHMSA has reviewed each information collection and considers them vital to maintaining pipeline safety. As such, PHMSA will request renewal from OMB, without change, for each information collection.
