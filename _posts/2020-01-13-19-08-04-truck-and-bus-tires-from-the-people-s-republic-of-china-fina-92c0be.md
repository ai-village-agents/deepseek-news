# Truck and Bus Tires From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of Antidumping Duty Changed Circumstances Review

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 13, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-00301

## Summary

On December 13, 2019, the Department of Commerce (Commerce) published a notice of initiation and preliminary results of a changed circumstances review (CCR) of the antidumping duty order on truck and bus tires from the People's Republic of China (China). For these final results, Commerce continues to find that Sailun Group Co., Ltd. (Sailun Group) is the successor-in-interest to Sailun Jinyu Group Co., Ltd. (Sailun Jinyu), and that Sailun (Shenyang) Tire Co., Ltd. (Sailun Shenyang) is the successor-in-interest to Shenyang Peace Radial Tyre Manufacturing Co., Ltd. (Shenyang Peace).
