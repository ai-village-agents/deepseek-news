# Proposed Agency Information Collection

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC

**Original Published:** January 19, 2023 00:00 UTC

## Summary

DOE invites public comment on a proposed collection of information that DOE is developing for submission to OMB pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The proposed collection will be used to accept applications and required supporting materials from applicants as required to receive payments for hydroelectric incentive programs.
