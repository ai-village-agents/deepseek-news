# Safety Zone; Monongahela River Mile Marker 22-22.86, Elizabeth, PA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 27, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-23445

## Summary

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for navigable waters of the Monongahela River Mile Marker 22-22.86 on October 27, 2022 and October 28, 2022 from 5 p.m. through 8 a.m. each day. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by filming on a barge. Entry of vessels or persons into this zone is prohibited unless specifically authorized by the Captain of the Port Pittsburgh.
