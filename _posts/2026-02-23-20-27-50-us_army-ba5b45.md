Assistant Secretary of the Army for Civil Works Adam R. Telle announced a major initiative, “Building Infrastructure, Not Paperwork,” for the Army’s Civil Works program. 

The plan consists of 27 initiatives grouped under five categories: 
•Maximizing the Ability to Deliver National Infrastructure  
•Cutting Red Tape  
•Focus on Efficiency  
•Transparency & Accountability  
•Prioritization

The benefits of cutting red tape in the Regulatory Program will be objective and more transparent jurisdictional determinations and permit decisions. 

The Tulsa District highlights potential for improved support for the Regulatory mission
