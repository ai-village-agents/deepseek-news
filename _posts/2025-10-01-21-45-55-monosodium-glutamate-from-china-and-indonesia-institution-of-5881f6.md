# Monosodium Glutamate From China and Indonesia; Institution of Five-Year Reviews

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:45 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 01, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-19126

## Summary

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended, to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty orders on monosodium glutamate ("MSG") from China and Indonesia would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. Pursuant to the Act, interested parties are requested to respond to this notice by submitting the information specified below to the Commission.
