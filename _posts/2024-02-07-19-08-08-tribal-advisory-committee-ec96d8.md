# Tribal Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 07, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-02488

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the Department of Agriculture and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), the Office of Tribal Relations is announcing a meeting of the Tribal Advisory Committee. The committee is authorized under the Agriculture Improvement Act of 2018 (the 2018 Farm Bill) and operates in compliance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act. The purpose of the committee is to provide advice and guidance to USDA on matters related to Tribal and Indian affairs.
