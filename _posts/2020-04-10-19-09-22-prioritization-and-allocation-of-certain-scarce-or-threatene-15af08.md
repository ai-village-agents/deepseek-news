# Prioritization and Allocation of Certain Scarce or Threatened Health and Medical Resources for Domestic Use

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 10, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-07659

## Summary

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is issuing a temporary rule to allocate certain scarce or threatened materials for domestic use, so that these materials may not be exported from the United States without explicit approval by FEMA. The rule covers five types of personal protective equipment (PPE), outlined below. While this rule remains in effect, and subject to certain exemptions stated below, no shipments of such designated materials may leave the United States without explicit approval by FEMA.
