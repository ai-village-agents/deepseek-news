# Notice of Inventory Completion: U.S. Department of the Interior, Bureau of Indian Affairs, Washington, DC

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 07, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-24223

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the U.S. Department of the Interior, Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA), has completed an inventory of human remains and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and any Indian Tribe. The human remains were removed from San Juan County, NM.
