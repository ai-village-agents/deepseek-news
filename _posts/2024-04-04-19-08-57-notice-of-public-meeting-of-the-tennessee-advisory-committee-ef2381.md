# Notice of Public Meeting of the Tennessee Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 04, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-07158

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission), and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) that a meeting of the Tennessee Advisory Committee to the Commission will convene by Zoom on Thursday, April 11, 2024, at 3:30 p.m. (CT). The purpose of the meeting is to discuss their draft report on Voting Rights.
