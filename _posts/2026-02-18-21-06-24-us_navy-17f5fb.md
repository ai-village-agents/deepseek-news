260206-N-KC192-1028 PORTSMOUTH, Va. (Feb. 6, 2026) Rear Adm. Robert Hawkins, commander, Naval Medical Forces Atlantic (NMFL), presents a a Navy and Marine Corps Achievement Medal to Hospital Corpsman 1st Class Charles Nallie, assigned to NMFL, during an award ceremony on board Naval Support Activity (NSA) Hampton Roads - Portsmouth Annex, Feb. 6, 2026. Nallie earned the award for actions taken as the Co-Chair for the 2025 Tidewater Classic Flag Football Challenge, a friendly football game between servicemembers to foster comradery and boost morale for commands in the area. (U.S. Navy photo by Mass Communication Specialist 2nd Class Levi Decker)
