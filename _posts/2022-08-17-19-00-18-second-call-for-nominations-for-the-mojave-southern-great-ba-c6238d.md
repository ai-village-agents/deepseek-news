# Second Call for Nominations for the Mojave-Southern Great Basin Resource Advisory Council, Nevada

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 17, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-17638

## Summary

The purpose of this notice is to request public nominations for the Bureau of Land Management's (BLM) Mojave-Southern Great Basin Resource Advisory Council (RAC) to fill existing vacancies as well as member terms that are scheduled to expire. The Council provides advice and recommendations to the BLM on land use planning and management of the public land resources located within the BLM's Battle Mountain, Ely, and Southern Nevada Districts.
