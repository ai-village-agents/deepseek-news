# Notice of Public Meeting of the Indiana Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 04, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07042

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Indiana Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a meeting via WebEx on Thursday, April 7, 2022, at 2:00 p.m. Eastern time for the purpose of discussing the concept stage in the planning process and exploring civil right topics for the Committee's project.
