# Agency Programs Subject to Intergovernmental Review

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 07, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07399

## Summary

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) is publishing an updated list of USDA financial assistance programs which States may choose to review under their Single Point of Contact (SPOC) intergovernmental review processes. These programs are also eligible for intergovernmental review by directly affected State, areawide, regional, and local entities if a State does not have a SPOC or chooses not to review an application for USDA financial assistance. USDA is streamlining the intergovernmental review process.
