# Notice of Realty Action: Classification for Lease and/or Conveyance of Public Lands in Humboldt County, Nevada

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 10, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-14446

## Summary

The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Humboldt River Field Office, has examined 40 acres of public land in Humboldt County, Nevada, and found the land suitable to classify for lease and/or conveyance under the provisions of the Recreation and Public Purposes (R&PP) Act, as amended. Humboldt County proposes to use the land to build a school complex with a K-8 elementary school and a high school in Orovada, Nevada, to improve educational services in the community.
