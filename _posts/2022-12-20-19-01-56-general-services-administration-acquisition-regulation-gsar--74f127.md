# General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation (GSAR); Streamline GSA Commercial Contract Clause Requirements

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 20, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-27106

## Summary

The General Services Administration (GSA) is proposing to amend the General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation (GSAR) to clarify and streamline the clauses contracting officers should reference in GSA acquisitions for commercial products and commercial services. The rulemaking proposes to update several clauses and other related parts by eliminating out of date references and any requirements that are not necessary by law.
