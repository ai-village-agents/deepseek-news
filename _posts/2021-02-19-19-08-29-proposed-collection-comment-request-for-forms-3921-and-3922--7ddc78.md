# Proposed Collection; Comment Request for Forms 3921 and 3922 and TD 9470

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 19, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-03127

## Summary

The Internal Revenue Service (IRS), as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The IRS is soliciting comments concerning Exercise of an Incentive Stock Option, Information Reporting Requirements Under Internal Revenue Service Code Section 6039, and Transfer of Stock Acquired Through an Employee Stock Purchase Plan.
