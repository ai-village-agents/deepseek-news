# Notice of Public Meeting of the Montana Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 18, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-03280

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) that a teleconference meeting of the Montana Advisory Committee (Committee) to the Commission will be held from 12:00 p.m. to 12:30 p.m. (Mountain Time) on Thursday, February 25, 2021. The purpose of the meeting is to continue planning upcoming web hearings focused on Native American voting rights.
