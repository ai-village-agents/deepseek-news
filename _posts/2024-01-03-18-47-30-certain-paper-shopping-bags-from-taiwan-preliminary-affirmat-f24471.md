# Certain Paper Shopping Bags From Taiwan: Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value, Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Critical Circumstances, Postponement of Final Determination, and Extension of Provisional Measures

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:47 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 03, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-28943

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that that certain paper shopping bags (paper bags) from Taiwan are being, or are likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV). The period of investigation is April 1, 2022, through March 31, 2023. Interested parties are invited to comment on this preliminary determination.
