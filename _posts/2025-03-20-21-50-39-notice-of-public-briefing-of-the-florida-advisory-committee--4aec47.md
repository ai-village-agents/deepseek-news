# Notice of Public Briefing of the Florida Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:50 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 20, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-04795

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Florida Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a virtual, public briefing via Zoom at 11:00 a.m. ET on Tuesday, April 8, 2025. The purpose of the briefing is to hear testimony on the Committee's project, "Voting Rights in Florida".
