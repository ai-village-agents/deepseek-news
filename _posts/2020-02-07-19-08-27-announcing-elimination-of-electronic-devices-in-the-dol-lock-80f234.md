# Announcing Elimination of Electronic Devices in the DOL Lock-Up Facility for Participating News Media Organizations With Pre-Release Access to Statistical Information

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 07, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-02383

## Summary

The Department of Labor (DOL) plans to eliminate use of all electronic devices in the lock-up facility and continue to rely on transmission sources readily available to the general public to provide simultaneous data access to all interested users. These sources include agency websites, social media channels, and email subscription lists.
