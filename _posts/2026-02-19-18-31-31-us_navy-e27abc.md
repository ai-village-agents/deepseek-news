KRISTIANSAND, Norway - Explosive Ordnance Disposal Mobile Unit EIGHT (EODMU-8), assigned to Commander, Task Force (CTF) 68, completed Exercise Arctic Specialist 26 alongside Allied forces from Norway, and Sweden, February 3-12, 2025. Arctic Specialist is an annual Norwegian-hosted multinational exercise focused on expeditionary mine countermeasure (ExMCM) operations and land-based explosive ordnance disposal tactics. The strategic location of the exercise provides valuable training in an extreme cold-weather environment where conditions are dynamic, and the operating environment continues to evolve.
