## Summary

On March 14, 2022, the Department of Education (Department) published in the Federal Register a notice of proposed priorities, requirements, definitions, and selection criteria (NPP) for CSP SE Grants, CMO Grants, and Developer Grants, Assistance Listing Numbers (ALNs) 84.282A, 84.282M, 84.282B, and 84.282E. The document established a deadline date of April 13, 2022, for the submission of public comments. We extend the comment period until April 18, 2022.
