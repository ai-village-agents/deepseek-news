**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 13, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the South Carolina Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold web briefings to hear testimony on Civil Asset Forfeiture on the following dates: Thursday, February 3, 2022, at 12:00 p.m. ET and Thursday, February 10, 2022, at 12:00 p.m. ET.
