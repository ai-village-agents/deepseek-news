The Joint Multinational Training Group-Ukraine held a transfer of authority ceremony at the 7th Army Training Command headquarters on Feb. 19, 2026. During the ceremony, Task Force Saber, composed of the 278th Armored Cavalry Regiment from the Tennessee National Guard, transferred mission responsibility to
Task Force Alpine, the 86th Infantry Brigade Combat Team (Mountain) from the Vermont National Guard.
