**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 28, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-01396

## Summary

The Department of Labor (DOL) is submitting the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) sponsored information collection request (ICR) titled, "Hexavalent Chromium standards for General Industry, Shipyard Employment, and Construction" to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval for continued use, without change, in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA). Public comments on the ICR are invited.
