The Office of the Naval Inspector General hosted a comprehensive two-day training course on interview and interrogation techniques on January 12 -13, 2026. The course hosted over 200 investigative professionals and was held in a hybrid format with over 40 participants gathered at the Admiral Gooding Center at the Washington Navy Yard, while an additional 160 joined virtually.
