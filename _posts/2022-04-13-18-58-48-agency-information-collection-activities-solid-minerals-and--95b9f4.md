# Agency Information Collection Activities: Solid Minerals and Geothermal Collections

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 13, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07893

## Summary

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 ("PRA"), ONRR is proposing to renew an information collection. Through this Information Collection Request ("ICR"), ONRR seeks renewed authority to collect information necessary to report the production and royalties on solid minerals and geothermal resources from Federal and Indian lands. ONRR uses forms ONRR-4292 (Coal Washing Allowance Report); ONRR-4293 (Coal Transportation Allowance Report); ONRR-4430 (Solid Minerals Production and Royalty Report); and ONRR-4440 (Solid Minerals Sales Summary) as part of these information collection requirements.
