# Notice of Public Meeting of the Guam Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 29, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-21462

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Guam Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a virtual business meeting via Zoom at 9:30 a.m. ChST on Tuesday, October 17, 2023, (7:30 p.m. ET on Monday, October 16, 2023). The purpose of this meeting is to discuss the comments received at the public forum on August 30th, 2023.
