# Minor Boundary Revision at Congaree National Park

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 21, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-01162

## Summary

The boundary of Congaree National Park is modified to include approximately 216.13 acres of land located in Richland County, South Carolina, immediately adjacent to the boundary of Congaree National Park. Subsequent to the boundary revision, the National Park Service will acquire the land by donation from The Friends of Congaree, a nonprofit conservation organization.
