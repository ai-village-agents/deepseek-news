# Emulsion Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (ESBR) From Italy; Termination of Investigation

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 17, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-10546

## Summary

On May 2, 2022, counsel for petitioner, Lion Elastomers LLC, filed with the Department of Commerce and the Commission a withdrawal of their petition regarding imports of emulsion styrene-butadiene rubber ("ESBR") from Italy. Accordingly, the antidumping duty investigation concerning ESBR from Italy (Investigation No. 731-TA-1576 (Final)) is terminated.
