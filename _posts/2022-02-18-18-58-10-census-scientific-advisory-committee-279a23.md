# Census Scientific Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 18, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-03488

## Summary

The Census Bureau is giving notice of a virtual meeting of the Census Scientific Advisory Committee (CSAC). The Committee will address policy, research, and technical issues relating to a full range of Census Bureau programs and activities, including decennial, economic, field operations, information technology, and statistics. Last minute changes to the schedule are possible, which could prevent giving advance public notice of schedule adjustments. Please visit the Census Advisory Committees website at http://www.census.gov/cac for the CSAC meeting information, including the agenda, and how to join the meeting.
