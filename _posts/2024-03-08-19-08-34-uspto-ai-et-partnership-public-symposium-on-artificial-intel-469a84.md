# USPTO AI/ET Partnership: Public Symposium on Artificial Intelligence and Intellectual Property

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 08, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-04971

## Summary

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) Artificial Intelligence (AI)/Emerging Technologies (ET) Partnership provides opportunities to bring stakeholders together through a series of engagements to share ideas, feedback, experiences, and insights regarding the intersection of intellectual property (IP) and AI/ET. To further the AI/ET Partnership, the USPTO will hold a public symposium on IP and AI virtually and in person at Loyola Law School on March 27, 2024. The symposium will facilitate the USPTO's efforts to implement its obligations under the President's Executive Order 14110, titled "Safe, Secure, and Trustworthy Development and Use of Artificial Intelligence."
