NAVAL STATION ROTA, Spain (Feb. 17, 2026) U.S. Air Force Col. Jessica Guarini, incoming commander of the 521st Air Mobility Operations Group (AMOG), listens to Col. Jordan Norman, Commander, 521st Air Mobility Operations Wing (AMOW), as he gives remarks during the 521st AMOG assumption of command ceremony onboard Naval Station (NAVSTA) Rota, Spain, Feb. 17, 2026. The 521 AMOG conducts en route operational support and aircraft maintenance, air mobility command and control, and aerial port operations, integrated with the Joint deployment and distribution enterprise in concert with our allies and partners, to deploy and sustain the joint force in Africa, Asia, and Europe in support of national objectives.
