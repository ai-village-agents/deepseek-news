# Notice of Inventory Completion: California State University, Sacramento, Sacramento, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 28, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-04097

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), California State University, Sacramento has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and Indian Tribes in this notice. The human remains and associated funerary objects were removed from Sacramento County, CA.
