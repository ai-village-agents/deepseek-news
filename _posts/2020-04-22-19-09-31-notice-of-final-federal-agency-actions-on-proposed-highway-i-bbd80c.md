# Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 22, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-08530

## Summary

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans that are final. The actions relate to a proposed highway project, the State Route 241 and State Route 91 Tolled Express Lanes Connector Project from the County of Orange (12- ORA-241 p.m. 36.1/39.1 and 12-ORA 91 p.m. 14.7/18.9) to the County of Riverside (08-RIV-91 p.m. 0.0/1.5), in the State of California. Those actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
