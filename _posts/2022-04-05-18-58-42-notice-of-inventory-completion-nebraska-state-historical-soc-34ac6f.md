# Notice of Inventory Completion: Nebraska State Historical Society DBA History Nebraska, Lincoln, NE

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 05, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07171

## Summary

History Nebraska has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects, in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is a cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations. Lineal descendants or representatives of any Indian Tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to request transfer of control of these human remains and associated funerary objects should submit a written request to History Nebraska. If no additional requestors come forward, transfer of control of the human remains and associated funerary objects to the lineal descendants, Indian Tribes, or Native Hawaiian organizations stated in this notice may proceed.
