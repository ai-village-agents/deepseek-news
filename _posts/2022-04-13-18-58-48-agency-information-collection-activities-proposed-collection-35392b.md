# Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request; Federal Assistance to Individuals and Households Program

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 13, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07889

## Summary

The Federal Emergency Management Agency, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public to take this opportunity to comment on an extension, without change, of a currently approved information collection. In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, this notice seeks comments concerning the Federal Emergency Management Agency's (FEMA's) Individuals and Households Program, providing financial assistance to individuals whose primary residences were destroyed as a result of a Presidentially-declared disaster.
