# Safety Zone; Ocean Cup, Catalina Island, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-18513

## Summary

The Coast Guard is establishing a safety zone near Ship Rock, Catalina Island, in support of the Ocean Cup Pacific Rum Run. This action is necessary to protect the area near Ship Rock, Catalina Island, public vessels, and the high-speed vessels participating in the event. This regulation would prohibit vessels from entering into, transiting through, or remaining within the designated area during the enforcement period unless specifically authorized by the Captain of the Port, Los Angeles--Long Beach, or his designated representative.
