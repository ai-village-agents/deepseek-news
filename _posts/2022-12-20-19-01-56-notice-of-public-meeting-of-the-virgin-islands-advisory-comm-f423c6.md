# Notice of Public Meeting of the Virgin Islands Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 20, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-27592

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Virgin Islands Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a web meeting via Zoom at 12 p.m. AST (11 a.m. ET) on Tuesday, January 3, 2023, for the purpose of identifying potential speakers for a series of briefing on voting rights.
