## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) published its notice in the Federal Register of November 30, 2020, in which Commerce announced the final results of the 2017-2018 administrative review of the antidumping duty (AD) order on certain softwood lumber products from Canada. In preparing the liquidation instructions, Commerce discovered that this notice inadvertently listed incorrect names for certain companies under review.
