Naval Supply Systems Command Fleet Logistics Center Jacksonville supply officers attend the Navy Supply Corps Office of Personnel (OP) Roadshow February 17, 2026 at Naval Station Mayport, Florida.

This multi-day engagement gives Supply Officers the opportunity to connect directly with community leadership and detailers to discuss career management, professional development, education opportunities, and mentorship. (Courtesy photo)
