# Public Hearing

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 12, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-07602

## Summary

The Susquehanna River Basin Commission will hold a public hearing on May 4, 2023. The Commission will hold this hearing in person and telephonically. At this public hearing, the Commission will hear testimony on the projects listed in the Supplementary Information section of this notice. Such projects are intended to be scheduled for Commission action at its next business meeting, tentatively scheduled for June 15, 2023, which will be noticed separately. The public should note that this public hearing will be the only opportunity to offer oral comments to the Commission for the listed projects. The deadline for the submission of written comments is May 15, 2023.
