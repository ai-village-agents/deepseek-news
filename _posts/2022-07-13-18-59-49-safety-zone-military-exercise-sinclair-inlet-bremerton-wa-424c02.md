# Safety Zone; Military Exercise, Sinclair Inlet, Bremerton, WA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 13, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-14895

## Summary

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zones for navigable waters within a 500 yard radius of a position located in Sinclair Inlet, Bremerton, WA. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by a military exercise involving helocast operations. Entry of vessels or persons into this zone is prohibited unless specifically authorized by the Captain of the Port Puget Sound.
