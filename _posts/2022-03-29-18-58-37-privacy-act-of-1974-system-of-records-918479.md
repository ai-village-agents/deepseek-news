# Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-06610

## Summary

CIGIE proposes to establish a system of records that is subject to the Privacy Act of 1974. CIGIE proposes this system of records to more efficiently track records in furtherance of its statutory mandate to maintain one or more academies for the professional training of auditors, inspectors, evaluators, and other personnel of the various offices of Inspector General per the Inspector General Act of 1978, as amended.
