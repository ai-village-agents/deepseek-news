# Advisory Committee on Bankruptcy Rules; Meeting of the Judicial Conference

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 17, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-05481

## Summary

The Advisory Committee on Bankruptcy Rules will hold a remote meeting on April 2 and April 3, 2020. The meeting will be open to public via telephonic conference for listening but not participation. An agenda and supporting materials will be posted at least 7 days in advance of the meeting at: http://www.uscourts.gov/rules-policies/ records-and-archives-rules-committees/agenda-books. The announcement for this meeting was previously published in 85 FR 3421.
