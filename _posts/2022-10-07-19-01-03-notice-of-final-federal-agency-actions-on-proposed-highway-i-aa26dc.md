# Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 07, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-21836

## Summary

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans that are final. The actions relate to a proposed highway project, the California Aqueduct Bridge Rehabilitation and Seismic Retrofit on State Route 166 about 2.6 miles east of Old River Road and 5 miles west of Interstate 5 in the County of Kern, State of California. Those actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
