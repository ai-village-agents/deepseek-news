**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC

## Summary

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans that are final. The actions relate to a proposed highway project, State Route 70, postmiles 46.0 to 47.0, approximately 4.3 miles northeast of the town of Pulga in the County Butte, State of California. Those actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
