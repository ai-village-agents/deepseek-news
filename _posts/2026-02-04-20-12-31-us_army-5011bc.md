U.S. Army Soldiers, all assigned to the 94th Army Air and Missile Defense Command, participate in a commander’s update brief (CUB) during their Keen Edge ’26 (KE26) exercise at Joint Base Pearl Harbor Hickam, Hawaii, February 2, 2026. KE26 was held from January 28 – February 4, 2026, and consisted of daily afternoon CUBs that helped all leadership end up with the same information about the simulated exercises that happened earlier in the day.
