Spc. Oscar Moore, with 41st Engineer Battalion, 10th Mountain Division (LI) receives a Volunteer of the Quarter certificate from the U.S. Army Medical Department Activity-Fort Drum command team, recognizing his volunteer work at animal shelters and food banks. More than 30 Fort Drum community members were honored for their selfless service during the quarterly volunteer recognition ceremony Feb. 20 inside The Peak.
