# Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: The Filson Historical Society, Louisville, KY

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 22, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-28182

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Filson Historical Society intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The cultural items were removed from Essex County, VA.
