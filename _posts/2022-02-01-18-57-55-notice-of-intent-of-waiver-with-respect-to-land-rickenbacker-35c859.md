# Notice of Intent of Waiver With Respect to Land; Rickenbacker International Airport, Columbus, Ohio

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 01, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-02023

## Summary

The FAA is considering a proposal to change approximately 328 acres of airport land from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use and to authorize the lease and ultimate sale of airport property located at Rickenbacker International Airport, Columbus, Ohio. The aforementioned land is not needed for aeronautical use. The property is located southeast of the airfield and currently consists of vacant land, paved roadways, fencing and utilities. The land is proposed to be used to expand the Rickenbacker Global Logistics Park (RGLP) and all activities necessary to prepare the site as a Cargo Campus for development capable of accommodating growth in bulk warehouse/distribution facilities.
