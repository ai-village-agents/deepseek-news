**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC

## Summary

The Internal Revenue Service, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The IRS is soliciting comments concerning modifications of commercial mortgage loans held by a real estate mortgage investment conduit.
