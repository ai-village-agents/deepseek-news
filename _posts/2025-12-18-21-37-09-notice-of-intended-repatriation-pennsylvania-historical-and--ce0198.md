# Notice of Intended Repatriation: Pennsylvania Historical and Museum Commission, The State Museum of Pennsylvania, Harrisburg, PA

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 18, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-23162

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Pennsylvania Historical and Museum Commission (PHMC), The State Museum of Pennsylvania intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of sacred objects and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
