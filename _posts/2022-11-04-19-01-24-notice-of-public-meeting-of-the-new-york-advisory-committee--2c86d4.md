**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 04, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the New York Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a public meeting via Zoom on Friday, November 18, 2022, at 1 p.m. ET to conduct Committee business and hear testimony regarding the child welfare system in New York.
