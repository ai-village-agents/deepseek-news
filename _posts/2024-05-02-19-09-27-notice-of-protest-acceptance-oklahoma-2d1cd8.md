# Notice of Protest Acceptance; Oklahoma

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC

## Summary

On September 29, 2020, the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) published in the Federal Register, Volume 85, Number 189, on page 61028, a notice entitled "Notice of Filing of Plats of Survey; New Mexico; Oklahoma." The Notice stated that four supplemental plats were scheduled to be officially filed 30 days after the date, unless a person or party who wished to protest any of these surveys filed a timely, written Notice of Protest. On October 23, 2020, the Bureau of Land Management received a timely protest to the filing of the four supplemental plats.
