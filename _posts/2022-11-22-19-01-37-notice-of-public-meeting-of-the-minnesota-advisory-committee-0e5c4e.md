**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Minnesota Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a virtual business meeting via Zoom at 1 p.m. CT on Monday, December 12, 2022, to discuss the next topic of study for the Committee.
