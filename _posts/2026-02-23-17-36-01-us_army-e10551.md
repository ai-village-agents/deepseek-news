Fort Carson and the Directorate of Emergency Services leadership officially activated the new Fire Station 34 by uncoupling a fire hose in a traditional firefighter ceremony Feb. 18, 2026. The 8,000-square-foot facility replaces the previous Station 34, which was activated in 1912 at Turkey Creek Recreational Center.
