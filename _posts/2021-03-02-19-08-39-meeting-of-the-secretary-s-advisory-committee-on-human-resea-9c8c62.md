**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 02, 2021 00:00 UTC

## Summary

Pursuant to Section 10(a) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, U.S.C. Appendix 2, notice is hereby given that the Secretary's Advisory Committee on Human Research Protections (SACHRP) will hold a meeting that will be open to the public. Information about SACHRP, the full meeting agenda, and instructions for linking to public access will be posted on the SACHRP website at http://www.dhhs.gov/ohrp/sachrp- committee/meetings/index.html.
