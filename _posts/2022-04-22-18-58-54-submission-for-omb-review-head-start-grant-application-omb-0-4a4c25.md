# Submission for OMB Review; Head Start Grant Application; (OMB #0970-0207)

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 22, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-08651

## Summary

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF) is requesting a 3-year extension of the Head Start Grant Application Instrument and Instructions (OMB #0970-0207, expiration 04/30/2022). There are no substantive changes requested to the instruments, but a few minor changes have been made to the reporting structure of applications related to facilities to reflect the information already being submitted by grant recipients.
