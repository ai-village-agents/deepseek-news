Volunteering for a regional food bank and a youth robotics competition were among the efforts that recently earned Fire Controlman (Aegis) 1st Class Jason Kilpatrick an honor from a local business association. The seasoned sailor with Naval Surface Warfare Center, Port Hueneme Division received the recognition from the West Ventura County Business Alliance during its 28th Annual Military Appreciation Dinner.
