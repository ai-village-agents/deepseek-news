U.S. Marine Corps Master Gunnery Sgt. Canon Richard, right, a communications chief with 13th Marine Expeditionary Unit, I Marine Expeditionary Force, reads the citation for a Navy and Marine Corps Commendation Medal awarded to Gunnery Sgt. Jose Antonio Lopez, left, a network chief with 13th MEU, I MEF, during an award ceremony on Marine Corps Base Camp Pendleton, California, Feb. 17, 2026. Lopez received his end of tour award for exemplary performance and successful execution of multiple critical tasks while effectively managing several key leadership roles during his time at the unit. (U.S. Marine Corps photo by Cpl. Martha Linares)
