# Special Local Regulation; Back River, Baltimore County, MD

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 08, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-14378

## Summary

The Coast Guard is establishing temporary special local regulations for certain waters of Back River. This action is necessary to provide for the safety of life on these navigable waters located in Baltimore County, MD during activities associated with an air show event from July 15, 2022 through July 17, 2022. This regulation prohibits persons and vessels from entering the regulated area unless authorized by the Captain of the Port, Maryland-National Capital Region or the Coast Guard Event Patrol Commander.
