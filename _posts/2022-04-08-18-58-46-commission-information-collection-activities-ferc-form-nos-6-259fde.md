# Commission Information Collection Activities (FERC Form Nos. 60, 60A, FERC-61, and FERC-555A); Consolidated Comment Request; Extension

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 08, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07401

## Summary

In compliance with the requirements of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission (Commission or FERC) is soliciting public comment on the currently approved information collections, FERC Form Nos. 60 and 60A (Annual Report of Centralized Service Companies), FERC-61 (Narrative Description of Service Company Functions), and FERC-555A (Preservation of Records Companies and Service Companies Subject to PUHCA). The Commission published a 60-day notice in the Federal Register on November 18, 2021, and received no comments on the 60-day notice.
