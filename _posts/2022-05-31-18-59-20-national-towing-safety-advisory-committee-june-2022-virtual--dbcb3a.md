# National Towing Safety Advisory Committee; June 2022 Virtual Meeting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 31, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-11560

## Summary

The National Towing Safety Advisory Committee (Committee) will conduct a virtual meeting to review and discuss matters relating to shallow-draft inland navigation, coastal waterway navigation, and towing safety. The Committee will also discuss new and existing tasking to include the Final Report for National Towing Safety Advisory Committee Task 21-01, that sought to identify the parameters Coast Guard officials should use to determine whether a vessel inspected under subchapters other than Subchapter M is performing "Occasional Towing". This virtual meeting will be open to the public.
