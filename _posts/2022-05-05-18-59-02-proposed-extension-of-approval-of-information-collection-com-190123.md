**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC

**Document Number:** 2022-09645

## Summary

Pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC) requests comment on a proposed extension of approval of information collection under the safety standard for automatic residential garage door operators. CPSC will consider all comments received in response to this notice before requesting an extension of this collection of information from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB).
