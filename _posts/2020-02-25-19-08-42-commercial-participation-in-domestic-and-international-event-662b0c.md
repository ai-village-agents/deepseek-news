# Commercial Participation in Domestic and International Events on Safety, Sustainability, and Emerging Markets in Outer Space

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 25, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-03684

## Summary

Following up on the success of the Space Enterprise Summit in June 2019, the U.S. Department of State seeks commercial space industry participation in a series of domestic and international events promoting space commerce as well as best practices for safety, sustainability, and emerging markets in outer space. These events and industry participation are in line with the President's Space Policy Directive-2 regarding Streamlining Regulations on Commercial Use of Space and Space Policy Directive-3 concerning National Space Traffic Management Policy.
