# Benzovindiflupyr; Pesticide Tolerances

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 09, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-02516

## Summary

This regulation establishes tolerances for residues of benzovindiflupyr in or on lowbush blueberries, ginseng, and sugar beet roots, leaves, and dried pulp. Interregional Research Project Number 4 (IR-4) and Syngenta Crop Protection requested these tolerances under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA).
