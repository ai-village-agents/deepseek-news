## Summary

The United States Postal Service[supreg] (Postal Service) is proposing to revise a Customer Privacy Act System of Records (SOR). This modification is being made to establish a new retention period for cloud-based mailpiece image storage, specific to the Informed Delivery[supreg] feature.
