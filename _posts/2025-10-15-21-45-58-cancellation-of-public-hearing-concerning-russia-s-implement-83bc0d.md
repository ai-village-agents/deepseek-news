# Cancellation of Public Hearing Concerning Russia's Implementation of Its WTO Commitments

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:45 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 15, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-19539

## Summary

USTR sought public comments to assist in the preparation of its annual report to Congress on Russia's implementation of its obligations as a Member of the World Trade Organization (WTO). Because USTR received no requests to testify, USTR is canceling the public hearing that was scheduled to take place on October 15, 2025.
