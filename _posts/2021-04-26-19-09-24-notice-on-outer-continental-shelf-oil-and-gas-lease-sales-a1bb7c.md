# Notice on Outer Continental Shelf Oil and Gas Lease Sales

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 26, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-08626

## Summary

Pursuant to the Bureau of Ocean Energy Management (BOEM) regulatory restrictions on joint bidding, the Director of BOEM is publishing a List of Restricted Joint Bidders. Each entity within one of the following groups is restricted from bidding with any entity in any of the other following groups at Outer Continental Shelf oil and gas lease sales held during the bidding period of May 1, 2021, through October 31, 2021.
