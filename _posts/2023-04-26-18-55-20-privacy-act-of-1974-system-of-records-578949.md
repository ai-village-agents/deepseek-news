# Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 26, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-08752

## Summary

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, the DoD is establishing a new Department-wide system of records titled, "DoD Claims Management Records," DoD-0016. This system of records covers records concerning the investigation, adjudication, and settlement of claims against or by the DoD.
