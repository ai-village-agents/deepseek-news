# Information Collection; Improving Customer Experience (OMB Circular A-11, Section 280 Implementation)

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 24, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO or Agency) as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, is announcing an opportunity for public comment on a new proposed collection of information by the Agency. Under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA), Federal agencies are required to publish notice in the Federal Register concerning each proposed collection of information, and to allow 60 days for public comment in response to the notice. This notice solicits comments on new collection proposed by the Agency.
