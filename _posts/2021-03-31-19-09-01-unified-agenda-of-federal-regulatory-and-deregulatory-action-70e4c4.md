# Unified Agenda of Federal Regulatory and Deregulatory Actions

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 31, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-04345

## Summary

This document is required pursuant to the Regulatory Flexibility Act. It provides information about the Unified Agenda published on December 9, 2020 at www.reginfo.gov/public/do/eAgendaMain under the previous administration. The Secretary of Education publishes a semiannual agenda of Federal regulatory and deregulatory actions. The agenda is issued under the authority of section 4(b) of Executive Order 12866, "Regulatory Planning and Review." The purpose of the agenda is to encourage more effective public participation in the regulatory process by providing the public with early information about the regulatory actions we plan to take.
