# Depository Library Council Meeting

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 29, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-16661

## Summary

The Depository Library Council (DLC) will meet virtually in conjunction with the Federal Depository Library Conference from Thursday, September 25, 2025, through Friday, September 26, 2025. The meetings will take place online, and anyone may register to attend at https://www.fdlp.gov/2025-fdl-conference. Closed captioning will also be provided. The purpose is to discuss matters affecting the Federal Depository Library Program. All sessions are open to the public.
