## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) determines that Hyundai Steel Co. (Hyundai Steel) and Dongkuk Steel Mill Co., Ltd. (DSM), exporters/producers of certain cut-to-length plate from the Republic of Korea (Korea), received de minimis net subsidy rates during the period of review (POR) January 1, 2018 through December 31, 2018.
