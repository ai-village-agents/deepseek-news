**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC

## Summary

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing two general licenses (GLs) issued in the Ukraine-/Russia-Related sanctions program: GL 15K, which was previously issued on OFAC's website and is now expired, and GL 15L, which was also previously issued on OFAC's website and had an expiration date of May 25, 2022.
