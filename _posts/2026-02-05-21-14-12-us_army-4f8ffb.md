District of Columbia National Guard Soldiers conduct emergency response training at the D.C. Armory in Washington, D.C., Feb. 5, 2026. The training ensures Soldiers remain prepared to rapidly respond to emergencies while supporting public safety operations throughout the National Capital Region. About 2,600 National Guard members are supporting the D.C. Safe and Beautiful mission, providing critical assistance to the Metropolitan Police Department to help ensure the safety of residents, commuters and visitors throughout the District. (U.S. Army National Guard photo by Spc. Paris Hayes)
