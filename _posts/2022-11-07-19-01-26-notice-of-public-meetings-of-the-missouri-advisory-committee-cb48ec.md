# Notice of Public Meetings of the Missouri Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 07, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-24198

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Missouri Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Thursday, November 10, 2022 at 11:30 a.m.-1 p.m. central time. The Committee will continue orientation and begin identifying potential civil rights topics for their first study of the 2022-2026 term.
