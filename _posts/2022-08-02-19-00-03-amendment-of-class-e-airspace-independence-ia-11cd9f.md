**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC

**Original Published:** August 02, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

This action amends the Class E airspace at Independence, IA. This action as the result of an airspace review conducted as part of the decommissioning of the Wapsie non-directional beacon (NDB). The name and geographic coordinates of the airport are also being updated to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
