RAPID CITY, S.D. — The LUH-72 Lakota helicopter plays a critical role in the South Dakota Army National Guard’s aviation mission, supporting search and rescue, law enforcement, and emergency response operations statewide. Chief Warrant Officer 2 Ben English, a Lakota pilot with Bravo Company, 1/112th Aviation Battalion, said the aircraft’s versatility and year-round training enable crews to operate safely and effectively across challenging terrain. Through regular partnerships with civilian agencies, the SDARNG strengthens readiness while serving communities throughout South Dakota.
