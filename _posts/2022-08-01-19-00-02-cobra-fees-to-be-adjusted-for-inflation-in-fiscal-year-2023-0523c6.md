**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC

## Summary

This document announces that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is adjusting certain customs user fees and corresponding limitations established by the Consolidated Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act (COBRA) for Fiscal Year 2023 in accordance with the Fixing America's Surface Transportation Act (FAST Act) as implemented by the CBP regulations.
