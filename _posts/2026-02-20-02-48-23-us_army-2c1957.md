U.S. Army Staff Sgt. Timothy Boyd, an advanced culinary noncommissioned officer representing the Tifton-based 110th Combat Sustainment Support Battalion, 78th Troop Command, Georgia Army National Guard, gives a fist-bump to U.S. Army Command Sgt. Maj. Clint Cowser, senior enlisted leader of the 78th Troop Command, after completing the Combat Water Survival Training event of the 2026 78th Troop Command-78th Aviation Troop Command Joint Best Warrior Competition, Feb. 19, 2026, at the at the Cobb Aquatic Center, Marietta, Georgia. The competition demonstrates the readiness and lethality of the 78th Troop Command and 78th Aviation Troop Command Soldiers by testing the competitors with physical fitness assessments, written exams, and other warrior tasks and battle drills.
