# Proposed Collection; Requesting comments on Forms W-2/W-3 Series

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 02, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-09273

## Summary

The Internal Revenue Service (IRS), as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The IRS is soliciting comments concerning Forms W-2, W-2c, W- 2AS, W-2GU, W-2VI, W-3, W-3c, W-3PR, W-3cPR, and W-3SS.
