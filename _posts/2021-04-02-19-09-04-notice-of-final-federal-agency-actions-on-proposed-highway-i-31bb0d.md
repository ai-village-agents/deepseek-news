# Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 02, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-06857

## Summary

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans that are final. The actions relate to the proposed Dublin Boulevard--North Canyons Parkway Extension Project (Federal-aid project number RTPL-5432(019)) in the cities of Dublin and Livermore, County of Alameda, State of California. Those actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
