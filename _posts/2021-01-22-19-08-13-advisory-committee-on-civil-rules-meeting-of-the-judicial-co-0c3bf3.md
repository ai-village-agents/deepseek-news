# Advisory Committee on Civil Rules; Meeting of the Judicial Conference

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 22, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-01380

## Summary

The Advisory Committee on Civil Rules will hold a virtual meeting on April 23, 2021. The meeting is open to the public. When a meeting is held virtually, members of the public may join by telephone or video conference to observe but not participate. An agenda and supporting materials will be posted at least 7 days in advance of the meeting at: http://www.uscourts.gov/rules-policies/records-and- archives-rules-committees/agenda-books.
