**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 7) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ"), finding respondent Top Golf Equipment Co.
