# Submission of Information Collections for OMB Review; Comment Request; Special Financial Assistance Information

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 31, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-11564

## Summary

The Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation (PBGC) is requesting that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) extend approval under the Paperwork Reduction Act of collections of information contained in PBGC's regulation on special financial assistance. The purpose of the information collections is to gather information necessary for PBGC to operate the special financial assistance program. This notice informs the public of PBGC's request and solicits public comment on the collections of information.
