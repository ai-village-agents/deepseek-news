# Metro-North Railroad's Request To Amend Its Positive Train Control Safety Plan and Positive Train Control System

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 17, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-10475

## Summary

This document provides the public with notice that, on May 9, 2023, Metro-North Railroad (MNR) submitted a request for amendment (RFA) to its FRA-approved Positive Train Control Safety Plan (PTCSP). As this RFA involves a request for FRA's approval of proposed material modifications to an FRA-certified positive train control (PTC) system, FRA is publishing this notice and inviting public comment on the railroad's RFA to its PTCSP.
