## Summary

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) regulations, FSIS is announcing its intention to renew an approved information collection regarding specified risk materials (SRMs) in cattle. The approval for this information collection will expire on April 30, 2026. FSIS is making no changes to the information collection.
