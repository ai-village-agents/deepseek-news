**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC

## Summary

Due to recent legislation, the Commission is initiating a review of regulations that relate to postal cost attribution in order to determine whether any revisions to those regulations, or to any analytical principles (including any costing methodologies or cost models) used in postal cost attribution, are appropriate. The Commission takes certain administrative steps and invites public comment.
