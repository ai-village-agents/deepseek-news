(Feb. 4, 2026) Navy Builder 1st Class John Henry, Naval Construction Training Center’s (NCTC) ‘A’ school instructor supervisor, explains NCTC’s training pipeline to Rear Adm. Greg Huffman, Commander of Naval Education and Training Command (NETC), during a tour of NCTC Gulfport facilities in Gulfport, Miss., Feb. 4. NETC is the largest shore command in the U.S. Navy and serves as the foundry to the Fleet by recruiting and delivering world-class training to highly skilled, operational and battle-ready warfighters.
