# Notice of Inventory Completion: Maine State Museum, Augusta, ME

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 09, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-09767

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Maine State Museum has completed an inventory of human remains and associated funerary objects and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and associated funerary objects and any Indian Tribe. The human remains and associated funerary objects were removed from Cumberland, Franklin, Sagadahoc, York, Oxford, and Knox Counties, ME.
