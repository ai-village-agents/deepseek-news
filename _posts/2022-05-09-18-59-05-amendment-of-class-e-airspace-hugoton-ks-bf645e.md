# Amendment of Class E Airspace; Hugoton, KS

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 09, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-09676

## Summary

This action amends the Class E airspace at Hugoton, KS. This action as the result of an airspace review conducted as part of the decommissioning of the Hugoton non-directional beacon (NDB). The geographic coordinates of the airport are also being updated to coincide with the FAA's aeronautical database.
