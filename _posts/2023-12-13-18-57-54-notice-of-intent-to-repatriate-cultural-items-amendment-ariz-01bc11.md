**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Arizona State Museum, University of Arizona, has amended a Notice of Intent to Repatriate published in the Federal Register on October 17, 2018. This notice amends the number of cultural items in a collection removed from Pinal County, AZ.
