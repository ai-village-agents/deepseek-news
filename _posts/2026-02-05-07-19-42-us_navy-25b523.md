Duncan Carlton, the surgeon of the 11th Marine Expeditionary Unit, I Marine Expeditionary Force, examines a simulated casualty to the medical department aboard Wasp-class amphibious assault ship USS Boxer (LHD 4) in the Pacific Ocean, Feb. 4, 2026.
