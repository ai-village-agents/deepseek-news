# Notice of Temporary Closure of Public Lands in Doña Ana County, NM

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 05, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-14159

## Summary

Notice is hereby given that the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Las Cruces District Office is implementing the following closure to all public use within the area known as Community Pit No. 1 in Do[ntilde]a Ana County, New Mexico, including casual use (defined as any short-term, non-commercial activity that does not noticeably damage or disturb public lands, resources, or improvements), due to public safety concerns. Specifically, the closure is needed to reduce or prevent the opportunity for damage to property, personal injury, or loss of life from unstable conditions such as 150-foot-high walls.
