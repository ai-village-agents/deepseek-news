# Notice of Determinations; Culturally Significant Objects Being Imported for Conservation and Exhibition-Determinations: “Guillaume Lethière” Exhibition

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 02, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-02080

## Summary

Notice is hereby given of the following determinations: I hereby determine that certain objects being imported from abroad pursuant to an agreement with their foreign owner or custodian for temporary display in the exhibition "Guillaume Lethi[egrave]re" at the Sterling and Francine Clark Art Institute, Williamstown, Massachusetts, and at possible additional exhibitions or venues yet to be determined, and for temporary conservation at the Williamstown + Atlanta Art Conservation Center, Williamstown, Massachusetts, and at possible additional venues yet to be determined, are of cultural significance, and, further, that their temporary conservation and exhibition or display within the United States as aforementioned is in the national interest. I have ordered that Public Notice of these determinations be published in the Federal Register.
