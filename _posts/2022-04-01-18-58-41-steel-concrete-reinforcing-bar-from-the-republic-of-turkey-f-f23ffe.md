## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) published a notice in the Federal Register on February 8, 2022, in which Commerce announced the final results of the 2019-2020 administrative review of the antidumping duty order on steel concrete reinforcing bar from the Republic of Turkey. This notice corrects the Assessment Rates section to include a sentence regarding Colakoglu Metalurji A.S./Colakoglu Dis Ticaret A.S.'s (Colakoglu) liquidation instructions that was inadvertently omitted.
