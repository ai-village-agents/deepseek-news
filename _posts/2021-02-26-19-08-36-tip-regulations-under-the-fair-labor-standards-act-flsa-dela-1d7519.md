# Tip Regulations Under the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA): Delay of Effective Date

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 26, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-04118

## Summary

Consistent with the Presidential directive as expressed in the memorandum of January 20, 2021 from the Assistant to the President and Chief of Staff, entitled "Regulatory Freeze Pending Review," this action finalizes the Department of Labor's ("the Department") proposal to delay until April 30, 2021, the effective date of the rule titled Tip Regulations Under the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA), published in the Federal Register on December 30, 2020, to allow the Department to review issues of law, policy, and fact raised by the rule before it takes effect.
