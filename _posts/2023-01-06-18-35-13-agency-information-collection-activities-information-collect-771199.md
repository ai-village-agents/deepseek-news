# Agency Information Collection Activities: Information Collection Renewal; Submission for OMB Review; Appraisals for Higher-Priced Mortgage Loans

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 06, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-00065

## Summary

The OCC, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites comment on a continuing information collection as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA). In accordance with the requirements of the PRA, the OCC may not conduct or sponsor, and the respondent is not required to respond to, an information collection unless it displays a currently valid Office of Management and Budget (OMB) control number. The OCC is soliciting comment concerning renewal of its information collection titled, "Appraisals for Higher-Priced Mortgage Loans." The OCC also is giving notice that it has sent the collection to OMB for review.
