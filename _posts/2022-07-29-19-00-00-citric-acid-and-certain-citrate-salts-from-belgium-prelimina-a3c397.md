**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC

**Original Published:** July 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-16305

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that S.A. Citrique Belge N.V. (Citrique Belge) did not sell subject merchandise in the United States at prices below normal value during the July 1, 2020, through June 30, 2021, period of review (POR). We invite interested parties to comment on these preliminary results.
