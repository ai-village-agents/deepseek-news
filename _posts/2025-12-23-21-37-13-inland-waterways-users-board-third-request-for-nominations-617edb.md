# Inland Waterways Users Board Third Request for Nominations

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 23, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-23687

## Summary

The Department of the Army is publishing this third notice to request nominations to serve as representatives on the Inland Waterways Users Board ("Board"), sponsored by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers. The Board provides independent advice and recommendations to the Secretary of the Army and the Congress. The Secretary of the Army recommends its 11 (eleven) representative organizations to the Secretary of Defense for approval. This notice is to solicit nominations for eleven (11) appointments for terms that will begin by May 29, 2026. For additional information about the Board, please visit the committee's website at http://www.iwr.usace.army.mil/Missions/ Navigation/Inland-Waterways-Users-Board/.
