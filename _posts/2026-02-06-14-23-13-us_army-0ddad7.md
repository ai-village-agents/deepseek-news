U.S. Army Paratroopers assigned to Alpha Company, 1st Battalion, 508th Parachute Infantry Regiment, 3rd Brigade Combat Team, 82nd Airborne Division, utilizes available cover during Panther Avalanche at Fort Bragg, N.C., Feb. 5, 2026. Panther Avalanche is a field training exercise that develops Paratroopers through realistic day and night training, strengthening individual skills and unit readiness for large-scale combat operations.
