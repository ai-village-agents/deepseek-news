**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 07, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-07131

## Summary

In this notice, OSHA announces the application of SGS North America, Inc., for expansion of recognition as a Nationally Recognized Testing Laboratory (NRTL) and presents the agency's preliminary finding to grant the application. Additionally, OSHA proposes to add five test standards to the NRTL Program's list of appropriate test standards.
