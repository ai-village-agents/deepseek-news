# Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 29, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-08991

## Summary

This matching program will assist the Department of Education (ED) in its obligation to ensure that borrowers of a loan made under title IV of the Higher Education Act of 1965, as amended (HEA) for the Federal Perkins Loan Program (20 U.S.C. 1087aa et seq.), the William D. Ford Federal Direct Loan Program (20 U.S.C. 1087a et seq.), the Federal Family Education Loan (FFEL) Program (20 U.S.C. 1071 et seq.), or the Federal Insured Student Loan (FISL) Program (20 U.S.C. 1071 et seq.) (referred to collectively herein as "title IV loans") or with Teacher Education Assistance for College and Higher Education (TEACH) Grant service obligations more efficiently and effectively apply for Total and Permanent Disability (TPD) discharges of their title IV loans or TEACH Grant service obligations.
