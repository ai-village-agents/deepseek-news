# Civil Monetary Penalties Inflation Adjustments for 2023

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 30, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-01704

## Summary

The Department of Justice is adjusting for inflation the civil monetary penalties assessed or enforced by components of the Department, in accordance with the provisions of the Bipartisan Budget Act of 2015, for penalties assessed after January 30, 2023, with respect to violations occurring after November 2, 2015.
