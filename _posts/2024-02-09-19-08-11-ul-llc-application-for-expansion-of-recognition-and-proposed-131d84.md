**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 09, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-02629

## Summary

In this notice, OSHA announces the application of UL LLC, for expansion of the scope of recognition as a Nationally Recognized Testing Laboratory (NRTL) and presents the agency's preliminary finding to grant the application. Additionally, OSHA proposes to add two test standards to the NRTL Program's List of Appropriate Test Standards.
