# Amendment of VOR Federal Airways V-161, V-190, and V-307, and Revocation of VOR Federal Airway V-516 in the Vicinity of Oswego, KS

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 27, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-08892

## Summary

This action amends VHF Omnidirectional Range (VOR) Federal airways V-161, V-190, and V-307, and revokes V-516. The FAA is taking this action due to the planned decommissioning of the VOR portion of the Oswego, KS, VOR/Distance Measuring Equipment (VOR/DME) navigational aid (NAVAID). The Oswego VOR is being decommissioned in support of the FAA's VOR Minimum Operational Network (MON) program.
