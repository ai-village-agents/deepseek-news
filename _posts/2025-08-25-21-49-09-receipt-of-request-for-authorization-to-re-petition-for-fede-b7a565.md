# Receipt of Request for Authorization To Re-Petition for Federal Acknowledgment as an American Indian Tribe

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 25, 2025 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Department of the Interior (Department) announces that the group known as the Miami Nation of Indians of the State of Indiana, Inc., has submitted a request for authorization to re-petition for Federal acknowledgment as an American Indian Tribe to the Office of Federal Acknowledgment (OFA). The Department invites public comment and evidence concerning the request.
