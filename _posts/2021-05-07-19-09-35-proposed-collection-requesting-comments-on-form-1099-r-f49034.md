# Proposed Collection; Requesting Comments on Form 1099-R

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 07, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-09680

## Summary

The Internal Revenue Service, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on proposed and/or continuing information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The IRS is soliciting comments concerning Form 1099-R, Distributions From Pensions, Annuities, Retirement or Profit-sharing Plans, IRAs, Insurance Contracts, etc.
