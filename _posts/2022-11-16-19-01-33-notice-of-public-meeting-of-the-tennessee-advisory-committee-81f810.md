**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission), and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) that a meeting of the Tennessee Advisory Committee to the Commission will convene by Zoom on Tuesday, December 6, 2022, at 12 p.m. (CT).
