# Agency Information Collection Activities; Proposed Collection; Comment Request; Application for the Release of Blocked Funds; Electronic License Application Form

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 22, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-08656

## Summary

The Department of the Treasury, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other Federal agencies to comment on proposed or continuing information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. Currently, the Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) within the Department of the Treasury is soliciting comments concerning OFAC's Electronic License Application Form TD-F 90-22.54, which is referred to throughout this Notice as the "OFAC Application for the Release of Blocked Funds."
