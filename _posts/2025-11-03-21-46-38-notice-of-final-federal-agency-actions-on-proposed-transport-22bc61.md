# Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Transportation Project in Texas

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 03, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-19746

## Summary

The FHWA, on behalf of the Texas Department of Transportation (TxDOT), is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by TxDOT and other Federal agencies that are final agency actions. These actions relate to the I-345 Connects project in Dallas County, Texas. The project will reconstruct the I-345 facility for 2.848 miles from I-30 to Spur 366 and will consist of depressing six 12-foot mainlanes (three in each direction).
