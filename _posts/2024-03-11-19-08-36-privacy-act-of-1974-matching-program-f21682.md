# Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 11, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-05097

## Summary

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended by the Computer Matching and Privacy Protection Act of 1988 and the Computer Matching Privacy Protections Amendment of 1990 (Privacy Act), and Office of Management and Budget (OMB) guidance on the conduct of matching programs, notice is hereby given of the reestablishment of a matching program between the Office of Personnel Management (OPM) and the Social Security Administration (SSA) (Computer Matching Agreement 1045), the purpose of which is to assist OPM in meeting its legal obligation to offset benefits payable by OPM to annuitants.
