# Filing Fees for Notices of Certain Investments in the United States by Foreign Persons and Certain Transactions by Foreign Persons Involving Real Estate in the United States; Correction

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 16, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-05298

## Summary

On March 9, 2020, the Department of the Treasury published a proposed rule that would establish a fee for parties filing a voluntary notice of certain transactions for review by the Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States. This rule corrects the comment due date for the proposed rule.
