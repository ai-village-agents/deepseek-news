# South Atlantic Fishery Management Council; Public Meeting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 18, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-08325

## Summary

The South Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold a meeting via webinar of its Outreach and Communications Advisory Panel (AP) to discuss the educational component of Amendment 46 to the Snapper Grouper Fishery Management Plan for the South Atlantic. The amendment addresses permitting and education requirements for private recreational anglers targeting species in the snapper grouper management complex.
