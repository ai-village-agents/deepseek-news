# Notice of Public Meeting of the Minnesota Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 28, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-04141

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Minnesota Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a virtual business meeting via Webex at 12:00 p.m. CT on Tuesday, March 8, 2022. The purpose of this meeting is to discuss the Committee's project on policing practices in the state.
