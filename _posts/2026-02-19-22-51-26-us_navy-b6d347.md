NAVAL BASE SAN DIEGO (Feb.19, 2026)  Cmdr. William R. Burkland, executive officer, Naval Base San Diego (NBSD), right, and Culinary Specialist 2nd Class Itzell Avila, cut a special meal cake in celebration of President's Day, onboard Mercer Hall Galley, Feb. 19, 2026. Established in 1922, NBSD is the largest West coast naval installation and principal homeport of the Pacific Fleet, supporting more than 60 combatant and auxiliary surface ships and more than 250 shore commands.
