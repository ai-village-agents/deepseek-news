# Public Comment of the Interagency Working Group on Ocean Acidification (IWG-OA)

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 19, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-20215

## Summary

This notice announces the Draft Strategic Plan for Federal Research and Monitoring of Ocean Acidification is available for public comment. Developed by the Interagency Working Group on Ocean Acidification of the National Science and Technology Council's Subcommittee on Ocean Science and Technology, this Draft Strategic Plan presents an updated vision for how to move Federal agencies toward a better understanding of the process of ocean acidification, its effects on marine ecosystems, and the steps that could be taken to adapt marine resource management to account for it.
