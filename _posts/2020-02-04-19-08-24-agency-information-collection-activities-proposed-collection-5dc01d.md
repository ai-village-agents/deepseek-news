## Summary

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") is seeking public comment on its proposal to extend for an additional three years, the current Paperwork Reduction Act ("PRA") clearance for information collection requirements contained in its Funeral Industry Practice Rule ("Funeral Rule" or "Rule"). That clearance expires on June 30, 2020.
