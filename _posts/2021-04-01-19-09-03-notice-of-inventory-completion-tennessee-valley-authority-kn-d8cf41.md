# Notice of Inventory Completion: Tennessee Valley Authority, Knoxville, TN

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 01, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-06660

## Summary

The Tennessee Valley Authority (TVA) has completed an inventory of associated funerary objects in consultation with the appropriate Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations, and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the associated funerary objects and any present-day Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations. Representatives of any Indian Tribe or Native Hawaiian organization not identified in this notice that wish to request transfer of control of these associated funerary objects should submit a written request to the TVA. If no additional requestors come forward, transfer of control of the associated funerary objects to the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations stated in this notice may proceed.
