PENSACOLA, Fla. (Feb. 19, 2026) Damage Controlman 1st Class Anna Dickin, an instructor at Engineering Learning Site, Surface Warfare Schools Command, approaches the stage to receive the 2025 Mid-Grade Enlisted Military Instructor of the Year award onboard Naval Air Station Pensacola, Fla., Feb. 19. NETC is the largest shore command in the U.S. Navy and leads the Navy’s “Street to Fleet” process – recruiting America’s sons and daughters and transforming them through world-class training into highly skilled, battle-ready warfighters. (Official U.S. Navy photo by Austen McClain)
