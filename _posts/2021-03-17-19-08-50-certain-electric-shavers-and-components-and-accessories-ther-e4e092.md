# Certain Electric Shavers and Components and Accessories Thereof; Notice of a Commission Determination Not To Review an Initial Determination Granting a Motion To Amend the Complaint and Notice of Investigation

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 17, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-05469

## Summary

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 8) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ"), granting a motion by Complainant Skull Shaver, LLC ("Skull Shaver") to amend the complaint and notice of investigation to clarify the names of certain respondents and to correct the names of certain respondents.
