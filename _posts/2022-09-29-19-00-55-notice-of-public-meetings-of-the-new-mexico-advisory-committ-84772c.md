**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 29, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the New Mexico Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a series of virtual meetings via ZoomGov on the following dates and times for the purpose of discussing planning and debriefing panels on education adequacy for Native American students.
