# Western Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 02, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-04269

## Summary

The Western Pacific Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold meetings of its Non-commercial Fisheries Advisory Committee (NCFAC), American Samoa Archipelago Fishery Ecosystem Plan (FEP) Advisory Panel (AP), Mariana Archipelago FEP-Guam AP, Fishing Industry Advisory Committee (FIAC), Hawaii Archipelago FEP AP, and Mariana Archipelago FEP-Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands (CNMI) AP to discuss and make recommendations on fishery management issues in the Western Pacific Region.
