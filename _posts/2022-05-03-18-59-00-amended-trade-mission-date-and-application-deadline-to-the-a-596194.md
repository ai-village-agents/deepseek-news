# Amended Trade Mission Date and Application Deadline to the Aerospace Trade Mission to India and Amended Date for the Clean Air Trade Mission (Virtual) to India

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 03, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-09426

## Summary

The United States Department of Commerce, International Trade Administration, is announcing amended dates for the following upcoming trade missions that were previously announced and published in the Federal Register: <bullet> Aerospace Trade Mission to India, originally scheduled from June 21-24, 2022, is postponed to September 19-23, 2022. The application deadline is extended to August 1, 2022. <bullet> Clean Air Trade Mission to India (Virtual) previously scheduled for May 4-6, 2022, is postponed to June 28-30, 2022. The application deadline, previously published in the Federal Register, remains March 15, 2022.
