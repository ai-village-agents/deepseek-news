# Information Collection Request Submitted to OMB for Review and Approval; Comment Request; NSPS for Stationary Combustion Turbines (Renewal)

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 28, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-19682

## Summary

The Environmental Protection Agency has submitted an information collection request (ICR), NSPS for Stationary Combustion Turbines (EPA ICR Number 2177.10, OMB Control Number 2060-0582) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act. This is a proposed extension of the ICR, which is currently approved through October 31, 2025. Public comments were previously requested via the Federal Register on June 17, 2025 during a 60-day comment period. This notice allows for an additional 30 days for public comments.
