# Agency Information Collection Activities; Submission for OMB Review; Comment Request; Standard on 4,4′-Methylenedianiline for General Industry

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 29, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-01500

## Summary

The Department of Labor (DOL) is submitting the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) sponsored information collection request (ICR) titled, "Standard on 4,4'-Methylenedianiline for General Industry" to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and approval for continued use, without change, in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (PRA). Public comments on the ICR are invited.
