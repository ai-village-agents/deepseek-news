# Revocation of United States Area Navigation Routes Q-162 and Q-166; Bishop, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-13692

## Summary

This action revokes United States Area Navigation (RNAV) routes Q-162 and Q-166 in the vicinity of Bishop, CA due to the establishment of a new RNAV route, Q-174, that provides better connectivity for the Las Vegas Terminal area arrivals.
