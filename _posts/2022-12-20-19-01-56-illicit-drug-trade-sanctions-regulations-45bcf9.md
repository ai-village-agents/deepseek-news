## Summary

The Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is adding regulations to implement a December 15, 2021 illicit drug trade-related Executive order. OFAC intends to supplement these regulations with a more comprehensive set of regulations, which may include additional interpretive guidance and definitions, general licenses, and other regulatory provisions.
