U.S. Army Pfc. Connor Metzler, a Chemical, Biological, Radiological and Nuclear Specialist assigned to Headquarters and Headquarters Company, Division Special Troops Battalion, 2d Infantry Division Sustainment Brigade, 2d Infantry Division/ROK-U.S. Combined Division, learns to use a PAS-13, Thermal Weapons Sight at Rodriguez Live Fire Complex, South Korea, Feb. 4, 2026. Champion Brigade operated the 2ID Consolidated Gunnery and sent crews from each of its own battalions to qualify on M2 .50 Caliber and M240 Machine Guns so 2ID can remain ready to fight tonight.
