# Use of Forfeitures in Qualified Retirement Plans

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 27, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-03778

## Summary

This document sets forth proposed regulations that would provide rules relating to the use of forfeitures in qualified retirement plans, including a deadline for the use of forfeitures in defined contribution plans. These proposed regulations would affect participants in, beneficiaries of, administrators of, and sponsors of qualified retirement plans.
