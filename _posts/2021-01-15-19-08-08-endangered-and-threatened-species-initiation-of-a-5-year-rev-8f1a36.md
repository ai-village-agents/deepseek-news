# Endangered and Threatened Species; Initiation of a 5-Year Review for the Arctic, Okhotsk, Baltic, and Ladoga Subspecies of the Ringed Seal; Extension of Information Request Period

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 15, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-00844

## Summary

NMFS hereby extends the information request period on the notice of initiation of a 5-year review of the Arctic (Pusa hispida hispida), Okhotsk (Pusa hispida ochotensis), Baltic (Pusa hispida botnica), and Ladoga (Pusa hispida ladogensis) subspecies of the ringed seal under the Endangered Species Act (ESA).
