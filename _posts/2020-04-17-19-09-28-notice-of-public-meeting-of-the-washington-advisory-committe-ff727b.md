**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Washington Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting via teleconference on Friday, May 8, 2020 at 2:00 p.m. Pacific Time. The purpose of the meeting for the Committee to discuss their hearing on Voting Rights and Felony Convictions in Washington.
