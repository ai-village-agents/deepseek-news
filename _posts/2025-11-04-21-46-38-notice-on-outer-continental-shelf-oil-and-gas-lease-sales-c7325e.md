# Notice on Outer Continental Shelf Oil and Gas Lease Sales

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 04, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-19780

## Summary

Pursuant to the Energy Policy and Conservation Act of 1975 and the Bureau of Ocean Energy Management's (BOEM) regulatory restrictions on joint bidding, BOEM is publishing this list of restricted joint bidders. Each entity within one of the following groups is restricted from bidding with any entity in any of the other groups listed below at Outer Continental Shelf oil and gas lease sales held during the bidding period of November 1, 2025, through April 30, 2026.
