# Notice of Release of the Department of State's FY 2021 Service Contract Inventory

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 04, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-22072

## Summary

Acting in compliance with section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010, the Department of State is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the FY 2021 Service Contract Inventory. The FY 2021 Service Contract Inventory includes the FY 2021 Planned Analysis, and the FY 2020 Meaningful Analysis. The inventory was developed in accordance with guidance issued by the Office of Management and Budget (OMB), Office of Federal Procurement Policy (OFPP). The Department of State has posted its FY 2021 Service Contract Inventory at the following link: https:// csm.state.gov/index2.html.
