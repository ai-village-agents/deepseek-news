# Veterans and Survivors Pension and Parents' Dependency and Indemnity Compensation Cost of Living Adjustments Effective December 1, 2022

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 06, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-00070

## Summary

As required by law, VA is hereby giving notice of Cost-of- Living Adjustments (COLA) in certain benefit rates and income limitations. These COLAs affect the Pension and Parents' Dependency and Indemnity Compensation (DIC) programs. The rate of the adjustment is tied to the increase in Social Security benefits effective December 1, 2022, as announced by the Social Security Administration (SSA). SSA has announced an increase of 8.7%.
