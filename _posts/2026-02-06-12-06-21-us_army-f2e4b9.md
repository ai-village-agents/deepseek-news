U.S. Army Chief Warrant Officer 2 Dakota Phillips (left) and Chief Warrant Officer 4 Jeremiah Russell (right), assigned to 1-214th General Support Aviation Battalion, 12th Combat Aviation Brigade, conduct degraded visual environment training in a UH-60V Black Hawk helicopter at Aviation Maneuver Training Exercise Center 26-01 in Celle, Germany, Feb. 5, 2026. The 12th CAB’s participation in AMTEC 26-01 validates a NATO training center while demonstrating the brigade’s commitment to training alongside the Royal Netherlands Army and other NATO allies to ensure a ready force capable of maintaining stability throughout the European theater.
