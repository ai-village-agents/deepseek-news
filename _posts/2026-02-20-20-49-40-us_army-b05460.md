Mr. Mark Aiken receives his certificate of retirement from Col. Stacey Moore-Callaway, the commander of the 407th Army Field Support Brigade and Cmd. Sgt. Maj. George Warren at Ft. Hood, Texas Feb. 20th, 2026. Mr. Aiken is retiring from his position as the Deputy Commander of the 407th and served in the U.S. Army as an officer and civilian for a combined total of 46 years. The 407th AFSB is headquartered at Fort Hood and provides comprehensive logistics, maintenance, and sustainment support to Army units and joint forces across the globe. (U.S. Army photo by Maj. Brian Sutherland)
