(Feb. 4, 2026) Chief Steelworker Austin Bracelin, a Naval Construction Training Center (NCTC) steelworker course manager, and Construction Mechanic 1st Class Matthew Cheney, a NCTC construction mechanic instructor, show Rear Adm. Greg Huffman, Commander of Naval Education and Training Command (NETC), various training equipment during a tour of NCTC Gulfport facilities in Gulfport, Miss., Feb. 4. NETC is the largest shore command in the U.S. Navy and serves as the foundry to the Fleet by recruiting and delivering world-class training to highly skilled, operational and battle-ready warfighters. (Official U.S. Navy photo by Mass Communication Specialist 2nd Class Kyleigh Williams)
