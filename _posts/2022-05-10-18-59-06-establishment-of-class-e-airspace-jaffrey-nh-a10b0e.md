# Establishment of Class E Airspace; Jaffrey, NH

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 10, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-09720

## Summary

This action establishes Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface for Jaffrey/Silver Ranch Airport, Jaffrey, NH, to accommodate area navigation (RNAV) global positioning system (GPS) standard instrument approach procedures (SIAPs) serving this airport. Controlled airspace is necessary for the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations in the area.
