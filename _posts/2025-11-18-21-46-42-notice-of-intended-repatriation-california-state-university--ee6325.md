# Notice of Intended Repatriation: California State University, Dominguez Hills, Carson, CA

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 18, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-20107

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the California State University, Dominguez Hills (CSUDH) intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
