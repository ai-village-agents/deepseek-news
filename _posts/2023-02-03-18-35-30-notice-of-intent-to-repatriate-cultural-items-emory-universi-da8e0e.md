# Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: Emory University, Michael C. Carlos Museum, Atlanta, GA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 03, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-02274

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), Emory University, Michael C. Carlos Museum (Emory) intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The cultural items were removed from Bartow County, GA.
