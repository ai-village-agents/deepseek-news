Lieutenant General Jeth B. Rey, Deputy Chief of Staff, G-6, visited United States Army Network Enterprise Technology Command (NETCOM) headquarters for a comprehensive review of NETCOM’s global, theater-focused posture. NETCOM Commanding General Major General McPhail outlined NETCOM’s mission to serve as the Army’s global IT platform and to lead global operations for the Army’s portion of the Department of War Information Network (DOWIN-A).
