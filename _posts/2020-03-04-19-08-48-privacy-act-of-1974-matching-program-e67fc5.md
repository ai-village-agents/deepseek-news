# Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 04, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-04457

## Summary

Pursuant to the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended by the Computer Matching and Privacy Protection Act (CMPPA) of 1988, HUD is providing notice of its intent to execute a new computer matching agreement with the U.S. Department of Education (ED). The matching agreement covers the exchange of data obtained by ED pertaining to delinquent debt. The purpose of the match is to update the Credit Alert Verification Reporting System (CAIVRS), which is maintained by HUD.
