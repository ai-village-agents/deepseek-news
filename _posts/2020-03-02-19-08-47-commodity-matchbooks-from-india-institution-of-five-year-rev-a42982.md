# Commodity Matchbooks From India; Institution of Five-Year Reviews

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 02, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-04076

## Summary

The Commission hereby gives notice that it has instituted reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act"), as amended, to determine whether revocation of the countervailing and the antidumping duty orders on commodity matchbooks from India would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury. Pursuant to the Act, interested parties are requested to respond to this notice by submitting the information specified below to the Commission.
