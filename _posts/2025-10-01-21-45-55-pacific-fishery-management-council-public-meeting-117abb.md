# Pacific Fishery Management Council; Public Meeting

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:45 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 01, 2025 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Pacific Fishery Management Council (Pacific Council) will hold an online meeting of its Ad Hoc Highly Migratory Species (HMS) Fisheries Innovation Workgroup (FIW) to discuss items on the Council's November meeting agenda related to the development of new HMS gears and the HMS Roadmap. This meeting is open to the public.
