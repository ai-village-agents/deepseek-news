**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC

## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) published a notice in the Federal Register of April 8, 2022, in which Commerce announced the final results of the 2019 administrative review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on certain cold-rolled steel flat products (cold- rolled steel) from the Republic of Korea (Korea). This notice incorrectly listed "Dongbu USA" and "POSCO International Corp. (POSCO International Corporation)" in "Appendix II: List of Non- Selected Companies."
