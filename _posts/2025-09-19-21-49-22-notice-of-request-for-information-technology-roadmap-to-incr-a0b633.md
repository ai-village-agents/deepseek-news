**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 19, 2025 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Office of Science and Technology Policy (OSTP) requests input from all interested parties on the development of a comprehensive, technology roadmap to increase wildfire firefighting capabilities at the Federal, State, and local levels, pursuant to Executive Order 14308. Through this Request for Information (RFI), OSTP seeks input from the public, including fire professionals, academia, private sector organizations, industry groups, venture capital, philanthropic organizations, and State, local, tribal, and territorial governments, and any other interest parties. other interest parties, on priorities for such a roadmap.
