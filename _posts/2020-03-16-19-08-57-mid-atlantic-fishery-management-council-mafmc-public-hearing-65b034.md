# Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council (MAFMC); Public Hearings

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 16, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-05305

## Summary

The Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold five public hearings in March and April 2020 to solicit public input on an amendment to the Council's Mackerel, Squid, and Butterfish (MSB) Fishery Management Plan (FMP). The amendment focuses on the FMP's goals and objectives as well as permitting in the Illex squid fishery. The Council is soliciting written comments on the amendment through April 20, 2020.
