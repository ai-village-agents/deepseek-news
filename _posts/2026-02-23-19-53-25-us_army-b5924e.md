SPRINGFIELD, Ill. – The Illinois Army National Guard honored Lt. Col. Lionel Gonzalez during a retirement ceremony recognizing more than 23 years of faithful and honorable service to the state of Illinois and the nation.

Hosted by Col. Paul Metzdorff, commander of the 129th Regiment, the ceremony celebrated Gonzalez’s career of leadership, mentorship and dedication to Soldier care.



The ceremony opened with an invocation from Chaplain Carson, who thanked Gonzalez and his family for their sacrifice and service. Gonzalez was joined by his wife, Natalie, and their two sons, Isaac and Ethan. Soldiers of the 129th Regiment presented Mrs. Gonzalez with flowers in recognition of her enduring support throughout her husband’s career.
