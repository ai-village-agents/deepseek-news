# Agency Information Collection Activities: Proposed Collection; Comment Request

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 20, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-05824

## Summary

We are planning to request that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) renew its approval for us to engage in the following information collection consisting of National Archives Trust Fund (NATF) order forms for genealogical research in the National Archives. The NATF forms included in this information collection are: NATF 84, National Archives Order for Copies of Land Entry Files; NATF 85, National Archives Order for Copies of Pension or Bounty Land Warrant Applications; and NATF 86, National Archives Order for Copies of Military Service Records. We invite you to comment on the proposed information collection.
