# Solicitation of Nominations for Appointment to the Lead Exposure and Prevention Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:45 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 02, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-19277

## Summary

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), is soliciting nominations for membership on the Lead Exposure and Prevention Advisory Committee (LEPAC). The LEPAC is composed of not more than 15 members that are Federal and non-Federal experts in fields associated with lead screening, the prevention of lead exposure, and services for individuals and communities affected by lead exposure.
