# Amendment Class D and Class E Airspace; South Florida; Correction

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 13, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-14829

## Summary

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) is correcting a final rule that appeared in the Federal Register on March 4, 2022, amending airspace for several airports in the south Florida area. This action corrects the legal description of Miami Executive Airport (formerly Kendall-Tamiami Executive Airport), by including the erroneously omitted surface E airspace description.
