# Steel Concrete Reinforcing Bar From Algeria: Preliminary Affirmative Countervailing Duty Determination

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:34 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 13, 2026 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2026-00493

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that countervailable subsidies are being provided to producers and exporters of steel concrete reinforcing bar (rebar) from Algeria. The period of investigation (POI) is January 1, 2024, through December 31, 2024. Interested parties are invited to comment on this preliminary determination.
