# Assessment of Fees for Dairy Import Licenses for the 2026 Tariff-Rate Import Quota Year

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 15, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-15549

## Summary

This notice announces a fee of $350 to be charged for the 2026 tariff-rate quota (TRQ) year for each license issued to a person or firm by the Department of Agriculture authorizing the importation of certain dairy articles, which are subject to tariff-rate quotas set forth in the Harmonized Tariff Schedule (HTS) of the United States.
