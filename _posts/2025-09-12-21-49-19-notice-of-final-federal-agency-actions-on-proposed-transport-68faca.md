# Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Transportation Project in State Florida

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 12, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-17639

## Summary

The FHWA, on behalf of the State Department of Transportation (FDOT), is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by FDOT and other Federal agencies that are final agency actions. These actions relate to the proposed Canoe Creek Road Project Development and Environment (PD&E) Study from Deer Run Road to US 192 (Financial Management 449217-1). The proposed improvements consist of reconstructing the existing two-lane roadway to a four-lane divided urban roadway including multimodal improvements.
