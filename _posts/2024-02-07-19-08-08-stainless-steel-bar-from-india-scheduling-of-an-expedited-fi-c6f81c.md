**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 07, 2024 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of an expedited review pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act") to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on stainless steel bar from India would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
