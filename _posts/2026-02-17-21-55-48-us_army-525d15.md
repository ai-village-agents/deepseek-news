New York Air National Guard Master Sgt. Sara Luna’s daughter, Kristin Luna, a sophomore at Center Moriches High School, has been named a semifinalist for the 2026 Operation Homefront Military Child of the Year Award for the National Guard category. Luna nominated Kristin for her resiliency, leadership and extensive volunteer work with the 106th Rescue Wing, local nonprofits, school activities and veterans’ organizations.
