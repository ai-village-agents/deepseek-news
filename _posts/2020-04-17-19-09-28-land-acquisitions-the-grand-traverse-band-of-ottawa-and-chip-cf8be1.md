# Land Acquisitions; the Grand Traverse Band of Ottawa and Chippewa Indians, Michigan

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 17, 2020 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Assistant Secretary--Indian Affairs has made a final determination to acquire 160.55 acres, more or less, of land in Acme Township, Grand Traverse County, Michigan into trust for the Grand Traverse Band of Ottawa and Chippewa Indians ("Tribe") for non-gaming purposes.
