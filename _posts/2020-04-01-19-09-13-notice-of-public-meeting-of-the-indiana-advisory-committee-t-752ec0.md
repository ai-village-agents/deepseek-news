**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Indiana Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting on Thursday, April 16, 2020, from 3-4 p.m. EDT for the purpose of discussing civil rights and lead contamination in the state.
