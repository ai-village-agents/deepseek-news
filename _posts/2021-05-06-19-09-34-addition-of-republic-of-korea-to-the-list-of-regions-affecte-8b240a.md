# Addition of Republic of Korea to the List of Regions Affected With African Swine Fever

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 06, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-09568

## Summary

We are advising the public that we have added the Republic of Korea (South Korea) to the list of regions that the Animal and Plant Health Inspection Services considers to be affected with African swine fever (ASF). We have taken this action because of confirmation of ASF in the Republic of Korea.
