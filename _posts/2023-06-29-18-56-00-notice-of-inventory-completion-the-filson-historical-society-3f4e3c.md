# Notice of Inventory Completion: The Filson Historical Society, Louisville, KY

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 29, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-13818

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Filson Historical Society has completed an inventory of human remains and has determined that there is no cultural affiliation between the human remains and any Indian Tribe. The human remains were removed from Muhlenberg County, KY.
