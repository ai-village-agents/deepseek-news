# Membership of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration Performance Review Board

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 16, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-22731

## Summary

NOAA announces the appointment of members who will serve on the 2023 NOAA Performance Review Board (PRB). The NOAA PRB is responsible for reviewing performance appraisals and ratings of Senior Executive Service (SES), Senior Level (SL), and Scientific and Professional (ST) members and making written recommendations to the appointing authority on retention and compensation matters, including performance-based pay adjustments, awarding of bonuses, and reviewing recommendations for potential Presidential Rank Award nominees. The appointment of members to the NOAA PRB will be for a period of 2 years.
