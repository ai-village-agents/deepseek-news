# Electronic Verification System Migrated to USPS Ship

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 25, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-08814

## Summary

The Postal Service is amending Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) to require the use of USPS Ship<SUP>TM</SUP> (aka Package Platform) for the acceptance and payment of all commercial domestic and international parcel mailings and discontinue the use of the Electronic Verification System (eVS[supreg]).
