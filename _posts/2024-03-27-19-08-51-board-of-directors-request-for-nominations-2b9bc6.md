# Board of Directors; Request for Nominations

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 27, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-06517

## Summary

The Unified Carrier Registration (UCR) Plan Board of Directors is requesting nominations of qualified individuals for all five of the motor carrier industry positions for appointment by FMCSA to the UCR Plan Board of Directors. The five vacancies have terms which expire on May 31, 2027. The nominees must be representatives from the motor carrier industry. At least one of the five motor carrier industry directors must be from a national trade association representing the general motor carrier of property industry and one of them must be from a motor carrier that falls within the smallest fleet fee bracket.
