# Notice of Public Meetings of the Pennsylvania Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-25940

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Pennsylvania Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a Zoom meeting on Tuesday December 13, 2022 from 2 p.m.-3 p.m. eastern time. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss/ debrief of themes that have emerged from the Committee testimony on zoning practices and fair housing in the commonwealth.
