# Notice of OFAC Sanctions Actions

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 19, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-08323

## Summary

The U.S. Department of the Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) is publishing an update to the identifying information of a person currently included in OFAC's Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Persons List (SDN List). All property and interests in property subject to U.S. jurisdiction of these persons are blocked, and U.S. persons are generally prohibited from engaging in transactions with them.
