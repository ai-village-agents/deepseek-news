# New Mailing Standards for Domestic Mailing Services Products

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC

## Summary

On April 6, 2022, the Postal Service (USPS[supreg]) filed a notice of mailing services price adjustments with the Postal Regulatory Commission (PRC), effective July 10, 2022. This proposed rule contains revisions to Mailing Standards of the United States Postal Service, Domestic Mail Manual (DMM[supreg]) to coincide with the price adjustments.
