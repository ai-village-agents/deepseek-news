# National Environmental Policy Act Procedures and Categorical Exclusions

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 02, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-06751

## Summary

The National Telecommunications and Information Administration ("NTIA") publishes this Notice that it will follow the First Responder Network Authority's ("FirstNet Authority") National Environmental Policy Act ("NEPA") procedures on an interim basis with modifications to account for NTIA's internal organization and establish 30 new categorical exclusions ("CEs") in compliance with NEPA, Council on Environmental Quality ("CEQ") regulations, and other related authorities.
