# Notice of Commencement of a Compliance Proceeding Pursuant to Section 129 of the Uruguay Round Agreements Act

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 11, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-14705

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is commencing a proceeding to gather information, analyze record evidence, and consider the determinations which would be necessary to bring its measures into conformity with the recommendations and rulings of the Dispute Settlement Body (DSB) of the World Trade Organization (WTO) in United States--Antidumping and Countervailing Duties on Ripe Olives from Spain (WTO/DS577). This dispute concerns the final determination issued in the countervailing duty (CVD) investigation of ripe olives from Spain.
