# Notice of Public Meeting of the Florida Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 09, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-02711

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Florida Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a virtual business meeting via Webex at 4:00 p.m. ET on Monday, February 28, 2022. The purpose of the meeting is for the Committee to discuss potential panelists for their project on Voting Rights.
