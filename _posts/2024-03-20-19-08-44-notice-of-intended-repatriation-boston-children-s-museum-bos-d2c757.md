# Notice of Intended Repatriation: Boston Children's Museum, Boston, MA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 20, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-05853

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), Boston Children's Museum intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and objects of cultural patrimony and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice.
