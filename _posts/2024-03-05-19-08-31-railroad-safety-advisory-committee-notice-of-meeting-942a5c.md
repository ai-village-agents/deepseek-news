# Railroad Safety Advisory Committee; Notice of Meeting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 05, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-04582

## Summary

FRA announces the sixty-sixth meeting of the Railroad Safety Advisory Committee (RSAC or Committee), a Federal Advisory Committee that provides advice and recommendations to FRA on railroad safety matters through a consensus process. The meeting's focus will be potential safety improvements in response to the February 3, 2023, freight train derailment in East Palestine, Ohio.
