# Shipyard Employment Standards; Extension of the Office of Management and Budget's (OMB) Approval of Information Collection (Paperwork) Requirements

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 27, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-08777

## Summary

OSHA solicits public comments concerning the proposal to extend OMB approval of the information collection requirements contained in the Shipyard Employment Standards. The purpose of the collection of information (paperwork) provisions of the Standards is to reduce workers' risk of death or serious injury by ensuring that equipment has been tested and is in safe operating condition.
