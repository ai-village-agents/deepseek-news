## Summary

Software is vital to America's global competitiveness, innovation, and national security. The economy, the nation's infrastructure, education, and healthcare all depend on software. Through AI engineering, the U.S. has made significant strides in healthcare, energy, transportation, and space exploration. Software engineering and AI underpin the industries that have created huge economic benefits for the country. This workshop will explore the fundamental research needed to support progress across these critical domains.
