# Federal Acquisition Regulation; Federal Acquisition Circular 2022-06; Small Entity Compliance Guide

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 26, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-08579

## Summary

This document is issued under the joint authority of DoD, GSA, and NASA. This Small Entity Compliance Guide has been prepared in accordance with section 212 of the Small Business Regulatory Enforcement Fairness Act of 1996. It consists of a summary of the rules appearing in Federal Acquisition Circular (FAC) 2022-06, which amends the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR). Interested parties may obtain further information regarding these rules by referring to FAC 2022-06, which precedes this document.
