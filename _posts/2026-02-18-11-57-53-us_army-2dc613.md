Following a challenging deployment in response to the January 2026 winter storm, the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) Savannah District Temporary Emergency Power Planning and Response Team (SAS PRT) has concluded its successful mission in Mississippi. The team installed 29 generators to restore power to critical public facilities, including water systems, across 11 counties impacted by the storm.
