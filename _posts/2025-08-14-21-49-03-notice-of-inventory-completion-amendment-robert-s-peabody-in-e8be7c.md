## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Robert S. Peabody Institute of Archaeology (RSPI) has amended a notice of inventory completion published in the Federal Register on January 26, 2024. This notice amends the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations with cultural affiliation.
