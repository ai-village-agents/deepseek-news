# Filing of Plats of Survey: California

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC

**Original Published:** August 25, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-18307

## Summary

The plats of survey of lands described in this notice are scheduled to be officially filed in the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), California State Office, Sacramento, California, 30 calendar days from the date of this publication. The surveys, which were executed at the request of the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service, Bureau of Indian Affairs and Bureau of Land Management, are necessary for the management of these lands.
