**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 11, 2024 00:00 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the New York Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a public meeting via Zoom. The purpose of the meeting is to vote on the Committee's draft report.
