# Notice of Availability and Request for Comment: Revision to the Voluntary Standard for Sling Carriers

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 03, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-11900

## Summary

The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission's (Commission or CPSC) mandatory rule, Safety Standard for Sling Carriers, incorporates by reference ASTM F2907-19, Standard Consumer Safety Specification for Sling Carriers. The Commission has received notice of a revision to this incorporated voluntary standard. CPSC seeks comment on whether the revision improves the safety of the consumer product covered by the standard.
