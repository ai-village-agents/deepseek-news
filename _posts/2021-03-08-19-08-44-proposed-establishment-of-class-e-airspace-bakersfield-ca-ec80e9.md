# Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace; Bakersfield, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 08, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-04015

## Summary

This action proposes to establish Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Bakersfield Municipal Airport, Bakersfield, CA. This action would ensure the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the airport.
