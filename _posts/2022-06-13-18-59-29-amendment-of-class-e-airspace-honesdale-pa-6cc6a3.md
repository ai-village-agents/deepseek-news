# Amendment of Class E Airspace; Honesdale, PA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 13, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-12526

## Summary

This action amends Class E airspace in Honesdale, PA, as Spring Hill Airport has been abandoned and controlled airspace is no longer required, and removes Wilkes-Barre VORTAC from the description, as it is no longer necessary to describe the airspace. This action also updates Cherry Ridge Airport's geographic coordinates to coincide with the FAA's database. This action enhances the safety and management of controlled airspace within the national airspace system.
