# Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 27, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-08889

## Summary

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans, that are final. The actions relate to a proposed highway project on State Route 55 (Post Miles 10.4 to R17.9) in the Cities of Orange, Tustin, Santa Ana, and Anaheim in the County of Orange, State of California. Those actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
