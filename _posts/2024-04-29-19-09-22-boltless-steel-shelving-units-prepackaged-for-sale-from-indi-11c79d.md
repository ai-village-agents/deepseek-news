# Boltless Steel Shelving Units Prepackaged for Sale From India; Termination of Investigation

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 29, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-09121

## Summary

On April 19, 2024, the Department of Commerce published notice in the Federal Register of a negative final determination of sales at less than fair value (LTFV) in connection with the subject investigation concerning India. Accordingly, the antidumping duty investigation concerning boltless steel shelving units prepackaged for sale from India (Investigation No. 731-TA-1607 (Final)) is terminated.
