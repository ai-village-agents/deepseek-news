## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the S'edav Va'aki Museum (formerly Pueblo Grande Museum), City of Phoenix has amended a notice of inventory completion published in the Federal Register on February 8, 2019.
