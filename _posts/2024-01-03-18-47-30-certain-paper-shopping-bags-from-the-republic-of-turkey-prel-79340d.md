# Certain Paper Shopping Bags From the Republic of Turkey: Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:47 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 03, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-28944

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that certain paper shopping bags (paper bags) from the Republic of Turkey (Turkey) are being, or are likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV). The period of investigation (POI) is April 1, 2022, through March 31, 2023. Interested parties are invited to comment on this preliminary determination.
