Kenneth Froberg, commanding officer, Recruit Training Command, presents the Academic Excellence Award to Airman Apprentice Nico Bowden during pass-in-review onboard U.S. Navy Recruit Training Command (RTC) in Great Lakes, Feb. 5, 2026.
