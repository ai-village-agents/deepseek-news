# Fair Lending Report of the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, June 2023

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC

**Original Published:** July 06, 2023 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is issuing its eleventh Fair Lending Report of the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Fair Lending Report) to Congress. The CFPB is committed to ensuring fair, equitable, and nondiscriminatory access to credit for both individuals and communities. This report describes our fair lending activities in supervision and enforcement; guidance and rulemaking; interagency coordination; and outreach and education for calendar year 2022.
