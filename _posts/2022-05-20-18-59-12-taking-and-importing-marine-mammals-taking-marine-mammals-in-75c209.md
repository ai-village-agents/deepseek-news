# Taking and Importing Marine Mammals; Taking Marine Mammals Incidental to Geophysical Surveys Related to Oil and Gas Activities in the Gulf of Mexico

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 20, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-10936

## Summary

In accordance with the Marine Mammal Protection Act (MMPA), as amended, its implementing regulations, and NMFS' MMPA Regulations for Taking Marine Mammals Incidental to Geophysical Surveys Related to Oil and Gas Activities in the Gulf of Mexico, notification is hereby given that a Letter of Authorization (LOA) has been issued to Echo Offshore, LLC (Echo) and its designees for the take of marine mammals incidental to geophysical survey activity in the Gulf of Mexico.
