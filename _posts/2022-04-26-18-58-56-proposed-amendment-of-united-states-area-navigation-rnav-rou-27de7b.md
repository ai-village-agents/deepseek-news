# Proposed Amendment of United States Area Navigation (RNAV) Route T-270; Shishmaref, AK

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 26, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-08783

## Summary

This action proposes to amend United States Area Navigation (RNAV) T-route, T-270 in the vicinity of Shishmaref, AK due to the planned decommissioning of the Shishmaref, AK, (SHH) Non-Directional Beacons (NDB) and the Norton Bay, AK, (OAY) NDB. Both NDBs will be decommissioned as part of a large and comprehensive T-route modernization project for the state of Alaska.
