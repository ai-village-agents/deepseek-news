# Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 06, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-02448

## Summary

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (Bureau), gives notice of the establishment of a revised Privacy Act System of Records. This revised system will collect information to enable the Bureau to carry out its responsibilities with respect to enforcement of title X of the Dodd- Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act and other Federal consumer financial law, including: The investigation of potential violations of Federal consumer financial law; the pursuit of administrative or civil enforcement actions; and the referral of matters, as appropriate, to the Department of Justice or other Federal or State agencies. The information will also be used for administrative purposes to ensure quality control, performance, and improving management processes.
