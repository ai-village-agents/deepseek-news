# Background Investigations for Persons or Entities With a Financial Interest in or Having a Management Responsibility for a Management Contract

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 16, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-13021

## Summary

On December 2, 2021, the National Indian Gaming Commission published a proposed rule to amend the part 537 procedures for processing a request for approval of a management contract under the Indian Gaming Regulatory Act. Comments sent to the listed email address, information@nigc.gov, may not have been received. In order to ensure that all submitted comments are received by the Commission for review, the NIGC is reopening the comment period for seven days to allow anyone that submitted comments during the original comment period to resubmit. If comments were submitted in any of the other methods specified in the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking, the NIGC received those comments, and there is no need to resubmit.
