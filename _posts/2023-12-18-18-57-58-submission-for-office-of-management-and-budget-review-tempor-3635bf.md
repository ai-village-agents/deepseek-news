# Submission for Office of Management and Budget Review; Temporary Assistance for Needy Families Expenditure Report, Form ACF-196R

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 18, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-27756

## Summary

The Administration for Children and Families (ACF) Office of Family Assistance (OFA) is requesting a 3-year extension of the Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) Expenditure Report, Form ACF-196R (Office of Management and Budget (OMB) #0970-0446 expires February 29, 2024). ACF is proposing minor updates to the form to update references to lapsed timeframes, add demonstrative tables that aid in a recipient's comprehension of reporting requirements, incorporate minor edits to the instructions, and to adjust formatting to improve presentation.
