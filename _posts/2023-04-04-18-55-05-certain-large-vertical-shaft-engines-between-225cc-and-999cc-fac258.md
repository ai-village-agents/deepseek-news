# Certain Large Vertical Shaft Engines Between 225cc and 999cc, and Parts Thereof From the People's Republic of China: Final Results of the Antidumping Duty Administrative Review; 2020-2022

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 04, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-06974

## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) continues to find that Honda Power Products (China) Co., Ltd. (Honda), the sole company subject to the administrative review of the antidumping duty order on certain large vertical shaft engines between 225cc and 999cc, and parts thereof (large VSE) from the People's Republic of China (China) covering the period of review (POR) August 19, 2020, through February 28, 2022, is not eligible for a separate rate and, thus, is part of the China-wide entity.
