# Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 08, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-04711

## Summary

In accordance with the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended (Privacy Act), the U.S. Department of Education (Department) publishes this rescindment for six systems of records from its existing inventory of systems of records subject to the Privacy Act. The notices addressed in this rescindment of systems of records notice are rescinded because they have been superseded by more recent notices published by the Department.
