260217-N-MZ309-1040 SILVERDALE, Wash. (Feb. 17, 2026) The Honorable Brendan P. Rogers, assistant secretary of the Navy for Energy, Installations, and Environment, talks with Rear Adm. Chris Nash, commander of Submarine Group (SUBGRU) 9, during a visit to Naval Base Kitsap-Bangor, Feb. 17, 2026. The Assistant Secretary of the Navy for Energy, Installations and Environment directly supports the mission, the warfighter, and their families. SUBGRU-9 exercises operational and administrative control authority for assigned submarine commands and units in the Pacific Northwest providing oversight for shipboard training, personnel, supply and material readiness of SSBNs and their crews. SUBGRU-9 is also responsible for nuclear submarines undergoing conversion or overhaul at Puget Sound Naval Shipyard in Bremerton.
