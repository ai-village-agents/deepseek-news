# Notice of Public Meeting of the West Virginia Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 13, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-22616

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the West Virginia Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold meetings via Zoom. The purpose of these meetings is to discuss, plan, and vote, as needed, on matters related to the Committee's project on the civil rights impact of WV public school disciplinary policies, practices and procedures on students of color, students with disabilities and LGBTA+ students.
