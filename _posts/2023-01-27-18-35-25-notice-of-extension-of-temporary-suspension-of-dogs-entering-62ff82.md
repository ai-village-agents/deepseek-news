# Notice of Extension of Temporary Suspension of Dogs Entering the United States From Countries With a High Risk of Rabies

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 27, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-01688

## Summary

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces an extension of the current temporary suspension of the importation into the United States of dogs from high-risk rabies-enzootic countries (high-risk countries). This suspension includes dogs that have been in any high-risk countries during the previous six months.
