# Rural Digital Opportunity Fund, Connect America Fund

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 25, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-15585

## Summary

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission or FCC) announces that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has approved, for a period of three years, an information collection associated with the rules for the Connect America Fund contained in the Commission's Rural Digital Opportunity Fund Order, FCC 20-5. This document is consistent with the Rural Digital Opportunity Fund Order, which stated that the Commission would publish a document in the Federal Register announcing the effective date of the new information collection requirements.
