# Implementation of a Change to the Parole Process for Cubans

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 28, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-09013

## Summary

This notice announces that the Secretary of Homeland Security has authorized a change to the Parole Process for Cubans that the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) described in a Federal Register notice on January 9, 2023. The change provides that those who have been interdicted at sea after April 27, 2023 will be ineligible for the announced parole process.
