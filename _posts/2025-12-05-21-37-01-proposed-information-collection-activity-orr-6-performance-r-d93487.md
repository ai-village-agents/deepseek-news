# Proposed Information Collection Activity; ORR-6 Performance Report

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 05, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-22021

## Summary

The Office of Refugee Resettlement (ORR), Administration for Children and Families (ACF), U.S. Department of Health and Human Services, seeks Office of Management and Budget (OMB) approval for a 3- year extension to the existing data collection for the ORR-6 Performance Report forms (OMB #0970-0036, expiration December 31, 2025) with minor changes to the instructions. The minor proposed changes remove parts of the instructions that are no longer relevant due to expired program funding and clarify existing instructions.
