# USDOT Fiscal Year 2023 Safe Streets and Roads for All Funding

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 12, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-07716

## Summary

The purpose of this notice is to solicit applications for Safe Streets and Roads for All (SS4A) grants. Funds for the fiscal year (FY) 2023 SS4A grant program are to be awarded on a competitive basis to support planning, infrastructure, behavioral, and operational initiatives to prevent death and serious injury on roads and streets involving all roadway users, including pedestrians; bicyclists; public transportation, personal conveyance, and micromobility users; motorists; and commercial vehicle operators.
