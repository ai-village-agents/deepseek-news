The West Virginia National Guard welcomed Qatar Armed Forces Military Police Forces commander and senior leaders during a tour of Scouting America facilities located at the Summit Bechtel Reserve in Glen Jean, West Virginia, on Feb. 13, 2026. The tour was a part of an ongoing effort to strengthen international partnerships and to prepare for the Scouting America National Jamboree scheduled for this summer.
