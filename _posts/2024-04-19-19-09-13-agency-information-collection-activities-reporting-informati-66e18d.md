**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC

## Summary

The Appraisal Subcommittee (ASC) is issuing this Notice of Request (Notice) for public comment on the reporting information for the National Registry of Appraisal Management Companies (AMC Registry) to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) of proposed collection of information. In conjunction with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the ASC submitted to the OMB a request for review of approval of information collection listed below. The purpose of this Notice is to allow an additional 30 days for public comment from all interested individuals and organizations.
