Chef Robert Irvine, a celebrity culinary chef, speaks to First Team Troopers during a visit to M1 Abrams tank crews conducting range operations on Fort Hood, Texas, Feb. 18th, 2026. Irvine met with Soldiers conducting live-fire qualification for the M1A2 SEPv3 main battle tank, extending his support for service members and discussing Army food modernization efforts such as Bistro 42.
