# Notice of Public Meeting of the Wyoming Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 05, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-11833

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Wyoming Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a public meeting via Zoom at 1:00 p.m. MT on Monday, June 26, 2023. The purpose of this meeting is to review the testimony received at the March and May briefings on housing discrimination in the state, as well as plan their next briefing and select panelists.
