## Summary

The Export-Import Bank of the United States (EXIM) is publishing revised regulations under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA). The revisions incorporate amendments to the FOIA under the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016, developments in case law, and changes in Federal and EXIM policies. The revisions also clarify procedural requirements.
