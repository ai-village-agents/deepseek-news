# Special Local Regulation; Dutch Shoe Regatta; San Diego, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 07, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-12231

## Summary

The Coast Guard will enforce the Dutch Shoe Regatta special local regulation on the waters of San Diego Bay, California on July 22, 2022. These special local regulations are necessary to provide for the safety of the participants, crew, spectators, sponsor vessels, and general users of the waterway. During the enforcement period, persons and vessels are prohibited from anchoring, blocking, loitering, or impeding within this regulated area unless authorized by the Captain of the Port, or his designated representative.
