# Agricultural Advisory Committee; Meeting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 10, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-24591

## Summary

The Commodity Futures Trading Commission (CFTC) announces that on December 7, 2022 from 9:00 a.m. to 12:45 p.m. (Eastern Standard Time), the Agricultural Advisory Committee (AAC or Committee) will hold an in-person public meeting at the CFTC's Washington, DC headquarters with options for the public to attend virtually. At this meeting, the AAC will focus on topics related to the agricultural economy, including geopolitical and sustainability issues, as well as recent developments in the agricultural derivatives markets. The AAC will also address procedural matters, including topics of discussion on a forward-looking basis.
