# Safety Zone; Barge Based Fireworks, Hudson River, Wappingers Falls, NY

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 12, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-10208

## Summary

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within a 600-foot radius of the fireworks launch site which is located on the Hudson River, Wappingers Falls, NY, in the vicinity south of Crum Elbow. This safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from the potential hazards during a fireworks display on May 28, 2022. Entry of vessels or persons into, transiting through, mooring, or anchoring within this zone are prohibited unless authorized by the Captain of the Port New York.
