**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 15, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-24490

## Summary

NMFS proposes specifications for the 2023 Atlantic bluefish fishery, as recommended by the Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council. This action is necessary to establish allowable harvest levels for the stock that will prevent overfishing and promote rebuilding, using the best scientific information available. This rule is intended to inform the public of the proposed fishery specifications and provide an opportunity for comment on the proposed action.
