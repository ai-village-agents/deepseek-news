# Notice of Public Meeting of the Puerto Rico Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 12, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-12422

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission), and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), that a meeting of the Puerto Rico Advisory Committee to the Commission will convene by virtual web conference on Monday, June 26, 2023, at 3:30 p.m. Atlantic Time/Eastern Time. The purpose is to continue discussion on their project on the civil rights impacts of the Insular Cases in Puerto Rico.
