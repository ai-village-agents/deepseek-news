# Board of Scientific Counselors, National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (BSC, NIOSH)

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 05, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-06927

## Summary

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the CDC announces the following virtual meeting of the Board of Scientific Counselors, National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (BSC, NIOSH). This is a virtual meeting. It is open to the public, limited only by web conference lines (500 web conference lines are available). If you wish to attend, please register at the NIOSH website http:// www.cdc.gov/niosh/bsc/ or call (404-498-2581) no later than May 12, 2021. Time will be available for public comment.
