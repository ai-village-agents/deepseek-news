**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC

**Original Published:** June 07, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is granting Illinois final authorization for changes to its hazardous waste program under the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA). The Agency published a Proposed Rule on February 1, 2022 and provided for public comment. No comments were received on the proposed revisions. No further opportunity for comment will be provided.
