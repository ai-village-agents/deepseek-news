# Silicon Metal From the Lao People's Democratic Republic: Preliminary Affirmative Determination of Sales at Less Than Fair Value

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 30, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-18983

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) preliminarily determines that silicon metal from the Lao People's Democratic Republic (Laos) is being, or are likely to be, sold in the United States at less than fair value (LTFV). The period of investigation (POI) is April 1, 2024, through March 31, 2025. Interested parties are invited to comment on this preliminary determination.
