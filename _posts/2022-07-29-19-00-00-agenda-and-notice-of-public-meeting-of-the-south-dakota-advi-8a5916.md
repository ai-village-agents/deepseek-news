# Agenda and Notice of Public Meeting of the South Dakota Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-16284

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission), and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA), that the South Dakota State Advisory Committee to the Commission will convene a meeting on Monday, August 8, 2022, at 3:30 p.m. (CT). The purpose of the meeting is to debrief after the committee's July briefing panel and continue planning future panels. The topic of study is voting rights and voting access in South Dakota.
