# Proposed Establishment of Class E Airspace; Shafter, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 08, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-04010

## Summary

This action proposes to establish Class E airspace extending upward from 700 feet above the surface at Shafter-Minter Field Airport, Shafter, CA. This action would ensure the safety and management of instrument flight rules (IFR) operations at the airport.
