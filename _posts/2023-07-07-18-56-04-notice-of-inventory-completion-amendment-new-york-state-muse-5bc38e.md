# Notice of Inventory Completion Amendment: New York State Museum, Albany, NY

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 07, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-14389

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the New York State Museum (NYSM) has amended a Notice of Inventory Completion published in the Federal Register on December 9, 2022. This notice amends the number of human remains and associated funerary objects removed from the Dennis site, in Menands, Albany County, NY.
