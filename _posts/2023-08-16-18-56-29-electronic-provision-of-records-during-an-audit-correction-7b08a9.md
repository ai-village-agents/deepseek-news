# Electronic Provision of Records During an Audit; Correction

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 16, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-17568

## Summary

On August 9, 2023, ONRR published a final rule amending its regulations to allow ONRR and other authorized Department of the Interior ("Department") representatives the option to require that an auditee use electronic means to provide records requested during an audit of an auditee's royalty reporting and payment. The final rule used a subpart that was designated reserved. This document corrects the final regulations by adding the subpart.
