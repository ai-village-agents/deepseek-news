# American Workforce Policy Advisory Board; Meeting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 19, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-03211

## Summary

The Office of the Under Secretary for Economic Affairs announces the fifth meeting of the American Workforce Policy Advisory Board (Advisory Board). Discussions of the Advisory Board will include its progress toward achieving the goals set at its inaugural meeting on March 6, 2019, as well as other Advisory Board matters. The meeting will take place in Mount Vernon, OH on March 19, 2020.
