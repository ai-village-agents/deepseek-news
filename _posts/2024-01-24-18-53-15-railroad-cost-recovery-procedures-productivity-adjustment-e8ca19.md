# Railroad Cost Recovery Procedures-Productivity Adjustment

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:53 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 24, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-01303

## Summary

In a decision served on January 19, 2024, the Board proposed to adopt 1.011 (1.1% per year) as the measure of average (geometric mean) change in railroad productivity for the 2018-2022 (five-year) period. The Board's January 19, 2024 decision stated that comments may be filed addressing any perceived data and computational errors in the Board's calculation. The decision also stated that, unless a further order is issued postponing the effective date, the decision will take effect on March 1, 2024.
