## Summary

The Corporation for National and Community Service (operating as AmeriCorps) is updating its regulations for processing requests for records under the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) to reflect changes made in the FOIA Improvement Act of 2016 and to make the regulations more user friendly through plain language.
