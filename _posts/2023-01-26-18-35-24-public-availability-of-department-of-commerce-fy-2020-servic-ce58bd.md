## Summary

In accordance with section 743 of Division C of the Consolidated Appropriations Act of 2010, the Department of Commerce (DOC) is publishing this notice to advise the public of the availability of the Fiscal Year (FY) 2020 Service Contract Inventory data, a report that analyzes DOC's FY 2019 Service Contract Inventory and a plan for the analysis of FY 2020 Service Contract Inventory.
