**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:02 UTC

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) published a notice in the Federal Register on December 15, 2022, in which Commerce announced the final results of the administrative review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on finished carbon steel flanges (flanges) from India covering the period January 1, 2020, through December 31, 2020. This notice corrects the names of three companies not selected for individual examination listed in Appendix II.
