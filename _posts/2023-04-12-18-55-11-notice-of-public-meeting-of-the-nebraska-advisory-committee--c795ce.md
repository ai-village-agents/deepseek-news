# Notice of Public Meeting of the Nebraska Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 12, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-07672

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the Nebraska Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a series of buriness meetings via web conference. The purpose of the meetings will be to discuss panel planning as part of their project on the Effects of Covid-19 on Education in the state.
