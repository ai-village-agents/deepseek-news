## Summary

The Kootenai National Forest is withdrawing its draft Supplemental Environmental Impact Statement (DSEIS) for the 2019 Montanore Mine Project. The Montanore Mine Project Notice of Intent was published in the Federal Register on December 28, 2017 (82 FR 61533) and the NOA was published in the Federal Register on June 21, 2019 (84 FR 29201).
