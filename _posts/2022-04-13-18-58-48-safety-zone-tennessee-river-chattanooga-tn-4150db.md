# Safety Zone; Tennessee River, Chattanooga, TN

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 13, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07819

## Summary

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters of the Tennessee River from mile marker (MM) 464.0 to 464.5. The temporary safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from potential hazards created by Chattanooga Presents--TN Aquarium 30th Anniversary Fireworks. Entry of vessels or persons into this zone is prohibited unless specifically authorized by the Captain of the Port Sector Ohio Valley or a designated representative.
