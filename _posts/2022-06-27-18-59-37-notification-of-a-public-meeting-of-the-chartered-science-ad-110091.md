# Notification of a Public Meeting of the Chartered Science Advisory Board

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 27, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-13629

## Summary

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office announces a public meeting of the chartered Science Advisory Board. The chartered SAB will meet to conduct quality reviews of two draft SAB reports: Review of EPA's Analyses to Support EPA's National Primary Drinking Water Rulemaking for PFAS, and Review of the EPA's Draft Fifth Contaminant Candidate List (CCL 5). The chartered SAB will also discuss any recommendations received from the SAB Work Group for Review of Science Supporting EPA Decisions with regard to SAB review of planned EPA actions.
