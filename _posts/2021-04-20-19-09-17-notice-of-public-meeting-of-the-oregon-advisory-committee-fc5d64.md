# Notice of Public Meeting of the Oregon Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act (FACA) that the Oregon Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting via web conference on Thursday, June 3, 2021, from 3:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. Pacific Time. The purpose of the meeting is for final review of report and vote.
