# Qualification of Drivers; Exemption Applications; Hearing

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 01, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-06860

## Summary

FMCSA announces receipt of applications from 14 individuals for an exemption from the hearing requirement in the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) to operate a commercial motor vehicle (CMV) in interstate commerce. If granted, the exemptions would enable these hard of hearing and deaf individuals to operate CMVs in interstate commerce.
