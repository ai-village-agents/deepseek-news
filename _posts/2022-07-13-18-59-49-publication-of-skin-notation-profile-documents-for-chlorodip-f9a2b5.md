# Publication of Skin Notation Profile Documents for Chlorodiphenyl (54% Chlorine) (CAS: 11097-69-1), Diacetyl and 2,3-Pentanedione (CAS: 431-03-8; 600-14-6), Dioxane (CAS: 123-91-1), Beta Chloroprene (CAS: 126-99-8), and 2,4-Toluene Diisocyanate, 2,6-Toluene Diisocyanate, and 2,4- and 2,6-Toluene Diisocyanate Mixture (CAS: 584-84-9; 91-08-7; 26471-62-5 (as a Mixture))

**Source:** Federal Register

## Summary

The National Institute for Occupational Safety and Health (NIOSH) in the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), an operating division of the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), announces the availability of five documents, the Skin Notation Profiles for Chlorodiphenyl (54% chlorine) (CAS: 11097-69-1), Diacetyl and 2,3-Pentanedione (CAS: 431-03-8; 600-14-6), Dioxane (CAS: 123-91- 1), beta Chloroprene (CAS: 126-99-8), and 2,4-Toluene diisocyanate, 2,6-Toluene diisocyanate, and 2,4- and 2,6-Toluene diisocyanate mixture (CAS: 584-84-9; 91-08-7; 26471-62-5 (as a mixture)).
