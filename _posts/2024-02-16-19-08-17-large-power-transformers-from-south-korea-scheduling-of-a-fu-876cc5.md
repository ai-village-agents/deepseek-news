**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC

**Original Published:** February 16, 2024 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of a full review pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act") to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty order on large power transformers from South Korea would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
