# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 09, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-07455

## Summary

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) has temporarily revised the Financial Statements for Holding Companies (FR Y-9; OMB No. 7100-0128) pursuant to the authority delegated to the Board by the Office of Management and Budget (OMB), (OMB Regulations on Controlling Paperwork Burdens on the Public). The revisions are applicable only to reports reflecting the March 31, 2020, as of date. Additionally, the Board invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the FR Y-9.
