# Notice of Department of State Sanctions Actions

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:34 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 27, 2026 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2026-01590

## Summary

The U.S. Department of State, in consultation with the U.S. Department of the Treasury and with other departments, as appropriate, has removed from the Department of the Treasury's List of Specially Designated Nationals and Blocked Persons (SDN List) administered by the Office of Foreign Asset Control (OFAC) the names of persons whose property and interests in property had been blocked pursuant to West Bank sanctions authorities.
