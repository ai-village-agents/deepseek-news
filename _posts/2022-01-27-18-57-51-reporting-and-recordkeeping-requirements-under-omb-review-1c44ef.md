## Summary

The Small Business Administration will submit the information collection described below to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for review and clearance in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, on or after the date of publication of this notice. SBA published the required 60-day public comment notice in the Federal Register on December 2, 2021, and is publishing this notice to allow all interested members of the public an additional 30 days to provide comments on the collection of information.
