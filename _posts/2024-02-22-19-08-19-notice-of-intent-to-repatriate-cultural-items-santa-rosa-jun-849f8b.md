# Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: Santa Rosa Junior College Multicultural Museum, Santa Rosa, CA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 22, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-03580

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the Santa Rosa Junior College Multicultural Museum (SRJCMM) intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The cultural items were removed from Los Angeles County and Santa Barbara County, CA.
