# Air Plan Approval; ID: Idaho Portion of the Logan UT-ID 2006 24-Hour PM2.5 Nonattainment Area; Moderate Plan Elements

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 20, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-02744

## Summary

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is taking final action to approve revisions to the Idaho State Implementation Plan (SIP) submitted on July 31, 2018. The submission includes Reasonable Further Progress and Quantitative Milestone attainment plan elements, along with updated Motor Vehicle Emissions Budgets, for the Idaho portion of the Logan, Utah-Idaho fine particulate matter (PM<INF>2.5</INF>) nonattainment area (Logan UT-ID area). The EPA's prior conditional approval for these elements will be removed and these elements are now fully approved.
