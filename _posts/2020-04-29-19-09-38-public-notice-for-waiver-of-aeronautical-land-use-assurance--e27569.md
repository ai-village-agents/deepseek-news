# Public Notice for Waiver of Aeronautical Land Use Assurance Friday Harbor Airport, Friday Harbor, WA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 29, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-09025

## Summary

Notice is being given that the FAA is considering a request from the Port of Friday Harbor Executive Director to change certain portions of the airport from aeronautical use to non-aeronautical use at the Friday Harbor Airport, Friday Harbor, WA. The request consists of 24 parcels, or portions thereof that are depicted on the Airport's current Exhibit A--Airport Property Map.
