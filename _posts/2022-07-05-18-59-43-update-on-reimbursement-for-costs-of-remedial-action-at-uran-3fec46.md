# Update on Reimbursement for Costs of Remedial Action at Uranium and Thorium Processing Sites

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 05, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-14208

## Summary

This Notice announces the Department of Energy's (DOE) acceptance of claims in FY 2022 from eligible uranium and thorium processing site licensees for reimbursement under the Energy Policy Act of 1992. The FY 2023 DOE Office of Environmental Management's Congressional Budget Request included $24.4 million for the Title X Uranium and Thorium Reimbursement Program.
