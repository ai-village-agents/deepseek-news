# Limitation on Claims Against a Proposed Public Transportation Project-Silver Line Project

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 18, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-25204

## Summary

This notice announces final environmental actions taken by the Federal Transit Administration (FTA) regarding the construction of the Silver Line Project, formerly known as the Cotton Belt Corridor Regional Rail Project. The purpose of this notice is to publicly announce FTA's environmental decisions on the subject project and to activate the limitation on any claims that may challenge these final environmental actions.
