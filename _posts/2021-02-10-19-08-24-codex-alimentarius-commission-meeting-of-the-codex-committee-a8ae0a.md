# Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Committee on Methods of Analysis and Sampling

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 10, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-02703

## Summary

The U.S Codex Office is sponsoring a public meeting on April 13, 2021. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 41st Session of the Codex Committee on Methods of Analysis and Sampling (CCMAS) of the Codex Alimentarius Commission in Budapest, Hungary, May 17-21, 2021. The U.S. Manager for Codex Alimentarius and the Acting Under Secretary for Trade and Foreign Agricultural Affairs (TFAA) recognize the importance of providing interested parties the opportunity to obtain background information on the 41st Session of the CCMAS and to address items on the agenda.
