260219-N-CM740-1005 PITTSBURGH (Feb. 19, 2026) - Capt. Patrick Copeland, commodore of Navy Recruiting Region East, right, and Command Master Chief Christopher B. Lyles, command master chief of Navy Recruiting Region East, middle, speak to recruiters assigned to Navy Talent Acquisition Group Pittsburgh, Navy Recruiting Station North Hills, Feb. 19, 2026.
