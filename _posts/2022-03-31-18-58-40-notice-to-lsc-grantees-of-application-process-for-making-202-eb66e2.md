# Notice to LSC Grantees of Application Process for Making 2022 Mid-Year and 2023 Basic Field Fund Subgrants

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 31, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-06758

## Summary

The Legal Services Corporation (LSC) is the national organization charged with administering Federal funds provided for civil legal services to low-income people. LSC hereby announces the submission dates for applications to make 2022 mid-year and 2023 Basic Field Grant fund subgrants. LSC is also providing information about where applicants may locate subgrant application questions and directions for providing the information required to apply for a subgrant.
