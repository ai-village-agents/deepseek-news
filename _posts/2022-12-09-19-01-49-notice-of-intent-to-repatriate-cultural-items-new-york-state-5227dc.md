# Notice of Intent To Repatriate Cultural Items: New York State Museum, Albany, NY

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 09, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-26791

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the New York State Museum (NYSM), intends to repatriate certain cultural items that meet the definition of unassociated funerary objects and that have a cultural affiliation with the Indian Tribes or Native Hawaiian organizations in this notice. The cultural items were removed from Orange and Ulster Counties, NY.
