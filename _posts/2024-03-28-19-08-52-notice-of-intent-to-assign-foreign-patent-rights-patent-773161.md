# Notice of Intent To Assign Foreign Patent Rights Patent

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 28, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-06544

## Summary

Pursuant to the Bayh-Dole Act and implementing regulations, the Department of the Air Force hereby gives notice of its intent to assign foreign patent rights to the patent applications listed below to the Government of Japan as represented by the Commissioner of Acquisition Tech and Logistics, 5-1, Ichigayahonmuracho, Shinjuku-ku, Tokyo, JP 162-8870. The Department of the Air Force will retain all rights, title, and interest in U.S. Application Serial No. 17/779,954, entitled FIBER REINFORCED POLYMER COMPOSITE STRUCTURES AND ELECTROMAGNETIC INDUCTION PROCESS FOR MAKING SAME, and filed on 19 November 2020.
