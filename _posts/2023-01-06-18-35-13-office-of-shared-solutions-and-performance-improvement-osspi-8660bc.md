# Office of Shared Solutions and Performance Improvement (OSSPI); Chief Data Officers Council (CDO); Notification of Upcoming Public Meeting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 06, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-00054

## Summary

The Federal Chief Data Officers Council (CDO Council) is having a public meeting during which the CDO Council will provide updates about its activities and the implementation of the Chief Data Officer role in the Federal Government. The meeting will include panel discussions on how CDOs are impacting their agency missions and collaborating across the Government to address data challenges. There will also be presentations about the role of data in key administration priorities such as Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, and Accessibility (DEIA). The meeting will include a chance to submit written questions.
