**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC

**Original Published:** January 04, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-28505

## Summary

The Department of Commerce (Commerce) published Federal Register notices of the preliminary and final results of the administrative review of the countervailing duty (CVD) order on common alloy aluminum sheet (aluminum sheet) from the People's Republic of China (China) covering the period April 23, 2018, through December 31, 2019, on June 25, 2021, and December 23, 2021, respectively. These notices contained incorrect spellings of company names subject to this administrative review.
