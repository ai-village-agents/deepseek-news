# Public Hearing

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:02 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 02, 2026 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-24201

## Summary

The Susquehanna River Basin Commission will hold a public hearing on January 29, 2026. The Commission will hold this hearing in person and telephonically. At this public hearing, the Commission will hear testimony on the projects and actions listed in the Supplementary Information section of this notice. Such projects and actions are intended to be scheduled for Commission action at its next business meeting, scheduled for March 12, 2026, which will be noticed separately. The public should note that this public hearing will be the only opportunity to offer oral comments to the Commission for the listed projects and actions. The deadline for the submission of written comments is February 9, 2026.
