# Notice of Public Meeting of the South Carolina Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 10, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-05006

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act that the South Carolina Advisory Committee (Committee) will hold a meeting via- teleconference on Thursday, April 1, 2021, at 12:00 p.m. (EST) the purpose of the meeting is to review the SAC's Findings and Recommendations on subminimum wages for people with disabilities.
