# Pipeline Safety: Request for Special Permit; Portland Natural Gas Transmission System

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:02 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-28337

## Summary

PHMSA is publishing this notice to solicit public comments on a request for special permit renewal received from the Portland Natural Gas Transmission System (PNGTS). The special permit request is seeking relief from compliance with certain requirements in the Federal pipeline safety regulations and proposes to add two (2) new pipeline segments to the existing special permit. At the conclusion of the 30- day comment period, PHMSA will review the comments received from this notice as part of its evaluation to grant or deny the special permit request.
