**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC

## Summary

In accordance with the Native American Graves Protection and Repatriation Act (NAGPRA), the California Department of Parks and Recreation has amended a Notice of Inventory Completion in the Federal Register on September 28, 2012. This notice amends the number of associated funerary objects in a collection removed from Shannon County, SD.
