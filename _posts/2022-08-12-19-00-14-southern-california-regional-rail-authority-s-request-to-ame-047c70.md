# Southern California Regional Rail Authority's Request To Amend Its Positive Train Control Safety Plan and Positive Train Control System

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 12, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-17317

## Summary

This document provides the public with notice that, on July 29, 2022, the Southern California Regional Rail Authority (Metrolink) submitted a request for amendment (RFA) to its FRA-approved Positive Train Control Safety Plan (PTCSP). As this RFA may involve a request for FRA's approval of proposed material modifications to an FRA- certified positive train control (PTC) system, FRA is publishing this notice and inviting public comment on the railroad's RFA to its PTCSP.
