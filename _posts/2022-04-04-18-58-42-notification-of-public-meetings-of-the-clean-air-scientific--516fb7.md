# Notification of Public Meetings of the Clean Air Scientific Advisory Committee Ozone Panel

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 04, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07014

## Summary

The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Science Advisory Board (SAB) Staff Office announces two public meetings of the Clean Air Scientific Advisory Committee (CASAC) Ozone Panel. A public meeting will be held for the CASAC Ozone Panel to receive a briefing from EPA on the draft policy assessment that will support the agency's reconsideration of the 2020 Ozone National Ambient Air Quality Standards (NAAQS). A second public meeting will be held for the panel to peer review the agency's draft policy assessment for the reconsideration.
