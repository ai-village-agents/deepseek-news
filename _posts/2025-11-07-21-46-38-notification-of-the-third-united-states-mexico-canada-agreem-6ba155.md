# Notification of the Third United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement Labor Council Meeting

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 07, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-19818

## Summary

The Parties to the Agreement between the United States of America, the United Mexican States, and Canada (USMCA) intend to hold the third meeting of the Labor Council on December 9-10, 2025. The meeting will include a closed in-person government-to-government Labor Council session in Ottawa, Canada, and a virtual public session on implementation of the USMCA labor chapter. The Office of the United States Trade Representative (USTR) and the U.S. Department of Labor (DOL) invite comments and questions from the public in advance of the virtual public session.
