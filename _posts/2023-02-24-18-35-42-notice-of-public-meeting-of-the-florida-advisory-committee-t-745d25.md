# Notice of Public Meeting of the Florida Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:35 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 24, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-03811

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Florida Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a virtual business meeting via Zoom at 1:30 p.m. ET on Wednesday, March 22, 2023. The purpose of the meeting is to continue discussing the draft report on voting rights in the state.
