# Notice of Meeting

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 17, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-17973

## Summary

This notice sets forth the agenda, time, and instructions to access or participate in the October 21, 2025, meeting of NACIQI, and provides information to members of the public regarding the meeting, including requesting to make written or oral comments. Committee members will meet in-person. Agency representatives have the option to meet in-person or virtually, and public attendees will participate virtually. The notice of this meeting is required under the Federal Advisory Committee Act and Section 114(d)(1)(B) of the Higher Education Act (HEA) of 1965, as amended.
