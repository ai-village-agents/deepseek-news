# Second Call for Nominations for the National Wild Horse and Burro Advisory Board

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 14, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-02979

## Summary

The purpose of this notice is to solicit public nominations for one position on the Wild Horse and Burro Advisory Board (Board) that expired on January 11, 2024. The Board provides advice concerning the management, protection, and control of wild free-roaming horses and burros on public lands administered by the Department of the Interior, through the Bureau of Land Management (BLM), and the Department of Agriculture, through the U.S. Forest Service.
