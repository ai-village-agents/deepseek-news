# Notice of Open Public Meetings

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 07, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-12190

## Summary

The Commission is mandated by Congress to investigate, assess, and report to Congress annually on the "the national security implications of the economic relationship between the United States and the People's Republic of China." Pursuant to this mandate, the Commission will hold a series of public meetings to review and edit drafts of the 2023 Annual Report to Congress.
