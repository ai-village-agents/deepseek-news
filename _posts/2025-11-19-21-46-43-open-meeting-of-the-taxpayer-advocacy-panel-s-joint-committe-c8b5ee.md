## Summary

An open meeting of the Taxpayer Advocacy Panel's Joint Committee Project Committee will be conducted. The Taxpayer Advocacy Panel is soliciting public comments, ideas, and suggestions to improve customer service at the Internal Revenue Service. This meeting will be held as a virtual video conference via the Microsoft Teams platform. That is the case for this meeting.
