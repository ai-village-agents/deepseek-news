# Special Local Regulations; Conch Republic Navy Parade and Battle, Key West, FL

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 20, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-08423

## Summary

The Coast Guard will enforce the special local regulations for the Conch Republic Navy Parade and Battle in Key West, Florida. Our regulation for Recurring Marine Events in Seventh Coast Guard District identifies the regulated area for this event. During the enforcement period no person or vessel may enter into, transit through, anchor in, or remain within the regulated area without approval from the Captain of the Port Key West or a designated representative.
