Soldiers and Civilians from the 30th Armored Brigade Combat Team, North Carolina Army National Guard, load M1000 Heavy Equipment Transport semi-trailers onto rail cars in coordination with Army Field Support Battalion-Bragg. Wearing high-visibility vests and hard hats, the team follows strict safety measures to protect against hazards such as moving equipment, falling tools, and overhead operations. The M1000 carries armored heavy equipment loads weighing up to 70 tons.
