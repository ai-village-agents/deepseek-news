# Initiation of Five-Year (Sunset) Reviews

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 01, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-16430

## Summary

In accordance with the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Act), the U.S. Department of Commerce (Commerce) is automatically initiating the five-year reviews (Sunset Reviews) of the antidumping and countervailing duty (AD/CVD) order(s) and suspended investigation(s) listed below. The U.S. International Trade Commission (the ITC) is publishing concurrently with this notice its notice of Institution of Five-Year Reviews which covers the same order(s) and suspended investigation(s).
