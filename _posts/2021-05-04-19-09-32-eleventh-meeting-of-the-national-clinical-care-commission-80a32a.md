# Eleventh Meeting of the National Clinical Care Commission

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:09 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** May 04, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-09277

## Summary

The National Clinical Care Commission (the Commission) will conduct its eleventh meeting virtually on May 19 and June 1, 2021. The Commission is charged to evaluate and make recommendations to the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) Secretary and Congress regarding improvements to the coordination and leveraging of federal programs related to diabetes and its complications.
