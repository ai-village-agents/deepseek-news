# Special Local Regulations; Marine Events Within the Seventh Coast Guard District

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC

**Original Published:** October 26, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Coast Guard will enforce a special local regulation for the Race World Offshore (RWO), Offshore World. During the enforcement period, no person or vessel may enter, transit through, anchor in, or remain within the regulated area without permission from the Captain of the Port Key West or a designated representative.
