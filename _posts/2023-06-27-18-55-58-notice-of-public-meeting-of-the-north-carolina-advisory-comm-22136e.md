# Notice of Public Meeting of the North Carolina Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 27, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-13667

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the North Carolina Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a public meeting via Zoom at 12:00 p.m. ET on Thursday, June 29, 2023. The purpose of the meeting is to discuss revisions to the report on Legal Financial Obligations in North Carolina and consider a preliminary vote.
