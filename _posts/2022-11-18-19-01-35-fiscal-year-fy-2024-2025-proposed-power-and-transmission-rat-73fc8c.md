## Summary

Bonneville is initiating a rate proceeding under the Northwest Power Planning and Conservation Act (Northwest Power Act) to establish power, transmission, and ancillary and control area services rates for the period from October 1, 2023, through September 30, 2025. Bonneville has designated this proceeding Docket No. BP-24.
