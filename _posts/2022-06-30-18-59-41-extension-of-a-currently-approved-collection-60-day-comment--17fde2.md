**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC

**Original Published:** June 30, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-13997

## Summary

In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, the National Endowment for the Humanities (NEH) is seeking comment concerning the renewal of its generic clearance for the collection of qualitative feedback on agency service delivery. This generic clearance fast-tracks the process for NEH to seek feedback from the public, through surveys and similar feedback instruments, regarding NEH services and programs.
