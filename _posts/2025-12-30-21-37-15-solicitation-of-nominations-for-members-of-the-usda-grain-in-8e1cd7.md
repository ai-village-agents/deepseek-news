# Solicitation of Nominations for Members of the USDA Grain Inspection Advisory Committee

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 30, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-23985

## Summary

USDA's Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) is seeking nominations for individuals to serve on the USDA Grain Inspection Advisory Committee (Advisory Committee). The Advisory Committee meets no less than once annually to advise AMS on the programs and services it delivers under the U.S. Grain Standards Act (USGSA). Recommendations by the Advisory Committee help AMS better meet the needs of its customers who operate in a dynamic and changing marketplace.
