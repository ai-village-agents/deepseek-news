# Inflation Adjustment to HR-1 Immigration Fees

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 21, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-20622

## Summary

U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS), a component of the Department of Homeland Security (DHS), is announcing inflationary adjustments to immigration-related fees administered by USCIS under the One Big Beautiful Bill Act (HR-1) for Fiscal Year (FY) 2026. HR-1 mandates that USCIS adjust the HR-1 fees. This notice outlines the adjusted fees and their effective date.
