NAVAL BASE POINT LOMA, Calif. (Feb. 6, 2026) - Patrick Henry High School Navy Junior Reserve Officers Training Corps (NJROTC) cadets tour the firefighting trainer at Submarine Training Facility, San Diego (STFSD) during a visit to Naval Base Point Loma, Feb. 6, 2026. STFSD provides basic training through advanced individual and unit instruction designed for qualifications and certification necessary of the officers and enlisted in the submarine and surface communities. (U.S. Navy photo by Electronics Technician (Nuclear) 1st Class Andrew Donchey)
