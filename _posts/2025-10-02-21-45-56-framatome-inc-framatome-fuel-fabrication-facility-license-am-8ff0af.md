# Framatome, Inc.; Framatome Fuel Fabrication Facility; License Amendment Application

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:45 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 02, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-19301

## Summary

The U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) staff docketed an application for the amendment of Special Nuclear Materials License No. SNM-1227, submitted by Framatome, Inc. dated September 20, 2024. (This application was accepted on July 7, 2025). The amended license would authorize Tri-structural Isotropic (TRISO) fuel fabrication to enrichment less than 10.0 weight percent Uranium-235 (U-235) in the Specialty Fuels Building at the Framatome Fuel Fabrication Facility in Richland, WA.
