# Safety Zone; Missouri River Mile Markers 139.5-139.2, Jefferson City, MO

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 17, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-22634

## Summary

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters in the Missouri River at Mile Markers (MM) 139.5- 139.2. The safety zone is needed to protect personnel, vessels, and the marine environment from all potential hazards associated with electrical line work. Entry of vessels or persons into this zone is prohibited unless specifically authorized by the Captain of the Port Sector Upper Mississippi River (COTP) or a designated representative.
