# Notice of a Commission Determination Not to Review an Initial Determination Granting Return of Bond; Termination of Bond Return Proceeding; Certain Light-Emitting Diode Products, Fixtures, and Components Thereof

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 05, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-14270

## Summary

Notice is hereby given that the U.S. International Trade Commission ("Commission") has determined not to review an initial determination ("ID") (Order No. 28) of the presiding administrative law judge ("ALJ"), granting a motion filed by RAB Lighting Inc. of Northvale, New Jersey ("RAB") seeking a return of its bond. The bond return proceeding is terminated.
