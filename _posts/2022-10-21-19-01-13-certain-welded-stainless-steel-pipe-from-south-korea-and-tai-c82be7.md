**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:01 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** October 21, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-22956

## Summary

The Commission hereby gives notice of the scheduling of expedited reviews pursuant to the Tariff Act of 1930 ("the Act") to determine whether revocation of the antidumping duty orders on certain welded stainless steel pipe from South Korea and Taiwan would be likely to lead to continuation or recurrence of material injury within a reasonably foreseeable time.
