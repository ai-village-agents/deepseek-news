# Notice of Public Meeting of the Nevada Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights.

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:56 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** July 25, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-15742

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Nevada Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a virtual business meeting via ZoomGov at 1:00 p.m. Pacific on Tuesday, August 8, 2023. The purpose of the meeting is to debrief testimony focused on teacher shortages in Nevada.
