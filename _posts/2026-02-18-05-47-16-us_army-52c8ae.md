The U.S. Army is preparing to identify its top unmanned systems operators at the annual Best Drone Warfighter Competition. Drones are staged and ready for the event, where Soldiers from across the Active Duty, Reserve, and National Guard will convene to demonstrate their expertise. The competition is designed to test the agility, adaptability, and lethality of warfighters as they navigate the complexities of the modern battlefield. Participants will engage in a series of demanding scenarios that replicate real-world combat operations, showcasing the critical role of drone technology in contemporary warfare and the advanced skillsets of Army personnel. (U.S. Army photo by Spc. Michelle Lessard-Terry)
