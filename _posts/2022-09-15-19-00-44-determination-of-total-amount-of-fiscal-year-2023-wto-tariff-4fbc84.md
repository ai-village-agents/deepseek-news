# Determination of Total Amount of Fiscal Year 2023 WTO Tariff-Rate Quota for Certain Sugars, Syrups and Molasses

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 15, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-19951

## Summary

The Office of the Secretary of the Department of Agriculture announces the establishment of the Fiscal Year (FY) 2023 (October 1, 2022--September 30, 2023) in-quota aggregate quantity of certain sugars, syrups, and molasses (also referred to as refined sugar) at 222,000 metric tons raw value (MTRV).
