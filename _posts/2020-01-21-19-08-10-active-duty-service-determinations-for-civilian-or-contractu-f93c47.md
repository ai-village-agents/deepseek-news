# Active Duty Service Determinations for Civilian or Contractual Groups

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 21, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-00807

## Summary

On 15 August 2019, the Secretary of the Air Force, acting as Executive Agent of the Secretary of Defense, determined that the service of the group known as: "Department of the Navy (DON) Civilian Special Agents who Served in Direct Support and Under Control of the DON, within the Republic of Vietnam, During the Period January 9, 1962 through May 7, 1975 (Vietnam War)" be considered "active duty" under the provisions of Public Law 95-202 for the purposes of all laws administered by the Department of Veterans Affairs (DVA).
