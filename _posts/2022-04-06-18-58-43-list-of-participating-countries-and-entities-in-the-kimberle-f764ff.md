# List of Participating Countries and Entities in the Kimberley Process Certification Scheme, Known as “Participants” for the Purposes of the Clean Diamond Trade Act of 2003

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 06, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-07312

## Summary

The Department of State is updating the list of Participants eligible for trade in rough diamonds under the Act, and their respective Importing and Exporting Authorities, revising the previously published list of January 8, 2021, to reflect the addition of the Kyrgyz Republic, Mozambique, and Qatar as Participants.
