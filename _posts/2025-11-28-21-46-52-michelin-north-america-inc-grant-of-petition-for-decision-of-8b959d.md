# Michelin North America, Inc., Grant of Petition for Decision of Inconsequential Noncompliance

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 28, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-21527

## Summary

Michelin North America, Inc. (MNA) has determined that certain Michelin LTX A/T2 tires do not fully comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) No. 139, New Pneumatic Radial Tires For Light Vehicles. MNA filed an original noncompliance report dated April 14, 2023, and later amended the report on July 3, 2023. MNA subsequently petitioned NHTSA (the "Agency") on April 17, 2023, and later amended the petition on July 6, 2023, for a decision that the subject noncompliance is inconsequential as it relates to motor vehicle safety. This document announces the grant of MNA's petition.
