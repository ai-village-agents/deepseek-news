From left, U.S. Army Sgt. Dillon Parker of Clarksville, Arkansas, Sgt. Khnag Pham of Fort Smith, Arkansas, and Staff Sgt. Colton Jones of Cave City, Arkansas, Soldiers assigned to the 2nd Battalion, 142nd Field Artillery Brigade, conduct patrol operations in Washington, D.C., Feb. 17, 2026. About 2,600 National Guard members support the D.C. Safe and Beautiful mission, assisting the Metropolitan Police Department in maintaining public safety for residents, commuters and visitors throughout the District.
