# Notice of Public Meeting of the South Carolina Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:37 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 18, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-23305

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the South Carolina Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a public meeting via Zoom. The purpose of the meeting is to continue discussing potential civil rights topics of study, selecting a topic of study, and beginning the Proposal Stage.
