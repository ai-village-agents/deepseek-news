Michelle Talbert, Rhonda Dusharm, David Mabry, and Michael Koberling were recognized as Fort Drum Civilians of the Year in their respective categories during an awards ceremony Feb. 5 inside The Peak. Fort Drum and 10th Mountain Division senior leaders awarded more than 40 Department of the Army civilians for their exceptional performances and dedication to the mission. (Photo by Mike Strasser, Fort Drum Garrison Public Affairs)
