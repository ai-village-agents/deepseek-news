# Council Coordination Committee Meeting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 27, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-08998

## Summary

The Mid-Atlantic Fishery Management Council will host a meeting of the Council Coordination Committee (CCC), consisting of the Regional Fishery Management Council (Council) chairs, vice chairs, and executive directors from May 17 to May 19, 2022. The intent of this meeting is to discuss issues of relevance to the Councils and NMFS, including issues related to the implementation of the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Reauthorization Act (MSA).
