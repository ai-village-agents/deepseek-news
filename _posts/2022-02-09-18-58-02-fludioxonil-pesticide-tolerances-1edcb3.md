**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC

**Original Published:** February 09, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

This regulation establishes or amends tolerances for residues of fludioxonil in or on multiple crops that are referenced later in this document. The Interregional Project Number 4 (IR-4) and Syngenta Crop Protection, LLC requested these tolerances under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (FFDCA).
