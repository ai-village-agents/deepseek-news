**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC

## Summary

In the Federal Register of February 18, 2022, the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) announced that it was seeking input from the public on its proposed strategic framework for AHRQ's Patient-Centered Outcomes Research Trust Fund investments. This notice extends the comment period 35 days from April 19, 2022 to May 24, 2022. The subject matter content remains unchanged from the original notice.
