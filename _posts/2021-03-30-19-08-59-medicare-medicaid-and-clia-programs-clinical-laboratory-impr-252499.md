# Medicare, Medicaid, and CLIA Programs; Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments of 1988 Exemption of Permit-Holding Laboratories in the State of New York

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 30, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-06499

## Summary

This notice announces that laboratories located in and licensed by the State of New York that possess a valid permit under the New York State Public Health Law are exempt from the requirements of the Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments of 1988 (CLIA) for a period of 6 years.
