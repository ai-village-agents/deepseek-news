# Stanislaus National Forest; California; Social and Ecological Resilience Across the Landscape 2.0 EIS

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:57 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 17, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-25427

## Summary

The Forest Service ("Forest Service"), United States Department of Agriculture, is preparing an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS) for the Social and Ecological Resilience Across the Landscape 2.0 (SERAL 2.0) project. The project area is approximately 160,000 acres in size, including approximately 119,000 acres of Forest Service lands. The project area includes the remainder of the Stanislaus Landscape--a Wildfire Crisis Strategy Priority Landscape identified in 2022. The project area also spans multiple High Risk Western Firesheds identified by the Secretary of Agriculture in January 2023.
