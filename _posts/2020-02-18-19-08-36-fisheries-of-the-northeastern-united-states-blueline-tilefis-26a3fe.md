# Fisheries of the Northeastern United States; Blueline Tilefish Fishery; 2020 Specifications

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 18, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-03198

## Summary

We are implementing 2020 specifications for the Mid-Atlantic blueline tilefish fishery, including the annual catch and total allowable landings limits. This action establishes allowable harvest levels and other management measures to prevent overfishing, consistent with the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act and the Tilefish Fishery Management Plan.
