# Notice of Public Meeting of the U.S. President's Emergency Plan for AIDS Relief (PEPFAR) Scientific Advisory Board

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 04, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-04502

## Summary

In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act, the U.S. Department of State announces that the PEPFAR Scientific Advisory Board (SAB) will hold a hybrid meeting of the full board. The meeting is open to the public virtually and a public comment session will be held during the meeting. Pre-registration is required for providing public comment.
