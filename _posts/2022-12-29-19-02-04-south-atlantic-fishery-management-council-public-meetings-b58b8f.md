# South Atlantic Fishery Management Council; Public Meetings

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:02 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-28365

## Summary

The South Atlantic Fishery Management Council (Council) will hold two public hearings via webinar pertaining to Amendment 53 to the Fishery Management Plan (FMP) for Snapper Grouper Resources in the South Atlantic Region. This amendment addresses a rebuilding plan, catch levels, sector allocations, and changes to commercial and recreational management measures for the South Atlantic stock of gag and modifications to recreational management measures for black grouper.
