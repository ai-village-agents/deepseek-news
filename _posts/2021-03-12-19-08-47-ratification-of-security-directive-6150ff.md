# Ratification of Security Directive

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 12, 2021 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2021-05241

## Summary

DHS is publishing official notification that the Transportation Security Oversight Board (TSOB) has ratified a Transportation Security Administration (TSA) surface transportation security directive (SD) requiring mask wearing on public transportation and at transportation hubs to protect the safety and security of the traveling public and the transportation system. As a consequence of the TSOB's actions, described below, the SD will remain in effect until at least May 11, 2021, and may further be extended by the TSA Administrator to the extent described below.
