# Arm & Rage, LLC, WJBE(AM), Powell, TN

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 31, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-06811

## Summary

In this document, the Federal Communications Commission (Commission) commences a proceeding to determine whether the license of Arm & Rage, LLC (A&R) for WJBE(AM), Powell, Tennessee, Facility ID No. 59693 should be revoked pursuant to sections 312(a) and (c) of the Communications Act of 1934, following the felony conviction of its sole member, Joseph Armstrong (Armstrong) for filing a false federal income tax return. The hearing will also consider the effect, if any, of A&R's late reporting of the conviction and late-filing of other information required to be placed in its online public inspection file.
