# Grain Inspection Advisory Committee Meeting

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 17, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-05490

## Summary

Pursuant to the Federal Advisory Committee Act, this constitutes notice of the upcoming meeting of the Agricultural Marketing Service (AMS) Grain Inspection Advisory Committee (Advisory Committee). The Advisory Committee meets no less than once annually to advise AMS on the programs and services delivered under the U.S. Grain Standards Act. Recommendations by the Advisory Committee help AMS better meet the needs of its customers who operate in a dynamic and changing marketplace.
