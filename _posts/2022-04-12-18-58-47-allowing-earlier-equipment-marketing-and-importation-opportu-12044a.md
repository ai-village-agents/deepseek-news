# Allowing Earlier Equipment Marketing and Importation Opportunities

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** April 12, 2022 00:00 UTC

## Summary

The Federal Communications Commission (Commission) announces that the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) has approved, for a period of three years, the new information collection associated with the Commission's Allowing Earlier Equipment Marketing and Importation Opportunities, Report and Order. This document is consistent with the Report and Order, which stated that the Commission would publish a document in the Federal Register announcing OMB approval and the effective date of the rules related to the information collection. The Commission also corrects a cross reference in the regulatory text.
