# Notice of Intent To Renew a Current Information Collection

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 30, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-06725

## Summary

The National Center for Science and Engineering Statistics (NCSES) within the National Science Foundation (NSF) is announcing plans to request renewal of the Higher Education Research and Development Survey. In accordance with the requirements of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, we are providing opportunity for public comment on this action. After obtaining and considering public comment, NSF will prepare the submission requesting that OMB approve clearance of this collection for three years.
