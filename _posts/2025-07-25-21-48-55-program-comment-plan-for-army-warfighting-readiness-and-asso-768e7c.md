## Summary

The Department of the Army (Army) is making its Program Comment Plan for Army Warfighting Readiness and Associated Buildings, Structures, and Landscapes (Army Program Comment Plan) available for public review. The Army Program Comment Plan is located on the Army's website: https://www.denix.osd.mil/army-pcwr/. This notice of availability for public review of the Army Program Comment Plan initiates the Army's public participation requirements for the Army's proposed Program Comment for Army Warfighting Readiness and Associated Buildings, Structures, and Landscapes (Program Comment).
