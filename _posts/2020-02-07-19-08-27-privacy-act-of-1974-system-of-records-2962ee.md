# Privacy Act of 1974; System of Records

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 07, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2020-02482

## Summary

As required by the Privacy Act of 1974, notice is hereby given that the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) is amending the system of records entitled, "Investigative Database--OMI-VA" (162VA10MI) as set forth in the Federal Register 75 FR 26847. VA is amending the system of records by revising the System Number; System Location; System Manager; Categories of Records in the System; Record Source Categories and Routine Uses of Records Maintained in the System and Policies. VA is republishing the system notice in its entirety.
