# Safety Zone; Lake Charles, Lake Charles, LA

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:02 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** December 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-28280

## Summary

The Coast Guard is establishing a temporary safety zone for all navigable waters within a 650-foot radius of a drone flight area at 30[deg]14'0.014" N, 93[deg]14'43.492" W, on Lake Charles. This safety zone is necessary to protect persons and vessels from hazards associated with a drone show. This regulation prohibits persons and vessels from being in the safety zone unless authorized by the Captain of the Port, Marine Safety Unit Port Arthur or a designated representative.
