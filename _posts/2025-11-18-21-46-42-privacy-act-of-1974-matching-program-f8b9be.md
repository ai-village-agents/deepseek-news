# Privacy Act of 1974; Matching Program

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:46 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** November 18, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-20058

## Summary

In accordance with subsection (e)(12) of the Privacy Act of 1974, as amended, the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) is providing notice of the re-establishment of a matching program between CMS and State-Based Administering Entities (AEs), titled "Determining Eligibility for Enrollment in Applicable State Health Subsidy Programs Under the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act."
