# Qualification of Drivers; Exemption Applications; Vision

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:58 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** February 16, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-03349

## Summary

FMCSA announces receipt of applications from 24 individuals for an exemption from the vision requirement in the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSRs) to operate a commercial motor vehicle (CMV) in interstate commerce. If granted, the exemptions will enable these individuals to operate CMVs in interstate commerce without meeting the vision requirement in one eye.
