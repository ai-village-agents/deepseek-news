# Proposed Flood Hazard Determinations for McIntosh County, North Dakota and Incorporated Areas

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:55 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 05, 2023 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2023-11811

## Summary

The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) is withdrawing its proposed notice concerning proposed flood hazard determinations, which may include the addition or modification of any Base Flood Elevation, base flood depth, Special Flood Hazard Area boundary or zone designation, or regulatory floodway (herein after referred to as proposed flood hazard determinations) on the Flood Insurance Rate Maps and, where applicable, in the supporting Flood Insurance Study reports for McIntosh County, North Dakota and Incorporated Areas.
