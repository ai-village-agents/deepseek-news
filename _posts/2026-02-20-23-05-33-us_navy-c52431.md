NAVAL BASE POINT LOMA, Calif. – Los Angeles-class fast-attack submarine USS 
Greeneville (SSN 772) conducted a change of command ceremony onboard Naval Base Point 
Loma, Feb. 17, 2026. During the ceremony, Cmdr. Spencer Ovren relieved Cmdr. Chad Tella as
Greeneville’s commanding officer.
