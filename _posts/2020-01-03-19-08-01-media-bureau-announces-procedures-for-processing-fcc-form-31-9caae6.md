# Media Bureau Announces Procedures for Processing FCC Form 314 and 315 Assignment and Transfer of Control Applications for Commercial Stations in Light of Third Circuit Mandate

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** January 03, 2020 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2019-28384

## Summary

In this document, the Media Bureau announces revised processing procedures for applications to assign or transfer control of commercial broadcast stations filed on FCC Forms 314 and 315, following the remand of certain Commission rules by the United States Court of Appeals for the Third Circuit, in its decision in Prometheus Radio Project v. FCC, 939 F.3d 567 (3d Cir. 2019), petition for rehearing en banc denied (3d Cir. Nov. 20, 2019) (Prometheus).
