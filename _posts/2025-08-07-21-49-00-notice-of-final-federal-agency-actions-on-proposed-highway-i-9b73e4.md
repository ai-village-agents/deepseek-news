# Notice of Final Federal Agency Actions on Proposed Highway in California

**Published:** February 04, 2026 21:49 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** August 07, 2025 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2025-14975

## Summary

The FHWA, on behalf of Caltrans, is issuing this notice to announce actions taken by Caltrans that are final. The actions relate to a proposed highway project, on United States Highway 101 (US 101) at the existing Old Bayshore Highway and Brokaw Road ramps, on Zanker Road and Bering Drive to the north of US 101, and on North Fourth Street and Skyport Drive to the south of US 101 in San Jose, State of California, in the County of Santa Clara. Those actions grant licenses, permits, and approvals for the project.
