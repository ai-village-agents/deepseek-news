# Mandatory Survey of Foreign-Residents' Holdings of U.S. Securities

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 07, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-04817

## Summary

By this Notice, the Department of the Treasury is informing the public that it is conducting a mandatory Survey of Foreign- Residents' Holdings of U.S. Securities as of June 30, 2024. This Notice constitutes legal notification to all United States persons (defined below) who meet the reporting requirements set forth in this Notice that they must respond to, and comply with, this survey. Additional copies of the reporting forms SHL (2024) and instructions may be printed from the internet at: https://home.treasury.gov/data/treasury- international-capital-tic-system-home-page/tic-forms-instructions/ forms-shl.
