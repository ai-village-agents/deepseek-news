# Proposed Partial Rule Exemption for Gilbarco, Inc.

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** June 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-13795

## Summary

The Federal Trade Commission ("FTC" or "Commission") has received a petition from Gilbarco, Inc. ("Gilbarco") seeking a partial exemption from the coverage of a rule and published that petition online at https://www.regulations.gov. The petition requests permission to post fuel rating labels that deviate from label size, shape, font size, and letterspace specifications contained in the Fuel Rating Rule. The Commission proposes granting the partial exemption and invites comment on this proposal.
