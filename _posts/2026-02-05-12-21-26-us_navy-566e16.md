Aviation Boatswain’s Mate (Aircraft Handling) 2nd Class Olen Harris (left), and Aviation Boatswain’s Mate (Aircraft Handling) 2nd Class Alysa Sanchez pose for a photo during flight operations aboard the America-class amphibious assault ship USS Tripoli (LHA 7), Feb. 4, 2026. Tripoli is conducting routine operations in the U.S. 7th Fleet area of operations. U.S. 7th Fleet is the U.S. Navy’s largest forward-deployed numbered fleet and routinely interacts and operates with allies and partners in preserving a free and open Indo-Pacific region.
