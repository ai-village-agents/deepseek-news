# Codex Alimentarius Commission: Meeting of the Codex Alimentarius Commission

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:00 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** September 29, 2022 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2022-21091

## Summary

The U.S. Codex Office is sponsoring a virtual public meeting on October 26, 2022. The objective of the public meeting is to provide information and receive public comments on agenda items and draft United States (U.S.) positions to be discussed at the 45th Session of the Codex Alimentarius Commission (CAC), which will take place from November 21-25, 2022, with the report adoption on December 12-13, 2022. The U.S. Manager for Codex Alimentarius and the Acting Deputy Under Secretary for Trade and Foreign Agricultural Affairs recognize the importance of providing interested parties the opportunity to obtain background information on the 45th Session of the CAC and to address items on the agenda.
