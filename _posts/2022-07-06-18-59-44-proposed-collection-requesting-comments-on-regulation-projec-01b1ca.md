# Proposed Collection; Requesting Comments on Regulation Project

**Published:** February 05, 2026 18:59 UTC

## Summary

The Internal Revenue Service, as part of its continuing effort to reduce paperwork and respondent burden, invites the general public and other federal agencies to take this opportunity to comment on proposed and/or continuing information collections, as required by the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995. The IRS is soliciting comments concerning final regulations in Treasury Decision (TD) 8458 relating to real estate mortgage investment conduits.
