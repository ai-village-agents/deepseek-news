# Notice of Public Meetings of the Pennsylvania Advisory Committee to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights

**Published:** February 05, 2026 19:08 UTC
**Source:** Federal Register
**Original Published:** March 05, 2024 00:00 UTC
**Document Number:** 2024-04566

## Summary

Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the provisions of the rules and regulations of the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights (Commission) and the Federal Advisory Committee Act, that the Pennsylvania Advisory Committee (Committee) to the U.S. Commission on Civil Rights will hold a series of public briefings via Zoom. The purpose of these briefings is to gather testimony regarding the civil rights inplications of the rising use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in education.
